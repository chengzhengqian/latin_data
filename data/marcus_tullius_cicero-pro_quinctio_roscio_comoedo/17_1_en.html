For at that time, owing to the disasters by which the republic was afflicted, no one’s property was secure; now, thanks to the kindness of the immortal gods, everyone’s possessions are assured; then the farm was uncultivated, without a building upon it; now it is highly cultivated and there is an excellent homestead. However, since you are naturally34 so ill-disposed, I will never relieve you of that worry and anxiety.a Roscius made a very good thing of the business and got a most productive farm; what has that to do with you? Make any settlement you like as to your half share. Now he changes his tactics and tries to invent what he is unable to prove. “It was about the whole business,” says he, “that you made a settlement.”

The whole cause then comes to this: did Roscius make an agreement with Flavius only for his own share or for the partnership as a whole? For if35 Roscius has received anything on account of the partnership, I admit that he ought to hand it over. “When he accepted the farm from Flavius, he made a settlement on behalf of the partnership, not for himself alone.” Why then did he not give security that no one should ask a farthingb more? Anyone who makes an agreement for himself alone leaves to the others their right of action unimpaired; one who makes an agreement for his partners gives security that none of them shall make any further claim.
