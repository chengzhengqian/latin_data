“Earth felt the gods in action, the stars were shaken, and swung seeking their former poise; for the whole palace of the sky broke and tumbled to ruin. And first Dione1 champions the arms of Caesar, and Pallas2 joins her side, and the child of Mars,3 who brandishes his tall spear. Phoebus, and his sister4 with him, and the son of Cyllene5 and the hero of Tiryns,6 like to him in all his deeds, receive Pompey the Great.

“The trumpets quavered, and Discord with dishevelled hair raised her Stygian head to the upper sky. Blood had dried on her face, tears ran from her bruised eyes, her teeth were mailed with a scurf of rust, her tongue was dripping with foulness and her face beset with snakes, her clothes were torn before her writhen breasts, and she waved a blood-red torch in her quivering hand.
