But Ascyltos let himself go completely, threw up his hands and made fun of everything and laughed till he cried. This annoyed one5 of Trimalchio’s fellow-freedmen, the man who was sitting next above me.

“What are you laughing at, mutton head? “he said.” Are our host’s good things not good enough for you? I suppose you are richer and used to better living.
