And when nails are scabrous, they must be loosened all round, where they are in contact with the flesh; next some of the following composition is put on them: sandarach and sulphur 8 grms. each; soda and orpiment 16 grms. each; liquid resin 32 grms. The finger is dressed again on the third day. Under this medicament, diseased nails fall off and in their stead better ones grow.
