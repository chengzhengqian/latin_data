Trinummus

Three-Dollar Day

Plot Summary

When going abroad, Charmides entrusts a hidden treasure and all his belongings to his friend Callicles. In the absence of Charmides, his son wastes his money badly: he even sells the house. Callicles buys it. His virgin sister is asked for in marriage5 without a dowry. In order to give her a dowry without causing offense, Callicles commissions someone to say that he is bringing gold from her father. When he comes to the house, old Charmides makes fun of him on his return; his children marry.
