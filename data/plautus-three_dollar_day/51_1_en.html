char (interrupting) After that I don’t want you to say anything.

syc But—

char (interrupting) I’m leaving, if you annoy me: Presto, no decent man ought to tell how he got from earth to heaven.39

syc I’ll do as I see you want me to. But show me the people I’m looking for, to whom I should bring these letters.

char What do you say? If by chance you were to spot Charmides950 in person now, the one who you say has given you those letters, would you recognize him? syc You really think I’m such a fool that I couldn’t recognize the man who I’ve spent my life with. Would he be so stupid as to entrust one thousand Philippics to me,955 money which he told me to bring to his son and to his friend Callicles, in whose charge he said he’d put his affairs here? Would he have entrusted it to me if he didn’t know me properly and I him?
