char Callippus?

syc No.

char Callidemides?

syc No.

char Callinicus?

syc No.

char Callimarchus?

syc It’s no use. And what’s more, I don’t care a straw, since I remember it for my own purposes.

char But as a matter of fact there are many Lesbonicuses here. Unless you tell me his father’s name, I can’t show920 you those people you’re looking for. What’s it like? On the chance that we can find it by guessing.

syc Like this—

char (interrupting) Chares? Or Charmides?

syc Yes, Charmides! That’ll be the fellow. May the gods ruin him!

char I already told you a while ago: it’s more appropriate for you to speak well of a friend than badly.

syc Did the worthless chap really hide between my lips and925 teeth?

char Stop insulting a friend in his absence.

syc Then why did that useless fellow hide from me?

char If you’d called him, he’d have replied to his name. But where is he himself?

syc I left him with Rhadamas in Monkeyland.
