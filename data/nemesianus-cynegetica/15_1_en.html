Contemplaverit in 1. 3 may be an archaistic return to the active form of the verb as used in early Latin; but the metrical quantity of notae which Longolius read in 1. 13 and of gulae in the last line of all is unclassical, and the frequent elision of a long vowel (11. 5, 6, 14 and 27) is noticeable. Teuffel considers the lines a late production, though they are usually printed along with the Cynegetica.
