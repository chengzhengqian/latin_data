CX. Which is the Second on the Civil War. b.c. 49

Gaius Caesar besieged Marseilles, which had closed its gates to him; he left his staff officers Gaius Trebonius and Decimus Brutus in charge of the siege, set out for Spain and received at Ilerda the surrender of Lucius Afranius and Marcus Petreius, deputies of Gnaeus Pompeius, together with their seven legions. He let them all go unharmed; Varro, also a deputy of Pompey, along with his army, was brought under Caesar’s control. He granted citizenship to the people of Gades.
