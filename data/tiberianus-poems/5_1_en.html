Tiberianus

a world—this home of men and gods—lucent, starred with the majestic bloom of youth. Touching this world (vouchsafe thy favour, I pray), grant to a willing mind the knowledge of the principles on which it was created, the manner of its origin and making. Grant, O Sire, that I may have power to learn causes majestic, by what alliance of things a thou didst of old upraise the world’s masses of matter, and of what light texture, intimate yet dissimilar, thou didst of old in thy might weave the soul, and what that vigorous element is which in quick-moving bodies constitutes life.
