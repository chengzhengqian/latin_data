Publilius Syrus

720Dumb pleasure is rather fear than joy. Goodness means inability to do a wrong. It makes a wide difference whether you were born wise or only look it. From virtue no man honourably withholds his love. Where freedom has fallen, none dare freely speak. 725The life of ease is a kingdom without the worry. Where all go wrong, the hope of remonstrance is removed. A few are justly destroyed that more may be reformed. All virtue finds an obstacle in fear. When the accuser is judge, force, not law, has power. 730In buying others’ goods, see you don’t have to sell your own. When an offence is soon corrected, scandal commonly overlooks it. When the innocent is found guilty, part of his native land is exiled. It is honourable to conquer, bitter to crush, handsome to forgive. Repentance follows on a hasty plan.
