Such were the terms she imposed: ‘I shall abide by the terms,’ said I. She laughed, exulting in the dominion I had given her over me. Next she fumigated every spot touched by the girls brought in, and mopped the threshold with clean water; she bade me change anew all the oil in the lamps, and thrice with burning sulphur touched my head. Then after every sheet on the bed had been changed, I made my obeisance, and on the couch we knew so well made peace.
