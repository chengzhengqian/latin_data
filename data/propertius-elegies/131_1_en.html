3.23 The lost tablets

So my accomplished tablets are lost then, 68 and so much splendid writing lost with them! Long usage at my hands had worn them down and bade them, though unsealed, be credited as mine. They had by now learned how to mollify girls in my absence, and in my absence utter some persuasive phrases. No setting of gold had made them precious: they were just cheap wax on common boxwood.
