You are mistaken, these journeys of yours indicate some furtive love affair: it is not Rome but my eyes that you flee, silly girl! You are wasting your time, the wiles you spin against me are futile, you are a novice laying familiar traps for me, who am an adept. But for me it matters less: the loss of your good name will cause you all the harm which you deserve. For lately rumour spoke ill of you to my ears, and all over the town there was nasty gossip.
