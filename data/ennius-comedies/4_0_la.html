FRL II: ENNIUS

DUBIUM (F 6)

The late-antique writer Fulgentius (6th c. AD) cites this fragment as from an otherwise unknown Ennian comedy (evidence in Baldwin 1988, 44). Since many of his quotations are problematic, Fulgentius is considered an unreliable source: whether this testimony is trustworthy and sufficient to posit a play called Telestis thus remains in doubt.
