But look what the dead man said to him in his sleep: “I am a guest from overseas, Diapontius.13 I live here, this dwelling place has been allotted to me: Orcus14 did not want to receive me into the Underworld because I lost500 my life before my time. I was deceived in violation of the obligations of hospitality: my host murdered me here and he secretly put me underground in this house without due rites, for the sake of gold, the criminal. Now move out from here. This house is under a curse, this dwelling place is defiled.” I could barely tell you in a year what 505 apparitions take place here.
