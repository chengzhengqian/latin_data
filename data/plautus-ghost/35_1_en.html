A house?

theo

tra

Yes, a house. But do you know what it’s like?

theo

How can I know?

tra

Goodness!

theo

What is it?

tra

Stop asking me.

theo

Why?

tra

A mirror-bright one, pure candor.

theo

Excellent. Well then? What’s the agreed price?

tra

As many Attic talents as you and I added together. But645 these forty minas he’s given as deposit; he’s taken from this chap what we gave the vendor. Do you understand? Well, after our house was the way I told you, he immediately bought himself another.

theo

Excellent.650

mis

Please pay him off so that he won’t kill us with his verbal vomiting. Forty-four minas are owed to him.
