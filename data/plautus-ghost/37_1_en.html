SIMO from his house, followed by a slave.
simo

I haven’t been so well entertained at home for this whole690 year nor has any single meal given me more pleasure. My wife gave me a very good lunch. Now she tells me to go to bed: no way. I immediately realized it wasn’t due to chance when she gave me a better lunch than usual: the695 old lady wanted to get me into bed. Sleep after lunch is no good. Away with you! I sneaked out of the house on the quiet. My wife’s in a perfect fury at home, I know that.
