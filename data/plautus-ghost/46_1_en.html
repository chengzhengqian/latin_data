They practice running and flee, but when they’re caught and brought back they get private funds27 in the shape of beatings which they couldn’t get in the shape of tips. They increase865 their funds from very little and create a treasure from it. I for one have the plan in my heart to be on my guard against a beating rather than that my back should feel pain.
