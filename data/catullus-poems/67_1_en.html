But rather, O ye brides, may concord evermore dwell in your homes, ever abiding Love. And you, my queen, when gazing up to the stars you propitiate Venus on festive days, let not me your handmaid want perfumes, but rather enrich me with bounteous gifts. Let the stars go hang! I would fain be the queen’s lock once more; then let Orion blaze next to Aquarius.

LXVII

Catullus

Hail, house-door, dear to a well-beloved husband and dear to a father, hail, and may Jupiter bless you with kindly help: they say, door, that you once did kindly service to Balbus, when the old man himself held the house, and since then, as they tell us, you did grudging service to his son, after the old man died and was buried and you became the door of a wedded house.
