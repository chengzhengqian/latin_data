For now shall Junia wed with Manlius, Junia as fair as Venus who dwells in Idalium, when she came to the Phrygian judge; a good maiden with a good omen,

like the Asian myrtle shining with flowering sprays, which the Hamadryad goddesses with dewy moisture nourish as a plaything for themselves.
