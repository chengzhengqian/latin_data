“You can see it’s useless to ask for hellebore when the sickly skin is already getting bloated. Face the disease at its approach, and what need will you have to promise the earth to Craterus? 17 Learn, you idiotic creatures, discover the rationale of existence: What are we and what sort of life are we born for? What rank is given us at the start? Where and when should we make a smooth turn around the post? 18 What should be the limit to money? What is it right to pray for? What are the uses of new-minted coin? How much should be lavished on your country and your nearest and dearest? What role is assigned you by god and where in the human world have you been stationed? Learn this, and don’t be unwilling to learn just because all those pots are going rotten in your richly stocked larder (since your defence of your fat Umbrians), along with the pepper and hams (mementoes of your Marsian client), and because the sprats from the first jar haven’t yet run out.
