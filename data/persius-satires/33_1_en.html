Then there are black ghouls and the dangers of the broken egg, 40 then there are huge Galli 41 and the one-eyed priestess with her rattle: 42 they hammer into you gods which puff up the body, if you have not taken the prescribed head of garlic three times in the morning.

Talk like this among the centurions with bulging veins and right away huge Pulfenius will give a coarse laugh and bid a clipped one hundred-penny coin for one hundred Greeks.
