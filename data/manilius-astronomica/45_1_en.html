Who, unless there came from heaven a power which calls us heavenward to the sacred fellowship of nature? Who could deny the sacrilege of grasping an unwilling heaven, enslaving it, as it were, in its own domain, and fetching it to earth? But, lest it take a long digression to vouch for what is plain to see, the faith it keeps c will create for our science authority and faith in it; for neither does our system deceive nor ever is deceived. Rightly and for true reasons trusted is the path that one must take, and the outcome follows even as before foretold. What Fortune has confirmed, what man would dare call false, gainsaying the casting of so great a vote?
