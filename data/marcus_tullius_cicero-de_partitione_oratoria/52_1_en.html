If a man has carelessly or under compulsion or by accident committed an action that in the case of persons acting deliberately and voluntarily would not be permissible, for the purpose of pleading forgiveness for the action pardon must be sued for by a form of petition that will be taken from a number of topics of equity.

That concludes my exposition, made in the briefest form within my power, of the whole subject of controversies—unless you yourself have some further question to ask.
