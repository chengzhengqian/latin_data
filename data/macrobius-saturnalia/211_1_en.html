So he spoke and set about paying the due honor of sacrifice on the altars, A bull to Neptune, a bull to you, fair Apollo.

7. In the second book of his Human Antiquities (fr. 8), Varro says that Dardanus brought the Penates from Samothrace to Phrygia, and that Aeneas brought them from Phrygia to Italy;27 yet he does not there make plain who the Penates are.
