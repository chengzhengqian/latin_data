He went until he reached the great grotto where dwelt the nymph, fair-haired, a terrible divinity who speaks with a human voice. A fire burned high on the hearth, from afar the scent of well-split cedar and citron could be savored all through island as they burnt. Within she sang with her lovely voice going up and down before the loom, weaving with a golden shuttle.
