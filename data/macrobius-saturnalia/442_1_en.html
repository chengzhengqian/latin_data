8. The heavens, meanwhile, revolve and night rushes on from Ocean. (A. 2.250)6

Ennius in Book 67 (205 Sk.):

The heavens, meanwhile, revolve with their vast constellations.

9. Upon his shoulder he makes the vault of heaven turn, studded with blazing stars. (A. 4.482)

Ennius in Book 1 (27 Sk.):

Who makes heaven wheel, studded with shining stars,

and Book 3 (145 Sk.):

He looked out at heaven, studded with shining stars,

and Book 10 (348 Sk.):

From this point night went on, studded with blazing stars.
