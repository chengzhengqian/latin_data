4. Is the following argument from the lesser not terribly moving? (A. 3.712–13*) Neither Helenus the seer—though he warned of many horrors— nor dreadful Celaeno predicted that I would feel such grief. Here we can only conclude that he judged all his past suffering less grievous than his father’s death.
