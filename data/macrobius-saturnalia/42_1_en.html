Most of the Greeks, and especially Athens, conduct rites in honor of Saturn, which they call the Cronia, and they celebrate the day: throughout the countryside and almost all the towns they carry on feasting and care for their slaves, each one his own: just so did our countrymen receive the custom of slaves dining together with their masters.115
