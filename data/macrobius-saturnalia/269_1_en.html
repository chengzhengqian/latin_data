Similarly, one of grape varieties:169 Aminean—named, of course, from the region where the Aminei lived, now the Falernian territory—asinusca, atrusca, albuelis, abena,170 apiana, Apician, udder-shaped (or as the Greeks say, boumasthos), hard-berry, wild, black-raisin, Maronian, Mareotic, Nomentan, precia, pramnia, psithia, pilleolata, Rhodian, wreath-vine, venucula, variola, hare-grape.’

8. As Servius spoke Praetextatus said, ‘I’d like to listen longer to our friend Servius, but the hour puts us in mind of sleep, so that “with the first light risen” (cf. A. 4.130) we may enjoy Symmachus’ holding forth at his own house.’ And with that they departed.
