Some say that this goddess has Juno’s power and for that she was given a scepter to hold in her left hand.256 Others believe the same goddess is Proserpina and receives a pig in sacrifice because a pig ate the crop that Ceres gave to mortals.257 Still others believe her to be Chthonian Hecate,258 the Boeotians, Semele.259
