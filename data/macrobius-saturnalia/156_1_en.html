13. But we should have a finer understanding: let’s not allow this sacred poem’s inner sancta to be concealed, but instead let’s trace the path to its secret meanings and fling open its inmost shrine so that it’s available to the worship of the learned.

14. And lest I seem to claim all topics for myself, I undertake to make plain the rhetorical skill of Virgil’s oeuvre—its most forceful conceits and expressions—though I do not want to deprive Eusebius, the most eloquent of speakers, of the chance to treat Maro’s oratorical skill, since he will be better suited by both his scholarship and his teaching.598 Let me also earnestly ask the rest of you who are gathered here to contribute remarks on whatever aspect of Maro’s genius has chiefly claimed your attention, like an offering to a communal banquet.’

15. All present fell in with this suggestion with remarkable speed, as each one rose to say how much he’d like to hear the others, not realizing that he too would be called on to contribute. So by urging each other eagerly on, they reached an easy and happy consensus; then turning to Praetextatus, they all asked him to reveal his preference first, with the rest to follow in order according to where they happened to be sitting.
