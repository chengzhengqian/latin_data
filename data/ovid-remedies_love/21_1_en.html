She was going, as the barbarian throng is wont to go, when it keeps the triennial feast of Edonian Bacchus, with hair dishevelled, and now, where she could, she gazed on the far-flung sea, now weary lay on the sandy ground. “Faithless Demophoon!” she cried to the deaf waves, and her sobbing broke the words she uttered. There was a narrow way o’er-cast by the long shadows, by which she oft betook herself to the sea. For the ninth time she trod her hapless path: “Let him see to it!” she cries, and turning pale looks at her girdle; she looks at the branches also; she hesitates, and shrinks from what she ventures, and is afraid, and sets her fingers at her neck. Then truly, Sithonian maid, could I wish thou hadst not been alone: the wood had not shed its leaves and wept for Phyllis. By example of Phyllis fear too much secrecy, O lover pained by thy mistress, O girl pained by thy lover!

A youth had performed whate’er my Muse commanded, and was nearly within the haven of his safety; he fell back, when he came among eager lovers, and Love resumed the arms he had put away. If you love, nor wish to love, see that you shun contagion; even beasts are hurt thereby. The eyes, in beholding the afflicted, themselves suffer affliction, and many things harm our bodies through chance encounter.
