On The Repudiation of A Pleader

If any person, who shall have been by this law granted as pleader, shall be a suspected character, the person to whom he shall have been granted by this law may reject him. . . . Then the praetor who shall by this law conduct the inquiry, shall, from the list of those pleaders whom he may by this law grant, grant another pleader to him who shall ask for such to be given him . . .
