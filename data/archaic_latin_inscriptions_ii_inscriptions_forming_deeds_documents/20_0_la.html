One of five thin plates or tablets of lead, pierced and crowded round a nail. Each of them curses in detail one person—Plotius, Avonia, Maxima Vesonia, Aquilia and an unknown man (the tablet on which it is written is called Secunda 5 for convenience) respectively. Since the contents of the curse are similar in each case, the tablets can be supplemented by each other. All the tablets were inscribed in the month of February by the same person, presumably a sorcerer employed by those
