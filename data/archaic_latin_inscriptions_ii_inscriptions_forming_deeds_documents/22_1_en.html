Should there so exist any written curse, 3 great or small—in what manner Plotius has, according to the laws of magic, composed any curse and entrusted it to writing, 4 in such manner I consign and hand him 5 over to thee, so that thou mayest consign and hand over that fellow, in the month of February. Blast him! damn him! blast him utterly! Hand him over, consign him, that he may not be able to behold, see, and contemplate any month further!
