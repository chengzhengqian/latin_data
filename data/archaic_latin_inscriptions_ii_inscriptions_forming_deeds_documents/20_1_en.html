Curses. 1

(i) A round tablet of lead found apparently at Cumae. Mixed Latin and Oscan. Interpretation of the names, etc., is not certain.

Lucius Harines, son of Herius Maturus, Gaius Eburius, Pomponius, Marcus Caedicius son of Marcus, Numerius Andripius, son of Numerius. May the— 2 of the whole lot of them stand upright! May their breath 3 be dry!

(ii) A small bronze plate found in a sepulchre at Cumae.

Summoned to 4 Hell’s court: Naevia Secunda, freedwoman of Lucius, or whatever other name she goes by.
