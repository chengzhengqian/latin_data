On the order of my argument, which requires me to discuss the perpetual punishment of those condemned with the devil before the eternal happiness of the saints.

In this book, with the help of God, we must discuss more carefully the kind of punishment that the devil will suffer with all who belong to him, after the two cities—the one of God, the other of the devil—by command of Christ our Lord, the Judge of the living and the dead, have come to their destined ends. My reason for this arrangement, that is, leaving till later what I have to say about the happiness of the saints, is this.
