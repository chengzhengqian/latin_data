XX

Are we to believe that good gods are more willing to associate with demons than with men?

But we shall be told that there is a pressing and compelling reason why the demons must act as intermediaries between gods and men, to carry prayers from men and to fetch back favourable answers from the gods. Then what, pray, is this reason, this important necessity? Because no God, they say, deals directly with man.

A fine thing, then, is the sanctity of God! He has no communication with a man who entreats him, but he has with a demon who acts insolently. He has no communication with a man full of repentance, but he has with a demon full of cheats. He has no communication with a man who takes refuge in his divinity, but he has with a demon who counterfeits divinity. He has no communication with a man who craves mercy, but he has with a demon who prompts men to vice.
