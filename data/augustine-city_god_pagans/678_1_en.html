We should not try to comprehend the infinite expanses of time preceding the existence of the universe any more than we should the infinite expanses of space outside the universe, because, just as there are no periods of time before it, so there are no positions in space outside it.

Next we must see what reply we should make to those who agree that God is the creator of the universe and yet ask questions about its location in time, and what answer they themselves will make about its location in space. For just as men inquire why it was created at that particular moment and not earlier, so the question may arise why it was created here where it is and not elsewhere.
