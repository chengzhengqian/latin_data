What more shall I say? No man of even moderate intelligence doubts that these spirits need not be worshipped for the sake of the life of blessedness that is to come after death. But perhaps we shall be told that all the gods are good, while of the demons some are bad and some good; and we shall be advised to worship those who are thought good that we may by their agency attain to a life of eternal bliss. The value of this theory must now be examined in the following book.
