Thus this city has one division which is gathered from among mortal men and is destined to be united with the immortal angels. In our day it endures the vicissitudes of a pilgrimage on earth or else, as far as those who have met death are concerned, is at rest in the secret repositories and abiding places of souls.4 I see that I must now explain, just as I did with reference to the angels, how this division also came into being through creation by the same God. The fact is that the human race took its rise from one man whom God created first, as we are assured in holy Scripture, which has, and rightly so, marvellous authority throughout the world and among all nations.5 That the nations would put faith in it is a true prediction divinely made by Scripture among its other true utterances.6
