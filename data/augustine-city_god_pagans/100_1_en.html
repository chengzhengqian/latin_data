It would be tedious to recite the many shameful and iniquitous acts with which the peace of that city was shaken in the period when the powerful were attempting to make the populace their subjects and the populace rebelled against subjection, and the champions of both parties were more motivated in their zeal by a contentious spirit than they were animated by any notion of fair play and virtue.
