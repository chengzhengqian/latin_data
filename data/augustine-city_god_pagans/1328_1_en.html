He had converse on earth for forty days with his disciples, and in their sight ascended into heaven, and after ten days sent the Holy Spirit, as he had promised, of whose coming to those who had believed the greatest and most compelling sign was that every one of them spoke with the tongues of all peoples, thus signifying that the united universal church would be found among all peoples and would thus speak in all tongues.
