But those who are wise according to God hold that the strongest argument in favour of all things that seem incredible to men, yet are found in the holy Scriptures, whose truth has already been upheld in many ways, is the reliable omnipotence of God; they are sure that he could in no way have lied in the Scriptures, and that he can do what to the unbeliever is impossible.
