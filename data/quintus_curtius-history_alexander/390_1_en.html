Meanwhile news was brought to the Macedonians that the king had been killed. What would have terrified other men animated them. For regardless of all danger, they broke through the wall with mattocks, and when they had made a breach, rushed into the city and cut down the Indi, more of whom fled 20 than dared to engage them. They spared neither old men, women, nor children; whoever met them they believed to be the person by whom the king had been wounded.
