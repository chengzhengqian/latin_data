I have given you 9 proofs of myself, and I have had proofs of you. The rock which you see has only one approach, which the barbarians beset, they neglect the rest; they have no sentinels except those that are watching our 10 camp. You will find a way, if you seek carefully for paths leading to the top. Nature has placed nothing so high, that valour cannot overcome it. It is by trying what others have despaired of that we have 11 Asia in our power. Go up to the summit; when you have attained it, you will give me a signal with white cloths. I will bring up forces and divert the enemy 12 from you to us. The reward for him who first reaches the top will be ten talentsb; he who comes next will receive one less, and the same proportion will be maintained up to the number of ten men.
