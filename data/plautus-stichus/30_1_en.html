I don’t know any less reliable animal. Did I seek omens500 in a matter of life and death for me through an animal that changes its place ten times a day? I’m resolved to assemble my friends in order to consult them on which terms now I . . . ought to starve.

Exit gelasimus to the right. ACT FOUR Enter antipho and pamphilippus from the left.

ant As truly as the gods may love me well and keep my505 daughters well for me, Pamphilippus, it is a pleasure for me to see that you’ve both, you and your brother, returned home to our country conducting your business so successfully.

pamphili I’d take security from you, if I didn’t see that you’re friendly toward me, Antipho; now that I realize that you’re friendly toward me, I’ll take your word for it.

ant I’d invite you to my place for dinner, if your brother510 hadn’t told me that you’re going to dine at his place today, when he was inviting me to his place to dinner. And it would have been more appropriate for me to give you two dinner on your arrival than to accept his invitation, but I wouldn’t have wished to oppose him. Now I have no desire to get your goodwill through mere words: tomorrow515 both you and he will be at my place together with your wives.
