pan Go, fetch the hanger-on Gelasimus, and bring him along with you; I want to send him to the harbor, if by chance any ship has come here from Asia8 yesterday or today; one slave is sitting at the harbor every day, but still I want someone to go and check. Hurry and return at once.

Exit panegyris into her house. Enter gelasimus from the right, without noticing Crocotium.
