sti I’ve had an absolutely delightful idea: like Cynic philosophers39 we’re being entertained on a bench rather than on couches.

sang Indeed, it’s much more pleasant here. But, our commander,705 why is the bowl resting in the meantime? See how many cups we’re drinking.

sti As many as you have fingers on a hand. There’s a Greek song: drink five or three, but not four.40
