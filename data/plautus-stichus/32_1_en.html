to, the same as I’m now saying to you—

epi (interrupting) I’m listening and paying attention carefully.

ant “I’ve given you my daughter so that you could sleep pleasantly with her; now I think it’s fair that I should be given someone by you in return so that I can sleep with her.”

epi Who says that? That one the same as you?

ant Now I’m speaking to you as the one the same as me. “No,550 I’ll give you two,” says that young man, “if one is too little; and if you’re not content with two,” he says, “two more will be added.”
