The day after he caught sight of the enemy he crushed their fleet, which was laden with supplies and encumbered by its own equipment and weaponry. It is idiocy to believe a war can be won by sitting around or making vows. You must seize your weapons, go down to the plain, and engage the enemy man to man. As Minucius made these comments like some public orator, tribunes and Roman knights would crowd around him, and his swaggering comments also made their way to the ears of the soldiers. And had it depended on a vote by the men, they made it clear that they would choose Minucius over Fabius, their leader.

15. Fabius’ eyes were focused no less on his own men than on the enemy, and he first of all made it clear that he was not cowed by them. Although he knew full well that his delaying tactics were discredited not just in the camp but now in Rome as well, he drew out the rest of the summer sticking resolutely to the same plan of campaign. As a result Hannibal, deprived of any hope of the engagement that he had done everything to bring on, began to look around for a site for winter quarters, because the region where he was could temporarily provision him, but not indefinitely—it was land of plantations and vines, all cultivated with crops that were succulent rather than essential.
