21. It looked as if the remainder of the summer was going to be uneventful after that, and it would have been as far as the Carthaginian enemy was concerned. But beside the fact that the Spaniards are a naturally fractious people always eager for violent change, now, after the Romans retired from the pass to the coastal areas, Mandonius and Indibilis (the former chieftain of the Ilergetes)90 roused their compatriots to insurrection and came to pillage the already pacified lands of Roman allies. A military tribune was sent by Scipio with some light armed auxiliaries to confront the insurgents and they easily routed in battle what was just a makeshift force, killing a thousand men, taking some prisoner and disarming most of the others. However, this uprising made Hasdrubal, now in retreat toward the Atlantic, turn back north of the Ebro to defend his allies. The Punic camp was located in the territory of the Illergavonenses,91 and the Roman camp at Nova Classis,92 when news suddenly arrived to shift the theater of operations elsewhere.
