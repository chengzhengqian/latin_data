This young man knows who the girl he raped is, but she doesn’t know him, and her father30 doesn’t even know that she’s been raped. I’ll make this old man from next door ask for her hand today. My reason for doing this is so that the man who’s raped her may marry her all the more easily. And this old man who’s going to ask for her hand, he’s the uncle of that young fellow who violated her chastity by35 night during the vigil held in honor of Ceres. 2 (sound of shouting from Euclio’s house) But now this old man’s shouting inside as usual. He’s throwing out the old woman so that she can’t learn his secret. I think he wants to look at his gold and check that it hasn’t been stolen.
