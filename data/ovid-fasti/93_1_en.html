Fasti, III

III. Kal. 30th

879 When four times from that day the shepherd shall have folded the cloyed kids, and four times the grass shall have whitened under the fresh dew, it will be time to adore Janus, and gentle Concord with him, and Roman Safety, and the altar of Peace.

Pr. Kal. 31st

883 The moon rules the months: the period of this month also ends with the worship of the Moon on the Aventine Hill.
