Plautus

Scaena

Athenis

The stage represents a street in Athens. There are two houses on it, with an altar of Apollo in between: the house on the left belongs to Bacchis; the one on the right belongs to Mnesilochus and his father. The street leads to the harbor on the left and to the city center on the right. The house of Pistoclerus and his father is not on stage but presumably lies on the way to the forum.
