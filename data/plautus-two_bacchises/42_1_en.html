The Two Bacchises

pis

Faster at wasting money than at writing.

mne

Speak. That’s written.

chry

“Now, my father, you should be careful of him. He’s coming740 up with tricks in order to take the money away from you. And he said that he really would take it.”Write that down explicitly. (Mnesilochus complies)

mne

Just tell me.

chry

“And he promises he’ll give that gold to me so I can give it to prostitutes and eat it up and waste it in Greek style in brothels, father. But, father, mind he doesn’t trick you today. Please be careful.” (Mnesilochus keeps writing)

mne

Speak further.745

chry

Write down—

mne

(interrupting) Just tell me what I should write.

chry

“But, father, I ask you to remember what you promised me: don’t beat him. But do guard him at your place at home in fetters.” (Mnesilochus finishes, Chrysalus turns to Pistoclerus) Give me the wax and thread immediately, you there. (passes the items on to Mnesilochus) Go on, fasten it and seal it quickly.

mne

Please, what’s the point of having it written in this way, telling him not to believe you in anything and to put you in irons and keep watch over you at home?

chry

Because I like it this way. Can’t you mind your own business751 instead of sparing me? I was relying on myself when I took on the job and I’m conducting my business at my own risk.

mne

Fair enough.

chry

Give me the tablets.

mne

Take them. (hands them over)

chry
