The Two Bacchises

Characters

  • Bacchis a prostitute; from Athens
  • Slave-Girl Of Bacchis only appears in the first scene
  • Pistoclerus a young man; reliable friend of Mnesilochus, falls in love with the Athenian Bacchis
  • Boy serves Cleomachus
  • Sister OF BACCHIS also a prostitute; from Samos
  • Lydus a slave-tutor; tried to bring up Pistoclerus strictly
  • Chrysalus a slave; serves Mnesilochus
  • Nicobulus an old man; father of Mnesilochus
  • Mnesilochus a young man; just returned from Ephesus, loves the Bacchis from Samos
  • Philoxenus an old man; father of Pistoclerus
  • Hanger-On follows Cleomachus
  • Artamo a slave overseer; serves Nicobulus
  • Cleomachus a soldier; also loves the Bacchis from Samos
