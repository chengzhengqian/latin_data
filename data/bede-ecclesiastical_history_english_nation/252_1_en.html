all parts brought both people and the foresaid king to the way of righteousness again: so that abandoning and throwing down their temples and the altars they had made, they opened the churches and confessing gladly the name of Christ, which they had gainsayed, they chose rather to die in Him with faith in resurrection than to live amongst idols in the filth of unbelief.
