Chapter XVIII

How the South Saxons received Eadbert and Eolla for their bishops, the West Saxons Daniel and Aldhelm for theirs; and of the writings of the same Aldhelm.

The 705th year of the Lord’s incarnation Aldfrid king of Northumberland died, the 20th year of his reign not yet fully expired; after whom succeeded his son Osred, a child of about 8 years old, and reigned 11 years.3 In the beginning of his reign Heddi, bishop of the West Saxons, departed to the heavenly life.
