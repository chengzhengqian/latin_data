Chapter X

How Wilbrord1 preaching in Frisland converted many to Christ; and how the two Hewalds his companions suffered martyrdom.

Now when the man of the Lord, Egbert, perceived that neither was he suffered himself to go to preach to the heathen, but was stayed for some other commodity of the holy Church, whereof he had warning by an oracle of God; neither did Witbert coming into those parts profit anything: he assayed yet to send for the setting forth of the word holy men willing to take pains, amongst whom the excellent Wilbrord was chief for his merit and degree of priest.
