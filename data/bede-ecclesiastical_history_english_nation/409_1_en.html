And after his death Forthere was made bishop in his place, who is living to this day; a man too himself much conversant in Holy Scripture.

And when these2 had governance of the bishopric it was determined by decree in the synod, that the province of the South Saxons, which to that day appertained to the diocese of Winchester, over which Daniel then was, should also have a see and bishop of their own: and Eadbert, abbot of the monastery of bishop Wilfrid of blessed memory, called Selsey, was consecrated their first bishop; and when he died Eolla took the office of bishop upon him. And when he some years past was taken away from this life, the bishopric was left vacant to this day.
