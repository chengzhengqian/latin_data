II

[Scaliger was the first to distinguish this as a separate poem: in the MSS. it runs on after Elegia I without break.]

Thus spoke Maecenas at the coming of fate, chill on the very brink of death. “Why,” said he, “did I not sink in death, Ο Jupiter, before young Drusus’ narrow day of life?
