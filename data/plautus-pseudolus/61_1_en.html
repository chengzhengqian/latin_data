bal

Haha! Now at last I can relax since that chap has left and taken the woman away. Now let Pseudolus come, the fount of iniquity, and take her away from me with his1055 tricks. I know quite for sure, I’d rather perjure myself with solemn words a thousand times over than let him make a laughingstock of me. Now I’ll make fun of him if I meet him; but I believe he’ll be in the mill, as was1060 agreed. Now I’d like Simo to come my way so that he may be equally joyful on account of my joy.
