Pseudolus

pseu

Can’t you look back here just this once, Ballio, since there’s profit in it?

bal

(finally turning round) I’ll look back on that consideration;265 yes, if I were sacrificing to Jupiter above and holding the inward parts in my hands so as to offer them, and if in the meantime some profit were offered to me, I’d rather leave the sacrifice. One can’t stand in the way of this sacred duty, be everything else as it may.

pseu

(aside) He cares absolutely nothing for the gods, whom one ought to fear absolutely.

bal

(aside) I’ll address him. (to Pseudolus) My warmest270 greetings, worst slave in Athens.

pseu

May the gods and goddesses love you according to my master’s wish or mine, or if you deserve otherwise, may they not love you and not do you any good turns.

bal
