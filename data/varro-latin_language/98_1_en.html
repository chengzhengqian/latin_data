On the Latin Language, VI

The Saturnalia ‘Festival of Saturn’ was named from Saturn, because on this daye was his festival, as on the second day thereafter the Opalia,f the festival of Ops.

23. The Angeronalia,a from Angerona,b to whom a sacrifice is made in the Acculeian Curia and of whom this day is a state festival. The Larentine Festival,c which certain writers call the Larentalia, was named from Acca Larentia,d to whom our priests officially perform ancestor-worship on the sixth day after the Saturnalia,e which day is from her called the Day of the Parentalia of Larentine Acca.f

24. This sacrifice is made in the Velabrum, where it ends in New Street, as certain authorities say, at the tomb of Acca, because near there the priests make offering to the departed spirits of the slavesa: both these placesb were outside the ancient city, not far from the Little Roman Gate, of which I spoke in the preceding book.c Septimontium Day was named from these septem montes ‘seven hills,’e on which the City is set; it is a holiday not of the people generally, but only of those who live on the hills, as only those who are of some pagus ‘country district’ have a holidayf at the Paganaliag ‘Festival of the Country Districts.’
