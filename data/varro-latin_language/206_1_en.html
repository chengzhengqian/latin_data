XXXI. 56. If words which develop from proper names ought to be alike if from like names, then since Parma and Roma are identical we shall say Romenses,a like Parmenses; or since Roma, Nola, Parma are alike, we shall say Parmani,a like Romani and Nolani. And from Pergamum and Ilium we shall have not only Pergamenus ‘Pergamene,’ but also Ilienusa; or like Ilius and Ilia ‘Ilian’ male and female, we shall say Pergamusa and Pergamaa respectively for a man and a woman of Pergamum. And since Asia and Libya are like names, we shall call the people Asiatic and Libyatic.b
