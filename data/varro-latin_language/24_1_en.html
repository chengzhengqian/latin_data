51. To the third region belong five hills, named from sanctuaries of gods; among these hills are two that are well-known. The Viminal Hill got its name from Jupiter Viminius ‘of the Osiers,’ because there was his altar; but there are somea who assign its name to the fact that there were vimineta ‘willow-copses’ there. The Quirinal Hill was so named because there was the sanctuary of Quirinusb; othersc say that it is derived from the Quirites, who came with Tatius from Curesd to the vicinity of Rome, because there they established their camp.
