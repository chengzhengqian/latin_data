71. In Ennius we finda:

Treasures which ten of the Coclites buried, High on the tops of Rhiphaean mountains.

Codes ‘one-eyed’ was derived from oculus ‘eye,’ as though ocles,b and denoted a person who had only one eye; therefore in the Curculioc there is this:

I think that you are from the race of Coclites; For they are one-eyed.
