82. The numerals which do not signify money, from quattuor ‘four’ to centum ‘hundred,’ have forms of triple function,a because quattuor is masculine, feminine, and neuter. When mille ‘thousand’ is reached, it takes on a fourth function,b that of a singular neuter, because the expression in use is mille ‘thousand’ of denarii,c from which is made a plural, milia ‘thousands’ of denarii.
