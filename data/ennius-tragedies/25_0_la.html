CRESPHONTES (F 43–48)

This drama may be about Cresphontes the Elder or Cresphontes the Younger. Cresphontes the Elder and his brothers conquered the Peloponnese and then divided it up; Cresphontes gained Messenia by deceit when the lots were cast.
