TELAMO (F 117–24)

Ennius’ tragedy seems to have focused on the conflict between the father Telamon and his son Teucer. The fragments include a dispute between Telamon and Teucer (F 118, 120, 123, 124), thoughts by Telamon on the care of the gods (F 117), and the description of the grief of a woman (F 122). The following unattributed fragments may belong to this tragedy: Enn. Trag. F 160 and Trag. inc. inc. 85–87 R.2–3 = F 58 TrRF. On the plot and the order of fragments, cf. Hermann 1838, 378–82; Ladewig 1848, 24.
