IPHIGENIA (F 82–88)

Tragedies of the same title are known for the Greek dramatists Aeschylus (F 94 TrGF), Sophocles (F 305–12 TrGF), Euripides (Iphigenia Taurica and Iphigenia Aulidensis) and Polyidus (?) (78 F 1–2 TrGF [Ἰφιγενεία ἡ ἐν Ταύροις?]) as well as for the Roman poet Naevius (Trag. 19 R.2 = 16 R.3 = F 16 TrRF [fort. Taurica]).
