178 Varro, On the Latin Language

In Ennius:1

thrash him with words, see to it that he will just mutter (?), crushed2

mussare [“mutter”] <is> said, because muti [“the mute”] do not say more than “mu”; hence the same <poet> says this that is least [Inc. F 13]: “and, as they say, they do not dare to utter mu.”
