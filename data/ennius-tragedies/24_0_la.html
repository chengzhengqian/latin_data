ATHAMAS (F 42)

There are several versions of the myth of Athamas’ eventful life. Since the only surviving fragment of Ennius’ drama describes Bacchic revels, this may refer to the Bacchic celebrations on Parnassus, during which Ino, Athamas’ second wife, is said to have been rediscovered.
