AGAMEMNON

From her very breast the fierce Hippolyte watched him wrench the spoil; when the clouds were struck by arrows the Stymphalids fell from the high heavens. The tree prolific of golden apples shrank from his hands, unused to being plucked, and recoiled upward with lightened branches. The clink of metallic leaves was heard by the chill guard that knew no sleep, as Alcides was leaving the grove entirely empty of the tawny metal that filled his arms. The hound of the underworld, dragged aloft with triple chains, fell silent, barked with none of his mouths, fearing the color of the unknown light. When you led the attack, the Dardanid’s perjured house collapsed, and felt the arrows that would threaten again;49 when you led the attack, it fell in as many days as Troy took years to fall.
