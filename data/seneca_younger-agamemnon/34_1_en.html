AGAMEMNON

cassandra

Yes, and Priam as well.44

agamemnon

Here is not Troy.

cassandra

Where Helen is,45 I think it Troy.

agamemnon

Do not fear your mistress, though a slave.

cassandra

Freedom is close.

agamemnon

Live in security!

cassandra

For me death is security.

agamemnon

There is no danger for you.

cassandra

But great danger for you.

agamemnon

What can a conqueror fear?

cassandra

What he does not fear.

  • 44I.e., a king fated to be murdered in his own palace: Agamemnon.
  • 45I.e., Clytemnestra, as Helen’s sister and an adulteress and bringer of death like her. (Helen herself is not present.) That Cassandra glances at Clytemnestra is suggested by Agamemnon’s reply.
