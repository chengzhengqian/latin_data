I would have no harsh heroic thundering in lofty measures, but the kind of song you modulate to a quieter lyre, unadorned, when the cultured Muse runs through your playful verses. You could sound out too with deeper chords, as once you sang when the gods saw Titans overpowered by the lightning bolt, or when mountains, piled on top of lofty mountains, built a stairway for brutal monsters, when Pelion stood with Ossa piled on it, and pine-clad Olympus burdened them both.
