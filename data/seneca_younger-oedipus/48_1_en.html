lift up your heads, a kindlier condition of the skies follows my going. You who feebly retain the breath of life on your sickbeds may freely take in life-giving drafts of air. Go, bring help to those abandoned to die: I am drawing with me the deadly maladies of the land. Savage Fates, the shuddering tremor of Disease, Wasting and black Plague and ravening Pain, come with me, with me: I rejoice to have such guides as these.
