Menaechmi

  • A Married Woman wife of Menaechmus I
  • An Old Man father-in-law of Menaechmus I
  • A Doctor very convinced of his own skills
  • Slaves With Straps work for the old man
Staging

The stage represents a street in Epidamnus. The left-hand side of the street leads to the harbor, the right-hand side to the city center. On the street there are two houses; the left one belongs to Menaechmus I, the other belongs to Erotium.
