You reproached him for living apart from his father.18 This cannot now be made a matter of reproach to him at his time of life. He had just won, in a political case, a victory that was annoying to mec yet glorious to himself, and, besides, his age allowed him to aspire to public officesd; then, not only with the permission of his father, but even with his advice, he separated from him, and since his father’s house was a long way from the Forum, in order to be able to visit our housese more easily, and to keep in touch with his own friends, he took a house on the Palatine at a moderate rent.
