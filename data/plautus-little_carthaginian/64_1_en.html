I was delighted there today with the really pleasing gifts of prostitutes, worthy of the most charming goddess Venus, and I didn’t despise her powers today; such a great amount of lovely things was there, all put neatly in their place. Incense and the odor of myrrh and everything filled the altars. The feast day didn’t seem mean, Venus, and neither did1180 your shrine: such a great number of female devotees was there, who had come to the Venus of Calydon.
