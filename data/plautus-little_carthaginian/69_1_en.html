han

Pay attention, women. First, if it were possible that the gods should never inflict undeserved things on those who don’t deserve them, I would like that to have happened; but as things are, it’s right for us, me, you, and your mother, to give eternal thanks to the gods for the good things they’re giving us, since the immortal gods1255 approve of our piety and crown it. You’re both my daughters and this is your relative, the son of (points to himself) this man’s brother, Agorastocles.

ade

(to Anterastilis) Please, are they delighting us with false joy?

ago

(to the girls) No, as truly as the gods may preserve me, this is your father. Give him your hands.
