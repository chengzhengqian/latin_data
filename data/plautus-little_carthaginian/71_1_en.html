(to the audience) If I don’t take revenge properly for the1280 mina I gave to the pimp, then the men about town can indeed poke fun at me. He even invited me in for lunch, the big crook, went out himself, and left me as majordomo in the house. When neither pimp nor girls returned and I wasn’t given anything to eat, I took security1285 for the larger part of the lunch and left.
