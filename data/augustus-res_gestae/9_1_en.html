10. By decree of the senate my name was included in the Salian hymn,b and it was enacted by law that my person should be sacred in perpetuity and that so long as I lived I should hold the tribunician power.c I declined to be made Pontifex Maximus in succession to a colleague still living, when the people tendered me that priesthood which my father had held.
