The provinces of the Spains, the Gauls, Africa, Sicily, and Sardinia took the same oath of allegiance.a Those who served under my standards at that time included more than 700 senators,b and among them eighty-three who had previously or have since been consuls up to the day on which these words were written, and about 170 have been priests.
