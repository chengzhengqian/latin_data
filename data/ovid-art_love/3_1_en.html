Are you attracted by early and still ripening years? a real maid will come before your eyes. Would you have a full-grown beauty? a thousand such will please you, and you will be at a loss which one to desire. Or do you perchance prefer a later and staider age? still more numerous, believe me, will be their array.

Only walk leisurely beneath the Pompeian shade,3 when the sun draws nigh to Hercules’ shaggy lion, or where the mother has added her own gifts to her son’s,4 a work rich with marble coating.
