The Art of Love

or Nestor in understanding, as great as was Calchas at the sacrifice, or Telamon’s son in arms, or Automedon in the chariot, so great a lover am I. Celebrate me, the prophet, O ye men; sing my praises, let my name be sung in all the world. I have given you armour; Vulcan gave armour to Achilles; do ye conquer, as he conquered, by virtue of the gift. But whosoever shall by my steel lay low the Amazon, let him inscribe upon his spoils “Naso was my Master.”

Lo! the young women are begging me to give them counsel: you will be my poetry’s next care.
