380–1

Quintilian: And again the same poet—

To mendaci (a liar) and furl (a thief) add ‘e’ when you order anyone to give it to a thief (furei) or to a liar (mendacei). b

382–3

Velius Longus: Again—

“This was done Mi uni” (to him alone); this ‘i’ you will write simple. “All this illei (they) did;” add ‘e’ that it may become richer. c
