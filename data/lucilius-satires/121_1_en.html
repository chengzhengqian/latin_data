Nonius: ‘Pedetemtim’ (step by step) and ‘pedepressim’ are terms used for cautiously, as it were with a slow and tardy walk . . .—

He on the other hand, outdoors and amongst a crowd, does everything gently, step by step, lest he hurt anyone.
