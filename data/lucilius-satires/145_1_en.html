  • a Mango may be wrong here. If it is right, Lucilius makes a pun on mango, magno.
  • bor possibly ‘that any bait can be used to pull my leg’; bolus can mean (a) a throw at dice; (b) a cast of a fishing-net, with extensions of meaning implying gain or loss; (c) a titbit. Emungere is properly to wipe the nose, here to ‘deceive,’ ‘diddle.’
