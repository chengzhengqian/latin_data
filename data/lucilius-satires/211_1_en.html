Porphyrio on Hor., Ep., II, 2, 94–5 shows that Lucilius sometimes divided a word between two lines. We have one example of this in lines 78–9.

There are other expressions, even whole lines of Latin, attributed to Lucilius by modern scholars on slender or no evidence. They will be found in Marx, 1, pp. 92–5, and Terzaghi, pp. 74–6.
