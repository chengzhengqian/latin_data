But Aemilius had a plan:

Priscianus: ‘Nexi’ as a perfect . . . —

‘he alone, I think was wide awake, and while he seemed to me to be doing this (i.e. hesitating), he also strung nets.’

and C. Matienus, duumvir of the fleet, was to help

Nonius: ‘Cernere’ also means to arrange . . . —

245

Praise for Aemilius—and Lucilius a :
