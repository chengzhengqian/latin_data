“At this proclamation from Mercury, the lust for so splendid a reward aroused the competitive zeal of every mortal man. This circumstance more than all else put an end to Psyche’s hesitation. As she was approaching her mistress’s door, one of Venus’ domestic staff, named Habit, ran towards her and instantly began shouting at the top of her voice. ‘So you have finally come to recognise that you have a mistress, you worthless hussy! This would be just like your usual thoughtless behaviour, pretending not to know how much trouble we have had to suffer in our search for you. But it’s a good job that I’m the one into whose hands you have fallen, for now you are caught in the very clutches of Death8 and you will certainly pay the penalty without delay for your gross insubordination.’
