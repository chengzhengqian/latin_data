Spangled with many golden amulets and shining like the starry sky, you will march triumphantly in the people’s joyous parades; in a silken apron I will bring you nuts and soft dainties, my saviour, and I will feed you full every day.

Yet29 in the midst of this fine food and the serene repose and complete happiness of your daily life, you will also enjoy this glorious honour. I will put a seal on the memory of my present fortune and of divine providence by giving a lasting testimony, and I will have a panel painted with the picture of our present escape and enshrine it in the entrance-hall of my home. People will come to see this simple tale, and will hear about it when stories are told, and the pens of the learned will perpetuate it. ‘A royal maiden flees captivity riding on an ass.’ You yourself will be added to the ancient tales of wonder, and from the fact of your actual existence we will now believe that Phrixus swam the sea on a ram’s back, that Arion piloted a dolphin, and that Europa rode on the back of a bull.28 But if Jupiter truly bellowed with the throat of a bull, perhaps in this ass I am riding lurks the face of a man or the likeness of a god.”

While the girl was repeating these sentiments again and again, mingling frequent sighs with her prayers, we arrived at a fork in the road. She seized my halter and tried hard to turn me to the right, because that was evidently the way to her parents’.
