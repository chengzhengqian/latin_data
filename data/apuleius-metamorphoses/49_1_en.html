“I was terrified at his words and started to test Fortune.34 I put my hand up and grasped my nose: it came away; I rubbed my ears: they fell off. The crowd were pointing their fingers at me and twisting their heads round to nod at me, and laughter broke out. Dripping with cold sweat, I escaped through the legs of the surrounding mob. I could never afterwards return to my ancestral home so maimed and so ludicrous, but I have let my hair grow long on both sides to hide the scars of my ears, and I have tightly attached this linen bandage for decency’s sake to conceal the shame of my nose.”
