“How I wish,” she replied, “that I could get you what you desire, Lucius. But, besides her nasty disposition, she always performs secret acts of this sort in seclusion and divorced from all company. Still, I will put your request ahead of my personal danger; I will watch for an opportune occasion and try very hard to accomplish what you want. Only, as I told you at the beginning, give me your promise and your silence about so serious a matter.”

As we chattered so, mutual desire excited our emotions and bodies alike. We threw off all our clothes and then, completely uncovered and naked, performed an orgy to Venus. When I was tired, Photis of her own generosity played the boy’s part with me as a bonus. Finally, when our eyes were drooping from lack of sleep, slumber flowed into them and held us fast until full daylight.
