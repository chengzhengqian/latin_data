“I grant the existence of the gods: do you then(2) Argument from atomism refuted: atomic doctrine opposed to science, teach me their origin, their dwelling-place, their bodily and spiritual nature, their mode of life; for these are the things which I want to know. In regard to all of them you make great play with the lawless domination of the atoms; from these you construct and create everything that comes upon the ground,b as they say. Now in the first place, there are no such things as atoms. For there is nothing . . .c incorporeal, but all space is filled with material bodies; hence there can be no such thing as void, and no such 66 thing as an indivisible body.
