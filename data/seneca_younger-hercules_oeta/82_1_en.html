HERCULES ON OETA

chorus

Never does glorious valor pass to the Stygian shades. Live, all, with courage, and the cruel fates will then not haul you over Lethe River. No: when the final hour is imposed at the end of your days, glory will open a path to heaven. But you, great conqueror of beasts and bringer of peace to the world, be with us! Continue to show regard for our earth, and if some new and strange-faced monster shakes the nations with grievous fear, crush it with the three-forked lightning bolts; hurl the lightning more valiantly than your very father.
