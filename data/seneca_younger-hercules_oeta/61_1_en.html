Stop these terrible threats prompted by your anger, father. It’s done, all over!88 She has paid the penalty you demand. Mother lies dead, slain by her own hand.

hercules

She died from her distress? She deserved to perish at the hands of Hercules in his rage. Lichas has lost a counterpart. Impulse and anger drive me berserk, even against her lifeless body. Why should she be spared my violence even as a corpse? Let wild beasts have her to feed on!

hyllus

The poor woman suffered more distress than the one she harmed; you might even have wanted to lighten it. She died by her own hand, but out of distress for you. She endured more than you are demanding. But it was no crime of a murderous wife that brought you down, no treachery of my mother’s. Nessus was the one that devised this trick, when he was hit by your arrows and breathed his last. The robe was smeared with the blood of that half-beast, father; now Nessus is exacting his recompense.
