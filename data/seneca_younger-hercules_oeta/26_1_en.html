Now too, in any plight you fear, take us loyal friends to share your fate; for loyalty is rare once good fortune begins to fail. You that hold a scepter, whoever you are: granted, the whole population together may knock at the hundred doors of your palace; but though so many people throng around you, among so many people there is scarcely one loyal. The Erinys holds the gilded doorway, and when the great doors open wide, in come betrayals and cagey tricks and the hidden sword; and when you prepare to go out in public, at your side is Envy. Each time the dawn supplants the night, each time consider a king newborn.47 Few cherish kings and not their thrones; most are drawn by the glitter of court.
