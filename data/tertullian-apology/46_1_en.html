XIX. Supreme antiquity, then, claims for these books the highest authority. And among you it is almost a superstition to make credit depend on time elapsed.

d [Extreme antiquity gives books authority. For Moses was the first prophet. He began in the past with foundation of the world, the production of mankind, and later on the mighty cataclysm that avenged the iniquity of that age; by prophecy down to his own day and thereafter, in his own story he gave pictures of things yet to be. In his book the sequence of events set in order from the beginning has permitted the computation of the world’s age.
