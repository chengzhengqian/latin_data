Great Jupiter, you save me, prosper my property, and bring me huge and lavish prosperity, praise, profit, pleasure,770 laughter, liveliness, and leisure, a parade of dishes, provisions, parties for drinking, fullness, joy. I definitely won’t seek anyone’s goodwill from now on; I can help a friend or destroy an enemy: this beautiful day has loaded me down with beautiful beauty.
