Enter HEGIO from his house, followed by a SLAVE-OVERSEER.
heg

Pay attention, will you? Those two prisoners I bought110 from among the spoils from the quaestors yesterday, put one of these separate chains on each of them and take off the heavier ones they’re bound with now. Let them walk around outside or inside if they wish, provided they’re115 watched over very carefully. A free man taken prisoner is like a wild bird: once he’s given a chance of escape, it’s enough, you can never catch him afterwards.

over

Well, we’d all rather be free than slaves.120

You at any rate don’t seem to be like that. 6

over

If I don’t have anything to give you, do you want me to give you . . . the slip?

If you give me the slip, there will immediately be something I can give you.

I’ll be like a wild bird, just as you tell me.
