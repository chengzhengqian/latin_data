Why should I demand to be well against your will? The740 risk to my life is at your own risk. After death, there is no evil in death for me to fear. Even if I live to a great age, it’s still only a short period for me to bear what you’re threatening me with. Farewell and be well, even though you deserve that I should say otherwise. You, Aristophontes,745 may you fare as you’ve deserved of me: this has happened to me because of you.

That’s violence, being pulled and pushed at the same750 time.

heg

That man’s been taken away into custody directly as he deserves. I’ll give those other prisoners a warning example so that nobody dares to begin such a trick. If it hadn’t754 been for the man who revealed it to me they’d have bridled me and led me on with their tricks permanently. Now I definitely won’t trust anyone in anything in future. Once bitten, twice shy. Dear me, I was hoping that I’d ransomed my son from slavery; that hope has fallen through.
