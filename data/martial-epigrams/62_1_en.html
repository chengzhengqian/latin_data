What good is it to me, a little book so slender that it is no thicker than any roller stick, 9 if it would take you three days to get through all of it? Never was aesthete so languid. Does the traveller flag so quickly? When you ought to drive to Bovillae, 10 do you want to change horses at the Camenae? Go ahead, tell me to publish my little books!

7

You’re a pretty declaimer, Attalus, a pretty pleader, you write pretty histories and pretty poems, you compose mimes prettily, epigrams prettily, you’re a pretty grammarian and a pretty astronomer, you’re prettily versed in the art of the lyre and likewise in the art of the ball. Seeing that you do nothing well but everything prettily, shall I tell you what you are? You’re a great trifler.
