Puffed up and delighting overmuch in an empty name, 20 and ashamed, wretch, to be Saturninus, you stirred up impious war beneath the Parrhasian bear like him who bore the arms of a Pharian consort. 21 Had you so completely forgotten the fate of this name, which the fearsome wrath of the Actian strait overwhelmed? Or did Rhine promise you what Nile did not give him, and was it likely that Arctic waters would be granted greater license? Even that other Antony fell to our arms, who compared to you, traitor, was a Caesar.
