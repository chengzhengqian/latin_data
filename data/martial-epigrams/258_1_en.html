Epigrams Book VII

70

Lesbia of the very Lesbians, Philaenis, rightly do you call the girl you fuck your girl-friend. 104

71

The wife has figs, 105 so does the husband, the daughter has figs and the son-in-law and the grandson. Neither the steward nor the bailiff nor the rugged ditcher is without the horrid growth, nor yet the ploughman. Since young and old alike have figs, it is remarkable that only the land is without them.
