Epigrams Book III

75

Your cock gave up standing long ago, Lupercus, and yet, crazy that you are, you struggle to rise. But rocket and lascivious onions have no effect, nor does shameless savory 84 help you any more. You have started corrupting pure mouths with your money; even so the badgered part doesn’t come alive. Could anyone wonder enough at this or believe it? What doesn’t stand, Lupercus, stands you in for a bundle. 85

76

You rise at old women, Bassus, you despise girls; not beauty but approaching death attracts you. I ask, is this not madness, is this not a crazy cock? You can do Hecuba, but you can’t do Andromache.

77

Mullet does not please you, Baeticus, neither does thrush. You never care for hare, nor boar either. Cookies are not to your liking nor squares of cut cake, neither do Libya and Phasis send their birds 86 for you. You devour capers and onions afloat in putrid fish sauce and flesh from a dubious ham.
