61

The audacious retailers had appropriated the entire city; no threshold kept within its own bounds. You bade 89 the narrow streets expand, Germanicus, and what had lately been a track became a road. No column 90 is girt with chained flagons, and the praetor is not forced to walk through the mud. The razor is not drawn blindly in a dense crowd, nor does the grimy cook shop monopolize the whole street. The barber, the taverner, the cook, the butcher keep to their own thresholds. Now it is Rome, it used to be a big shop.
