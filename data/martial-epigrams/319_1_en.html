Antistius Rusticus has died in the cruel land of Cappadocia. O guilty region, grievous crime! Nigrina brought back her dear husband’s bones in her bosom and complained that the journey was not long enough. When she gave the hallowed urn to the tomb she envies, she felt robbed of her man and widowed a second time.

When Velius was at Caesar’s side in Arctic warfare, he vowed this bird 49 to Mars for his general’s behoof. The moon had not quite completed eight orbits before the god was demanding the vow already due. 50 The goose hurried joyfully to its appointed altar, 51 and fell, a small offering, victim to the sacred hearth.
