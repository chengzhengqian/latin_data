You are marrying your lover, Proculina, and making the adulterer of yesterday your husband so that the Julian law can’t put a mark against you. That’s not a marriage, Proculina, it’s a confession.

23

You are always telling my cock to stand for you, Lesbia. Believe me, a cock is not a finger. For all your urging with seductive hands and words, your face 25 is in authority against you.

24

Carisianus 26 is the drollest of beings: he walks about in a gown during the Saturnalia. 27
