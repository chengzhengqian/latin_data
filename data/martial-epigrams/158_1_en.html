Whoa, there’s enough, whoa now, little book! We have got to the bosses. 128 But you want to go on further and keep going, there’s no holding you at the final sheet, as though you had not finished the business which was finished even on page one. 129 Already the reader grows querulous and weary, already the very copyist says “Whoa, there’s enough, whoa now, little book!”

  • 128The singular in the sense of roller-stick would seem more appropriate; cf. 2.6.11n, Hor. Epod. 14.8. But cf. 11.107.1.
  • 129I.e. the book might as well never have been written.
