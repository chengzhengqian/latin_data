Lydia is as spacious as the arse of a brazen horseman, 43 as a swift hoop, noisy with its clattering bronze, 44 as the wheel through which the acrobat often leaps without touching it, 45 as an old shoe soaked in muddy water, as the wide-meshed nets that wait for stray thrushes, as the awnings denied by the South Wind 46 in Pompey’s theater, as an armlet that slipped from a consumptive catamite, as a mattress divorced from its Leuconian stuffing, as the old breeches of a pauper Briton, and as the ugly throat of a pelican 47 of Ravenna. I am said to have fucked her in amarine fishpond. I don’t know; I think I fucked the fishpond.
