When Rome, goddess of lands and nations, that has no equal and no second, was joyfully reckoning Trajan’s 12 future years through so many generations, and saw in so great a Leader a soldier, brave and young and martial, proud of such a ruler she spoke: “Nobles of Parthia and chieftains of the Seres, ye Thracians, Sarmatians, Getans, Britons, I can show you a Caesar: come.”
