3. Tablets of citrus wood

If we had not been cut into thin tablets, we should be the noble burden of a Libyan tusk. 7

4. Five-leaved tablets

The happy forecourt of the master is warm with the slaughter of steers when exalted honor is granted by a five-leaved wax tablet. 8

5. Ivory tablets

Lest somber wax dim your failing eyes, let black letters paint snow-white ivory for your use.

6. Three-leaved tablets

You will think our three-leaved tablets no paltry gift 9 when your mistress writes to you that she is coming.
