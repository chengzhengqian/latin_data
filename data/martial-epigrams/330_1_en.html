On Kinsfolk Day, when many a bird is sent as a present, as I make thrushes ready for Stella and for you, Flaccus, a great, burdensome crowd comes to my mind, in which each thinks himself my prime favorite. 92 To oblige two is my desire; but to offend more is hardly safe, while to send presents to many is onerous. I shall earn pardon the only way I can, and give thrushes neither to Stella nor to you, Flaccus.

56

Spendophorus heads for Libya’s cities as his master’s armour-bearer. Cupid, prepare weapons to give the boy, those with which you pierce young men and soft girls. But let there be also a smooth spear in his tender hand.
