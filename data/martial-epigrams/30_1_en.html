Neither did rustic Sassina send her milky cones nor came olives from Picene jars. No, naked boar, and a tiny one at that, such as an unarmed midget might lay low. And nothing of it came our way, we all just watched. The arena is apt to serve us boar in that fashion. 51 After such behavior I hope that boar will never again be served you, but that you will be served to the same boar as Charidemus. 52

Do my pages large and small 53 contain the gamesome runs of hares and the play of lions? Do I do the same thing twice over? Well, if this seems too much to you, Stella, why not on your side give me hare twice over for dinner?
