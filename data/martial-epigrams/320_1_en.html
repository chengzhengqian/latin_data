Jupiter laughed at the falsehood of his Idaean tomb when he saw the Flavian temple of the Augustan heaven; 52 and at table, already drenched with copious nectar, as he handed the cup to son Mars, looking the while at Phoebus and Phoebus’ sister (Alcides and the loyal Arcadian 53 were there too), “You gave me,” he said, “a Gnosian monument. See how much greater a thing it is to be Caesar’s father.”
