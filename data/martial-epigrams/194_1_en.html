2

It used to be a game to betray the sacred marriage torch and a game to castrate innocent males. You forbid both, 1 Caesar, and come to the aid of future generations; for by your order their birth is made safe. Under your rule no man shall be either eunuch or adulterer. Formerly (alas for our morals!) even a eunuch was an adulterer.
