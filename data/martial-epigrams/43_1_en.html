Charinus is in the pink, and yet he’s pale. Charinus drinks sparingly, and yet he’s pale. Charinus has a good digestion, and yet he’s pale. Charinus goes out in the sun, and yet he’s pale. Charinus paints his skin, and yet he’s pale. Charinus licks a cunt, and yet he’s pale. 96

Dry-eyed himself but with words of comfort for his weeping friends, he resolved to go to the Stygian pool.
