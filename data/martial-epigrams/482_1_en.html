96

Since your husband’s life and fidelity is known to you and no other woman presses or threatens your marriage bed, why do you foolishly torture yourself because of pages, as though they were your rivals? Such affairs are short and fleeting. I shall show you that the boys do more for you than for their master. Thanks to them you are the only woman for your husband. They give him what you his wife don’t want to give. “But I will give it,” you say, “so that my spouse’s love doesn’t go gadding from our bedroom.” That is not the same thing. I want a Chian fig, not one of the big sort. And in case you are in any doubt as to which is the Chian, the big one is yours. A married lady and a woman ought to know her limitations. Leave their part 138 to the boys and use yours.

97

Your wife is a girl such as a husband would hardly ask for in his most extravagant prayers, rich, noble, cultivated, virtuous. You burst your loins, Bassus, but you do it with long-haired boys whom you have procured for yourself
