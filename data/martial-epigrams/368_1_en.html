If she had been your schoolfellow or your teacher, Sappho, you would have been more accomplished and you would have been chaste; but unkind Phaon would have loved Sulpicia, if he had seen her and you side by side—in vain, for she would not live as the Thunderer’s spouse or Bacchus’ girl or Apollo’s, should Calenus be taken from her. 63

36

Whatever the unconscionable 64 smoke rooms of Massilia bring together, 65 whatever jar takes age from fire, comes from you, Munna. You send your unfortunate friends dire poisons by sea and length of road, and at no easy price, but one which would content a flagon of Falernian or Setine, beloved of its cellars. 66 Methinks the reason why it is so long since you came to Rome is to avoid drinking your own wines.
