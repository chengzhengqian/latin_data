Greatest of censors, prince of princes, though Rome already owes you so many triumphs, so many temples coming to birth, so many reborn, so many spectacles, so many gods, so many cities, she owes you more because she is chaste.

I have bought a country property for a large sum and I ask you, Caecilianus, to lend me a hundred thousand. Don’t you answer me? I think you say to yourself: “You won’t pay it back.” That is why I ask, Caecilianus. 4

6

There are three actors in a comedy, but your Paula, Lupercus, loves four. Paula loves a muta persona 5 too.
