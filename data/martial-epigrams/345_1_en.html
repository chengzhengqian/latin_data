If I were invited to dinner in different heavens by Caesar’s summoner on the one hand and Jupiter’s on the other, though the stars 149 were closer and the Palace farther off, I should give this answer to be returned to the High Ones: “Seek on for one who would rather be the Thunderer’s 150 guest: my Jupiter, see, keeps me on earth.” 151

92

Condylus, you lament that you have been so long a slave; you don’t know a master’s afflictions and a slave’s advantages.
