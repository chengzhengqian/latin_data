83

When your mind’s at ease, you’re the worst fellow in the world, Naevolus; when you’re worried, you’re the best. When your mind’s at ease, you return nobody’s “good day,” you scorn us all, no one’s a free man to you, nobody exists. 121 Worried, you make presents, greet as “lord” and “patron,” ask to dinner. Be worried, Naevolus.

84

There’s nobody in the community, not in all Rome, who can prove that Thais has been fucked by himself, though many desire and beg. Is Thais so virtuous? I ask. No, she sucks.

85

We drink from glass, you from murrine, Ponticus. For why? A transparent cup would reveal two wines.
