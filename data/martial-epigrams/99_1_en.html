27

You never invite me back, though you often come to dinner at my invitation. I forgive you, Gallus, if only you invite nobody. But you invite others. Both of us have a fault. “What fault?” say you. I have no sense and you, Gallus, have no sense of decency.

28

You are surprised that Marius’ ear smells disagreeably. That is your doing. You chatter, Nestor, in his ear.
