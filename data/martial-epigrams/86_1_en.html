Epigrams Book II

83

Husband, you mutilated your wife’s unhappy lover, and his face, maimed of nose and ears, misses its former self. Do you suppose you are sufficiently avenged? You err. He can also give suck. 80

84

The hero son of Poeas81 was an effeminate who yielded easily to men. Thus Venus is said to have avenged Paris’ wounds. That is why Sicilian Sertorius licks a cunt. It seems he killed 82 another Eryx, Rufus.

85

Water boiled and iced protected by a container of light wickerwork shall be your present at this Saturn’s season. If you grumble at my sending you a summer gift in December, why don’t you send me a shorn 83 gown?
