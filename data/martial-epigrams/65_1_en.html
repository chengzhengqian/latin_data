You pass your cup for a pledge to no one, Hormus. That is humanity, not arrogance.

16

Zoilus is ill. His bedclothes make this fever. If he gets well, what will be the use of his scarlet coverlets or an underblanket 21 from Nile or one dyed in smelly Sidonian purple? What but sickness shows off such silly wealth? What do you want with doctors? Dismiss all the Machaons. Do you want to get well? Take my bedclothes.
