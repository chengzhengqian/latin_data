Epigrams Book XIII

115. Caecuban

Generous Caecuban 120 is ripened at Amyclae near Fundi. The green vine is born in the middle of the marsh.

116. Signine

Will you drink Signine, that checks loose bowels? 121 Lest you repress them too much, let your thirst be sparing.

117. Mamertine

If a jar of Mamertine 122 as old as Nestor be given to you, it can bear any name you please. 123

118. Tarragonese

Tarraco, that will yield only to the vintages of Campania, gave birth to these wines, which rival Latin 124 jars.

119. Nomentan

A Nomentan vintage gives you my wine. If Quintus 125 loves you, you will drink better.
