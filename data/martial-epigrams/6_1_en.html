15 (13)

A mother sow, struck by a heavy weapon and laid open by the wound, lost life and gave it at one and the same time. How sure was the hand that poised the steel! I believe this hand was Lucina’s. Dying, the creature sampled the divine power of either Diana. 26 By one the parent was delivered, by the other the beast was slain.

16 (14)

A wild sow, now pregnant, sent forth her progeny, pledge of her ripe womb, made parent by a wound. Nor did the offspring lie on the ground, but ran as the mother fell. How ingenious are sudden chances! 27
