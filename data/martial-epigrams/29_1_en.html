When Porcia heard of her husband Brutus’ fate and her grief sought the weapons that had been put out of its reach, “Have you not learned,” she said, “that there is no denying death? I had thought my father 50 had taught that lesson by the manner of his dying.” So saying, she eagerly imbibed glowing embers. What now, you tiresome servitors? Deny steel?
