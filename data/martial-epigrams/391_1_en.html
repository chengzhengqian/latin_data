You take down all the praetors’ documents, Cotta, and you accept wax tablets. You are a conscientious fellow. 153

89

Your work and happy glory, Polyclitus, glory that Phidias’ hands would have been fain to earn, Juno shines with a countenance that would have overcome her fellow-goddesses on Ida—the judge 154 would not have hesitated. If her brother 155 did not love his own Juno, Polyclitus, her brother 156 might have loved yours.
