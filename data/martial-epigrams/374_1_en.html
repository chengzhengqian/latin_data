When my guests are satisfied, I shall offer ripe fruit and leesless wine from a Nomentan flagon twice three years old in Frontinus’ consulship. 86 To boot there will be merriment free of malice, frank speech that gives no anxiety the morning after, nothing you would wish you hadn’t said. Let my guest talk of Scorpus 87 and the Green; let my cups get no man put on trial.
