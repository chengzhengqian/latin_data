With them the confectioner tries out his skills, cunningly putting variously flavored Capelliana 53 into a rue-leaf. So he fills side dishes and platters and polished saucers and hollow plates. You don’t have a gown or a fireplace or a bug-trodden bed or a patched up mat of thirsty rushes or a boy slave or an older one or a maidservant or a baby or a key or a dog or a cup. 55 Yet, Nestor, you aspire to be called and seen as a poor man and seek to have a place in the community. You lie and flatter yourself with a false honor. It is not poverty, Nestor, to have nothing.
