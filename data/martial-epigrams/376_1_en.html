Numa saw Thelys the eunuch in a gown and said he was a convicted adulteress. 100

53

I am Scorpus, the glory of the clamorous circus, your applause, Rome, and brief darling. Envious Lachesis snatched me away ere my thirtieth year, 101 but, counting my victories, believed me an old man.

54

You set out good tables, Olus, but set them out covered. It’s absurd. At that rate, I can own good tables.
