You have had your fun, it’s enough. Marry, you wanton cunts. Only virtuous love is permitted you. 54 Is this virtuous love? Laetoria marries Lygdus. As a wife she will be more disreputable than she lately was as an adulteress.

The team of the Blues is lashed and lashed, but doesn’t run. It’s doing a big job, Catianus. 55

47
