Epigrams Book II

38

Linus, you ask me what I get out of my land near Nomentum. This is what I get out of the land: I don’t see you, Linus.

39

You give scarlet and violet dresses to a notorious adulteress. Would you like to give her a present she has deserved? Send her a gown. 37

40

Tongilius is said to have a severe attack of semi-tertian. I know the fellow’s tricks: he is hungry and thirsty. Now the crafty nets are set for fat thrushes, the hook is thrown to mullet and pike. Caecuban is strained and dark Falernian, the ripenings of Opimius’ year, is put in small glass bottles. 38 All his doctors ordered Tongilius to take baths. Fools, do you believe this is fever? It’s greed.
