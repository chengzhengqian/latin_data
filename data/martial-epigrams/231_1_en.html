7

Though the wintry Bear and rude Peuce and Hister warming to the beat of hooves and Rhine, whose presumptious horn has now been shattered for the third time, 9 detain you, the world’s supreme ruler and earth’s parent, as you subdue the realms of a perfidious race, yet you cannot be absent from our prayers. We are there, Caesar, with our eyes and our minds. And so wholly do you alone hold the thoughts of all that the very crowd in the great Circus knows not whether Passerinus runs or Tigris. 10
