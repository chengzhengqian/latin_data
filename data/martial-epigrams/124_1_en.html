Epigrams Book III

98

You ask how lean is your arse, Sabellus? You can sodomize with your arse.

99

You ought not to be angry, Cerdo, with my little book. Your craft has been wounded by my verse, 116 not your reputation. Permit harmless jests. Why should I not be allowed to joke, if you were allowed to slaughter?

100

I sent your runner back to you at midday, Rufus, and I imagine he was wet through when he brought my verses, for the sky happened to be pouring with torrential rain. That book deserved to be sent in no other way. 117
