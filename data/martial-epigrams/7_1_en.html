18 (16)

The bull was snatched up in the midst of the arena and departed for the sky. This was the work, not of art, but of devotion. 30

19 (16b)

A bull had carried Europa through his brother’s sea; 31 but now a bull has borne Alcides to the stars. 32 Compare now, Fame, the steers of Caesar and of Jove; even though they bore an equal load, this one bore his load higher.
