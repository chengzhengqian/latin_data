Epigrams Book VI

25

Marcellinus, true son of a good father, whom the shivering Bear covers with her Parrhasian 28 wagon, hear what an old friend and your father’s friend wishes for you, and keep these prayers in a mindful heart: that your courage be cautious and rash ardor not carry you into the thick of swords and cruel spears. Let crazy folk crave war and savage Mars. You can be both your father’s soldier and your Leader’s. 29

26

Our friend Sotades’ head is in danger. 30 You think Sotades is on trial? Not so. Sotades can no longer erect: he licks.
