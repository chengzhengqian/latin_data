What a hero you are, who can do without dregs of red vinegar and straw and black bread! Come, let your pillow swell with Leuconian wool, 85 and silky purple drape your couches, and a boy sleep with you who lately tormented the guests with his rosy face as he mixed the Caecuban: oh, how eager you will be to live three time the years of Nestor, how you will want to lose no instant of any day! It is easy to hold life cheap with straightened means: he who can be wretched plays the man.

Are you surprised, Severus, that I send verses to a poet when I invite you, poet Severus, to dinner? Jupiter has his fill of ambrosia and lives on nectar; yet we give raw entrails and wine to Jove. Since all things have been granted you by the gods’ gift, if you don’t want what you already have, what then will you accept?
