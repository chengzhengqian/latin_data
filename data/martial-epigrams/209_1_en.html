You pay no attention and hear me this while with languid ear, seeming not to care. You will die unbathed, Oppianus.

43

While happy Baiae indulges you, Castricus, and you swim in water white with sulphur springs, the ease of my Nomentan farm and a cottage not oppressive to its acres 52 restores me. This is for me the sunshine of Baiae and soft Lucrine, this is for me, Castricus, your rich men’s resorts. Time was when I would gladly run anywhere I fancied to fashionable waters, had no fear of long journeys. But nowadays I like places near the city, retreats within easy reach; it’s enough if I can be lazy.
