Gold-inlaid plate is always placed before Calpetanus whether he dines abroad or at home in Rome. He always dines so even in an inn or in the country. Has he no other service then? Not so, he has no service of his own. 111

  • 111“Calpetanus” is satirized for his ostentatious use of plate which is not his own.
