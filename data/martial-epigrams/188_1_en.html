74

Asia and Europe cover Pompey’s sons, but the earth of Libya, if any earth, covers himself. No wonder he is scattered over the whole world. So vast a ruin could not lie in one spot. 142

75

Quintus, you can call Laelia, who married you on account of the law, 143 your lawful wife.

76

By often drinking poison Mithridates achieved immunity from pernicious drugs. You too, Cinna, by always dining so badly 144 have taken care that you never die of hunger.

77

Someone is said to have made a neat remark, Marullus: he said you carry oil in your ear. 145

  • 142For a series of epigrams on this theme, perhaps by Seneca, see Anth. Lat. Bailey 396–399; 452–454 (= Riese 400–403; 454–456).
  • 143The Lex Julia against adultery, revived by Domitian: cf. 6.7.
  • 144Here referring to quantity rather than quality. “Cinna” accustomed himself to short commons.
  • 145You listen to great men with an ear as inclined as if you carried oil in it. Said “of flatterers, who say pleasant rather than salutary things”: Erasm. Adag. s.v. Oleum in auricula ferre (Ker).
