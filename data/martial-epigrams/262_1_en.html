“There are thirty bad epigrams in the whole book.” If there are as many good ones, Lausus, it’s a good book.

82

So large a sheath covers Menophilus’ penis that it would be enough by itself for all our comic actors. 126 I had supposed (we often bathe together) that he was anxious to spare his voice, Flaccus. But while he was in a game in the middle of the sportsground with everybody watching, the sheath slipped off the poor soul; he was circumcised.
