Epigrams Book IX

82

An astrologer said you would go to pot shortly, Munna, and I don’t think he lied when he said it. For fearing that you would leave something behind after your death, you drained your patrimony with your extravagance, and your two millions melted away in less than a year. Tell me, Munna, isn’t that going to pot shortly? 135

83

Among the mighty marvels of your arena, Caesar, which outdoes the famous shows of the old Leaders, our eyes acknowledge that they owe you much, but our ears more, in that habitual reciters become spectators. 136
