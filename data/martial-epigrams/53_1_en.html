You often take water in between your wine, Rufus, and if urged by a friend, you drink at rare intervals an ounce 120 of diluted Falernian. Has Naevia promised you a night of bliss and do you prefer sure fornication in sober naughtiness? You sigh, you say nothing, you groan. She has refused. Well then, you may drink bumper 121 after bumper and kill harsh pain with neat liquor. Why spare yourself, Rufus? You have to sleep.
