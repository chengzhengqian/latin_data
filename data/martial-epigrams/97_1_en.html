21

A servant with a branded forehead saved a proscribed man. 30 This was not saving his master’s life but putting him to shame. 31

22

Apicius, you had spent twice thirty million on your belly and there still remained to you a cool ten million. To you this was like hunger and thirst. Loath to tolerate it, you swallowed poison, your final potation. You never did anything more like a gourmet, Apicius.

23

You hand all the victuals to the slaves behind you. Why is your table not placed at your feet? 32
