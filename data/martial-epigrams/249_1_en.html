Though he has some three hundred tables, Annius has pages instead of tables. The platters speed across and the dishes fly. You are welcome to such banquets, my fine gentlemen. A walking dinner puts me off.

I send you the little gifts of a suburban 74 garden: eggs for your throat, 75 Severus, fruit for your palate.
