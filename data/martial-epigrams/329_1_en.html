Epigrams Book IX

52

If you believe me, Quintus Ovidius, I love your birthday Kalends of April 87 as much as my own of March—you deserve it. Happy both days, days to be marked by me with superior pebbles. 88 The one gave me life, but the other a friend. Quintus, your Kalends give me more.

53

I wished to give you a trifling birthday present, Quintus. You forbid it. You are an imperious fellow; I must obey your admonition. Let it be as both of us will wish, as gives both of us pleasure: you give me something, Quintus.
