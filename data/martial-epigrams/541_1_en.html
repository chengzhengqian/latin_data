Epigrams Book XIV

128. Hooded overcoat

Gaul clothes you in a Santonian hooded overcoat. 113 Recently it was the coat of long-tailed monkeys. 114

129. Red Canusian cloak

Rome wears more browns, Gaul reds; and boys and soldiers like this color.

130. Leather overcoat

Though you start your journey with ever so clear a sky, never be without a leather coat 115 against sudden showers.

  • 114Alluding, it is supposed, to a recent show.
