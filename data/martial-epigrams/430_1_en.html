72

Natta devours the willy of his young athlete, compared to whom Priapus is a eunuch.

73

When I ask you, Lygdus, you always swear that you’ll come to me and you appoint a time and appoint a place. But when I have lain taut with protracted excitement in vain, often my left hand comes to my rescue in your stead. What should I wish upon you, false lad, for such deserts and such morals? Lygdus, may you carry a one-eyed mistress’ sunshade.
