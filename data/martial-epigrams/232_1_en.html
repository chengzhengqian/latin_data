Eros is sodomized, Linus sucks. What’s it to you, Olus, what either one of them does with his own skin? 14 Matho fornicates for a hundred thousand. What’s it to you, Olus? You won’t be a pauper on that account, Matho will. Sertorius dines till daybreak. What’s it to you, Olus, when you can snore all night? Lupus owes Titus seven hundred thousand. What’s it to you, Olus? Don’t give or lend Lupus a penny. What does have to do with you, Olus, what ought rather to be your concern, you pretend not to know.
