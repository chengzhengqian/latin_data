42

If you don’t bathe in the warm baths of Etruscus, you will die unbathed, Oppianus. No waters will so charm you, not the springs of Aponus by women untried, 45 nor soft Sinuessa and the hot waves of Passer 46 or proud Anxur, nor the shallows of Phoebus or Baiae the paramount. Nowhere is the clear, open sky so bright. The very light stays longer there, from no place does the day depart more slowly. There the quarries of Taygetus 47 are green and stones which the Phrygian and the Libyan 48 have deeply hewn contend in varied beauty. Sleek alabaster breathes arid heat and snakestones are warm with slender flame.
