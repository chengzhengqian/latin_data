But the dialecticians’ vocabulary is none of it the popular language, they use words of their own; and indeed this is a feature shared by almost all the sciences: either new names have to be coined for new things, or names taken from other things have to be used metaphorically. Well then, those qualitiesa are of two sorts, primary and derivative.
