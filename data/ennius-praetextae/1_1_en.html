PRAETEXTAE

PRAETEXTAE (F 1–5)

main types: plays on contemporary history and plays on the foundation stories of Rome.

Bibl.: Suerbaum 1994, 361; 2002, 129; 2003, 222; Manuwald 2001 [2004], 75–78, 148–50. Lit.: Manuwald 2001; Kragelund 2002; Boyle 2006, 83–87.

AMBRACIA (F 1–4)

games vowed to Iuppiter Optimus Maximus at Ambracia and celebrated in 186 BC (Liv. 39.5.7–10, 39.22.1–2).

Bibl.: Suerbaum 1994, 361; 2002, 129; 2003, 222; Manuwald 2001 [2004], 149. Lit.: Suerbaum 1994, 361; 2002, 129; Manuwald 2001, 162–72; Boyle 2006, 83–85.
