PRAETEXTAE

​

2 Nonius

veget [“enlivens”: archaic word], instead of “invigorates” or “causes to rise” or “it is vigorous.” . . . Ennius in Ambracia:

and you [Neptune?] enliven the salty seas with immense winds

​

3 Nonius

cunctant [“they hesitate”: active], instead of cunctantur [deponent form]. . . . Ennius in Ambracia:

you advise well: he hesitates: o, see the brave man!

​

4 Nonius

populat [“plunders”]: there is also a passive [i.e., deponent form] populatur. . . . Ennius in Ambracia:

bold slaves lay waste the fields of their masters at home
