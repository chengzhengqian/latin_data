AMBRACIA (F 1–4)

Marcus Fulvius Nobilior waged war against the Aetolians in 189 BC and captured the city of Ambracia (Liv. 38.3.9–11.9; Polyb. 21.25–30). Ennius accompanied him on that campaign and described his achievement in literary works, presumably in Annales 15 and in this praetexta (cf. T 10, 29, 88, 92, 95, 96).
