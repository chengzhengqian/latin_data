SABINAE (F 5)

The title suggests a play representing the famous abduction of the Sabine women after the foundation of Rome (e.g., Liv. 1.9–13; Ov. Fast. 3.187–228; Plut. Rom. 14–19; Dion. Hal. Ant. Rom. 2.30–46). The surviving fragment may represent the women intervening in the fight between their fathers and husbands.
