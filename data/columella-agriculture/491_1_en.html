So the subject which has still to be dealt with is horticulture, which the husbandman of old carried out in a half-hearted and negligent fashion but which is now quite a popular pursuit. Though, indeed, among the ancients there was a stricter parsimony, the poor had a more generous diet, since highest and lowest alike sustained life on an abundance of milk and the flesh of wild and domestic animals as though on water and corn.
