Even those, however, which have no defects will thrive somewhat better if they are wetted with unsalted lees of oil. Some people have pronounced30 this to be the best time for establishing seed-nurseries, and have advised this time for the sowing broadcast of the berries of laurel and myrtle and the other evergreens in beds. The same persons have expressed the opinion that upright and other kinds of ivy ought to be planted from February 13th onwards and even as late as March 1st.

On March 15th the Scorpion begins to set; it signifies a storm. On March 16th the Scorpion sets: the31 weather is wintry. On March 17th the Sun passes into Aries (the Ram): the wind is North or Northwest. On March 21st the Horse (Pegasus) sets in the early morning: the winds are northerly. On March 23rd Aries begins to rise: it is a rainy day and occasionally it snows. On March 24th and 25th the spring equinox is a sign of storm.
