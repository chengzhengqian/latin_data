Quom1 inferior omni via2 grassaretur,

Macrob. Exc. Bob., GL 5.652.3; Macrob. Exc. Paris. GL 5.626.22: to illustrate grassor, the frequentative of gradior.

54* inc. 22M, inc. 58Mc, inc. 103D, inc. 72K

inbecilla est fortitudo dum pendet

Schol. Stat. ad Theb. 5.384 (also quoting Luc. 3.692–93): to illustrate how the force of blows is lessened when dealt from a position of instability caused by rolling seas.
