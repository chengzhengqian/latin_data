SALLUST

SHIPS AND SAILING, FRR. 26–31

26* 3.64M, inc. 23Mc, 3.42M, 3.52K

crebritate fluctuum, ut aquilone solet

Serv. & Serv. auct. ad Aen. 1.116: to illustrate Servius’ remark that Aquilo (the North Wind) produces constant waves.

27* inc. 13M, inc. 49Mc, inc. 57D, inc. 23K

inpediebant iussa nautarum

Serv. & Serv. auct. ad Aen. 3.129: to illustrate celeuma (= the boatswain’s call giving the time to the rowers), a Greek term glossed as praeceptum (a command).

28* 3.55M, inc. 21Mc, 4.23D, 4.38K

triplici fluctu

Serv. auct. ad Aen. 1.116: cited as a Latin expression modeled on the Greek τρικυμία (= a huge wave).
