When the senate was moved by the tearful pleas of the publicani180 to order that these contracts be revoked and new ones issued, the censors issued an edict that removed from the bidding those who had pooh-poohed their original contracts and then they let out all the same contracts at only slightly reduced figures. It was a remarkable censorship, full of controversies that hounded Marcus Porcius throughout his life since it was to him that its stringent character was attributed.

That same year two colonies were established: Potentia in the territory of Picenum, and Pisaurum in Gallic territory.181 Colonists were each granted six acres. The land was distributed and the colonies established by the same triumvirs, Quintus Fabius Labeo, Marcus Fulvius Flaccus and Quintus Fulvius Nobilior. The consuls of that year achieved nothing of note in either the domestic or the military sphere.

45. The consuls presided over the election of Marcus Claudius Marcellus and Quintus Fabius Labeo182 as consuls for the following year.
