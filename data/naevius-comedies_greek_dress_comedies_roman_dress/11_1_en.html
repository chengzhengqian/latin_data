A Masked Play b

Festus

: There is a certain play of Naevius entitled A Masked Play which, so people think, was the first acted by masked players. But since it was in fact many years after this when comic and tragic actors began to use masks, it is more likely that, owing to a scarcity of comic actors, that play, when newly produced, was acted by players of Atellan farces, who are properly called masked, because they have the right of not being forced c to take off their masks and lay them on the stage, while all other players are strictly required to submit to this.
