38 Varro, On the Latin Language

cornua [horns] is said from curvor [curvature] because most horns are curva [curved]. [26]

learn that we, whom they call Muses, are the Camenae1

Casmenae is the early name as it originated and is written elsewhere. Carmenae is formed from the same origin. In many words, what the ancients pronounced as s they later pronounce as r.
