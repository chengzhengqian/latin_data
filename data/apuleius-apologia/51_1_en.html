He is the one, however, whom according to you I prostrated with my spells, just because he once happened to fall over in my presence. 2Most of his fellow slaves are here, since you demanded that I produce them. All of them can say what it is about Thallus they detest, why none of them dares to eat with him from the same dish or drink from the same cup. 3But why mention slaves? You can see for yourselves.
