LXXXV. Holochrysos taken in wine cures strangury,use of holochrysos. and applied as linimentd fluxes from the eyes; with burnt lees of wine and pearl barley it removes lichens. The root of chrysocome is warming and astringent. It is given in drink for complaints of liver and lungs, while a decoction in hydromel is prescribed for pains in the womb. It promotes menstruation, and if given raw reducese the water of dropsy.
