Some apply to patches of mange Spanish fly pounded with liquid pitch, first preparing the skin with soda—the application is caustic, and care must be taken not to cause deep sores—and prescribe that afterwards to the sores so formed be applied the heads, gall, and dung of mice with hellebore and pepper.

XXXV. Nits are removed by dog fat, snakes takenCures for nits. in food like eels, or by the cast slough of snakes taken in drink; dandruff by sheeps’ gall with Cimolian chalk rubbed on the head until it dries off.

XXXVI.
