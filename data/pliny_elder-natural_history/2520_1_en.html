Pangonus. is composed of almost every colour. ‘Pangonus,’ or ‘all-angles,’ is no longer than a finger, and it is only its more numerous plane faces that prevent it from being taken for rock-crystal. As for the ‘paneros,’Paneros. or ‘all-love,’ Metrodorus does not describe it, but he§ 35. cites quite a tasteful poem on the stone composed by Queen Timaris and dedicated to Venus.
