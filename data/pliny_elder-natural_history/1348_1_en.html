Basil, sorrel, spinach, cress, rocket,Varieties, etc., of kitchen-garden plants. orage, coriander and dill are plants of which there is only one kind, as they are the same in every locality and no better in one place than another. It is a common belief that rue which you have stolen grows better, just as stolen bees are believed to do very badly. Wild mint, cat-mint, endive and pennyroyal spring up even without being sown. On the other hand plants which we have mentioned and are going to mention have several varieties, and particularly parsley.
