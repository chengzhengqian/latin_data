In so many places and by so many fires does nature burn the countries of the earth.

Moreover, as this one element has a fertileMarvels of fire. principle that engenders itself and grows out of the smallest sparks, what must be expected to happen in future among all these funeral pyres of the earth? What is the natural principle that pastures a most voracious appetite on the whole world while itself unimpaired? Add thereto the innumerable stars and the mighty sun, add the fires of man’s making and also those implanted in the nature of stone and of timber rubbing against itself, and again the fire of clouds, and the sources of thunderbolts—and doubtless all marvels will be surpassed by the fact that there has ever been a single day on which there has not been a universal conflagration, when also hollow mirrorsb facing the sun’s rays set things alight more easily than any other fire. What of the countless small but natural eruptions of fire?
