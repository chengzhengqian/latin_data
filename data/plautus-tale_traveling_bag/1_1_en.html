STAGING

The stage represents a street in a coastal town. On it are the houses of Gorgines, to the left, and of Dinia, to the right. Between them there is a myrtle grove with a temple of Venus, which may be hidden behind the bushes. On the right, the street leads into the town center, and on the left, to the countryside and the coast.
