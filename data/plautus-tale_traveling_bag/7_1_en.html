Exeunt gorgines and aspasius into their house.

cac (going toward the left exit) Why the deuce should I look for a patron after losing the dispute? Really, I was a wretched, cursed, and unlucky fellow a while ago, since I didn’t look around myself a hundred times when I saw it. As for that crook, there’s a place among the myrtle65 trees, from there he ambushed me. I know it as surely as I know that I’m standing here: I’ve lost the booty I caught, unless I prepare some countercounsel similar to myself.6 I’ll stand here and watch if I can see any friend. (steps aside)
