Pindus, Othrys, and Pholoe fell from the grasp of the giants; down at last fell ponderous Ossa from Rhoetus’ hand; Aegeon was laid low, and Briareus and Ephialtes and Mimas, who were wont to lick the northern Wain with their feet. 1 Enceladus fell by thy father’s hand, Typhoeus by thy brother’s; and now the one supports an Euboean 2 mountain, the other a Sicilian.”

Then Orpheus changed his theme, making his mother the whole burden of his song, and teaching the strings to hymn Calliope. The Muses rose in homage at this praise of their sister, and the goddess was gladdened even more by a son’s devotion than by his song. But if it is well pleasing to sing a mother’s praises, and if I lack the power to match the ancient lyre, yet in Avitus, to whom I have vowed my song, we have here the father of his people, and though my muse be weaker, my theme is greater.
