FRL II: ENNIUS

PROTREPTICUS (F 1)

The titles Protrepticus and Praecepta, both attested for Ennius, are often thought to refer to the same work, but with only one mention and one fragment for each that is impossible to confirm.

Protrepticus is the title of a work by the late Roman poet Ausonius and also appears in Greek literature, for instance
