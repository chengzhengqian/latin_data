FRL II: ENNIUS

EPIGRAMMATA (F 1–3)

Ennius is said to have introduced to Rome not only the hexameter, but also the elegiac distich (T 109). The preserved couplets allude to the death and lasting achievements of either Ennius himself or Scipio. They are all believed to be separate epigrams, a genre for which the Saturnian meter had previously been used. They may have been influenced by (near-)contemporary Greek epigrammatic poetry.
