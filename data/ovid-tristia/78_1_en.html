Tristia, III

Scythian tongues chatter on almost every side, and I think I could write in Getic measure.1 O believe me, I fear that Sintic and Pontic language may be mingled with the Latin in my writings. Such as my book is, then, deem it worthy of indulgence and pardon it because of the circumstances of my fate.

  • 1See Ex P. iv. 13. 19 ff.
