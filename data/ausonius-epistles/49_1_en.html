He now has sailed right up to your villa Hebromagus and made it the depôt for his goods, that thence by barge grain may be carried down for my service, as he avers. This guest, then, lest you be burdened, speed on his way in a few days, that, transported forthwith by the help of your vessel as far as the township’s harbour, he may deliver Lucaniacus from famine by now, by now Perusian, by now Saguntine.2

If I receive this boon I ask of you, you shall be worshipped above Ceres: old Triptolemus or, as some call him, Epimenides, or Buzyges,3 the bailiff’s patron, will I arrange to make inferior to your god-head, for this corn will become your gift.
