Careful students of the old writers will, I know, find still more variation; but I fear that even this is too much.

Quintilian’s own views

I have to admit that I now hold a somewhat different opinion from that which I held in the past. If I were to think only of my reputation, it might be safest to change nothing of the views which I both held and indeed approved for many years; but I cannot bear to feel that I have kept anything back, especially in a work which I am writing to be of some service to our worthy students.
