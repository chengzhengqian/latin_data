Irony as a Figure

I have found authority for calling eironeia “Dissimulation”;76 but as that word is an inadequate description of the power of this Figure as a whole, I shall, as usual, be content with the Greek name. The Figure Irony, then, is not very different in its generic character from the Trope Irony.77 In both, we are asked to understand the opposite of what is said. But a more careful look easily shows that they are different species.
