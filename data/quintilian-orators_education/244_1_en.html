Deliberative oratory

I am surprised that Deliberative speeches also have been thought by some to be concerned with only one question, namely that of expediency. And this principle is perfectly sound, if we are fortunate enough always to be addressing a council of the good and wise. With the inexperienced however (to whom one often has to give advice) and especially with the people, which contains an uneducated majority, we have to keep the two things separate and conform more to ordinary understandings.
