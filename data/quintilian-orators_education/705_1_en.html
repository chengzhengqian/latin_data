“Being at a loss”

Hesitation28 too gives a certain guarantee of sincerity: we pretend to be asking ourselves where to begin and where to stop, what it is best to say, or whether to speak at all. All the texts are full of examples of this sort. One is enough for the moment: “For my part, I do not know which way to turn. Shall I deny that there was a scandal about bribery of the court?” and so on.29 This device works also for the past, because we can pretend that we did once hesitate.

Communication and related Figures

The Figure called Communication30 is not very different from this.
