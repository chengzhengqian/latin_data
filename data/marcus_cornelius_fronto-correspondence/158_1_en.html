Marcus Antoninus to Fronto

To my master. 162 a.d.

In what holiday-wise we have kept our holiday at Alsium1 I will not put on paper, that you may not be yourself troubled and scold me, my master. On my return to Lorium2 I found my little lady3 slightly feverish. The doctor says, if we soon ............ If you were well, I should be happier. For I hope to see you already enjoying the use of sound eyes .... Farewell, my master.
