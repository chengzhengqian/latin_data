Fronto to Marcus Aurelius as Caesar

Hail, my Lord. ? 140–143 a.d.

After I had already closed and sealed the preceding letter, it occurred to me that those who plead in this case—and many seem likely to plead in it—may speak against Herodes in less measured terms. Take care how you think that I alone am concerned in this affair. Farewell, my Lord, and live, that I may be happy. Capreolus, who is now away, and our friend Marcianus2 seem likely to plead; Villianus too, it seems.
