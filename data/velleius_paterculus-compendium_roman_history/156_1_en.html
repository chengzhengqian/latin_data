At last he was prevailed upon rather by reason than by the honour, since he saw that whatever he did not undertake to protect was likely to perish. He is the only man to whose lot it has fallen to refuse the principate for a longer time, almost, than others had fought to secure it.

After heaven had claimed his father, and human honours had been paid to his body as divine honours were paid to his soul,a the first of his tasks as emperor was the regulation of the comitia, instructions for which Augustus had left in his own handwriting. On this occasion it was my lot and that of my brother, as Caesar’s candidates,b to be named for the praetorship immediately after those of noble families and those who had held the priesthoods, and indeed to have had the distinction of being the last to be recommended by Augustus and the first to be named by Tiberius Caesar.
