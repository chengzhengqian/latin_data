Ptolemy because of his age was at that time still under the control of others; his guardians were both preparing war against Antiochus, in order to clear their title to Hollow Syria, and were promising the Romans everything for the war with Macedonia. Masinissa was helping the Romans with grain and was preparing to send to the war troops with elephants under command of his son Misagenes.
