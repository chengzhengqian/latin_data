His words were ended. He renews the banquet and brings back harmony, and at last sends starry night down from Olympus. Then the choir of Muses and Apollo, striker of the lyre, whose wont it is to tell of the Phlegraean fight, appear, and the Phrygian henchman1 bears round the heavy bowl. They rise when slumber calls, and turn themselves each to his own dwelling.
