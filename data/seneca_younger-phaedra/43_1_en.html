What land will show its new face to the stars? Is some Cycladic island rising? While we ask such questions in amazement, behold the whole sea bellows, and all the surrounding cliffs roar back; swelling with a monster, the flood rushes toward the land. Its crest showers a fountain of brine, it foams and spews out water at intervals, just as a whale, journeying through Ocean’s deeps, pours out of its mouth the stream it has swallowed. The ball of water shakes, trembles, breaks open, and brings to shore an evil that outdoes fear; the sea rushes onto the land, chasing its own monster.

My lips tremble with fear. What an impression was given by that vast body! A bull, carrying high its dark-blue neck, with a tall crest rising above its greenish brow; ears erect and shaggy; eyes of varying color, both such as the overlord of a wild herd might have, and such as a seaborn creature: in part the eyes spew flame, in part they gleam with a striking sea-blue quality.
