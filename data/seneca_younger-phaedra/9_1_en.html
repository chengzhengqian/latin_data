Those who grow too extravagant through prosperity, overflowing with luxury, are always seeking out the unusual. Then lust steals in, that dire companion of great good fortune. Ordinary meals are not satisfactory, nor fabrics of a sane style nor cheap drinking cups. Why does this infection choose pampered homes, and steal less often into humble families? Why is it that chaste love dwells beneath lowly roofs, that average folk have sane affections and modest status is self-controlled, while the rich and those bolstered by royal status seek more than what is right? Excessive power wants power beyond its power. Think what befits a woman endowed with a high throne: fear and respect the scepter of your returning husband.
