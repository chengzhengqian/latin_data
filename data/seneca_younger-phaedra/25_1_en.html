Some crimes are made honorable by success. Come, begin, my spirit! (to Hippolytus) Lend me your ears awhile in private, I pray you. Any companion with you should leave.

hippolytus

See, the place is clear and free of any observation.

phaedra

Yet my lips refuse passage to the words I begin. A great force impels my speech, a greater restrains it. I call all you gods to witness that this thing I want—I do not want.

hippolytus

Your spirit desires to utter something but cannot?

phaedra

Light cares can speak, huge cares are dumfounded.26

hippolytus

Entrust your cares to my ears, mother.

phaedra
