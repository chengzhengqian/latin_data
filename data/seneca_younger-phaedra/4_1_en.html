If a favored worshipper takes your power into the forests, his nets hold the beasts fast-bound, no creature’s feet break the snares; the prey is brought in on a groaning wagon; then the hounds return with muzzles reddened by plentiful blood, and the rustic throng head for their cottages in a long triumphal procession.

Ah! Favor me, goddess: the clear-voiced hounds have sent the sign; I am called to the woods. I shall go this way, where a path shortens the lengthy journey.
