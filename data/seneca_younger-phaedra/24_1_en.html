Often Love puts a harness even on the stubborn, and changes their hatred. Look at your mother’s kingdom: fierce as they are, those women feel Venus’ yoke. You are testimony to that, as the sole boy of the race.25

hippolytus

I think of it as the one consolation for my mother’s loss, that now I am allowed to hate all women.

nurse

(aside) Like a hard crag, intractable on every side, that resists the seas and flings the attacking waves far back, so he rebuffs my words. But Phaedra approaches impetuously, impatient with delay. What will befall? What will be the upshot of her madness? Suddenly her body falls to the ground in a faint, and a deathlike pallor has spread over her face. (to Phaedra) Raise your head, find your voice again. See, child, your own Hippolytus is holding you.

phaedra

Who restores me to pain, and restarts these powerful tides in my mind? How good it was to escape from myself!

hippolytus

Why do you shun the sweet gift of light returned?
