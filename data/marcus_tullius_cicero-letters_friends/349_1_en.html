I talk about whatever comes uppermost, as they say, and transform sighs into shouts of laughter. Do you manage matters better, actually making mock of a philosopher? When he put his question whether anybody had anything to ask, you called out ‘Who’s going to ask me to dinner? Been wondering all day.’6 The poor dunderhead7 thought you would be enquiring whether there is one sky or an infinite number. What business is that of yours? But, confound it, is a dinner any business of yours, especially one in this house?8

Well, so life passes. Every day a bit of reading, or a bit of writing. Then, since something is due to my friends, I dine with them. We don’t go beyond the law, if there is such a thing nowadays, we even stop short of it, and that by a considerable margin. So you don’t have to dread my arrival. You’ll receive a guest with a small appetite for food, but a large one for frolic.
