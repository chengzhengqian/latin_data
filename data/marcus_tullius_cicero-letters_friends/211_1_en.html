106 (XV.14) CICERO TO CASSIUS

Cilicia, latter October (?) 51

From M. Cicero, Imperator, to C. Cassius, Proquaestor,1 greetings.

Your recommendation gives me a friend in the person of M. Fabius; but I am none the richer on his account. These many years past he has been mine absolutely, highly regarded by me for his good nature and attentive courtesy. However, I have become much the more his friend for having perceived what an exceptional regard he has for you.
