So I hope you will give him the most generous of welcomes, and do your best to ensure that his activity in a mission which he undertook contrary to his own convenience in deference to my wishes may shine as conspicuously as possible. He is desirous (the natural instinct of a man of mould) to gain all the commendation he can from me, his instigator, and from the municipality. In that he will succeed, if through this recommendation of mine he enlists your good will.
