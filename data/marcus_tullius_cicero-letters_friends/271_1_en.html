142 (XIII.48) CICERO TO C. SEXTILIUS RUFUS

Italy, beginning of 49 (?)

From Cicero to C. Sextilius Rufus, Quaestor, greetings.

I recommend all the inhabitants of Cyprus to your favour, but more especially those of Paphos. Anything you do for them I shall greatly appreciate. I am all the more ready to recommend them to you because I think it is to the advantage of your own reputation (which I have much at heart) that on your first arrival in the island as Quaestor you should set a pattern for others to follow. This I think you will the more easily achieve if you see fit to follow the enactment of your friend P. Lentulus and my own ordinances. I am confident that this will tend much to your credit.
