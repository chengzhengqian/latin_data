165 (XIV.21) CICERO TO TERENTIA

Brundisium, ca. 3 June 47

From Tullius to his dear Terentia greetings.

I hope you are well, as I am.

Do your best to regain your health. Please make all necessary provisions and arrangements as circumstances require and write to me as often as possible on all points.

Good-bye.
