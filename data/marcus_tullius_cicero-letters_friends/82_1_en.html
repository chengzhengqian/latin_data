I must not urge you at greater length. I will only point out that if you let slip this opportunity of securing the friendship of a most eminent and generous personage in a wealthy province at just the right time of your life, you will never find a better. In this opinion, as you jurists say in your books, Q. Cornelius concurs.

I am glad that you have not gone to Britain. You are quit of the fatigues, and I shall not be listening to your accounts of it all! Please send me full details as to where you will be spending the winter, in what expectations or under what conditions.
