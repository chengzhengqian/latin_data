Let us have a rule to assign just penalties to offences, lest you flay with the terrible scourge what calls for the strap. Fora as to your striking with the rod one who deserves sterner measures, I am not afraid of that, when you say that theft is on a par with highway robbery, and when you threaten to prune away all crimes, great and small, with the same hook, if men would but give you royal power.

124If the wise man is rich,b and a good cobbler, and alone handsome and a king, why crave what you already have?c “You do not know,” he answers, “what our father Chrysippusd means.
