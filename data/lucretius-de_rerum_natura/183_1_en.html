One lover will actually deride another, and bid him propitiate Venus as being the victim of a discreditable love, and often, poor wretch, casts not a glance at his own surpassing misery. The black girl is a nut-brown maid,a the dirty and rank is a sweet disorder, the green-eyed is a little Pallas,b the stringy and wooden is a gazelle, the squat little dwarf is one of the Graces, a pinch of embodied wit; the huge virago is a “stunner,” and full of dignity; if she stutters and cannot speak, elle zezayec; the dumb is modest; the fiery, spiteful chatterbox is a little squib; when she is too skinny to live, she is his maigrelette, his cherie; she is svelte when she is half dead of consumption. A swollen thing with large breasts is Ceres herself after the birth of lacchus, the pug-nosed is Silena or Madame Satyr, the thick-lipped is “all kiss.” It would be a long task if I were to try to go through all the list.
