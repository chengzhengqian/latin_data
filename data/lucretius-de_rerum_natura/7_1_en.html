Nor do I fail to understand that it is difficultMy task is difficult, to make clear the dark discoveries of the Greeks in Latin verses, especially since we have often to employ new words because of the poverty of the language and the novelty of the mattersb; but still it is your merit, and the expected delight of yourbut the hope of gaining your friendship inspires me to tackle it. pleasant friendship,c that persuades me to undergo any labour,d and entices me to spend the tranquil nights in wakefulness, seeking by what words and what poetry at last I may be able to display clear lights before your mind, whereby you may see into the heart of things hidden.
