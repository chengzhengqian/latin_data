An image may also be transmitted from mirrorImages may be reflected from mirror to mirror, changing sides each time. to mirror, so that as many as five or six images have often been produced. For whatever lies hidden behind in the inner parts of a house, however tortuous and secluded be the ways in between, may yet be all brought out through these involved passages by means of a number of mirrors and seen to be in the house. So truly does the image shine across from mirror to mirror; and when it has been presented left, it becomes right again, then once more it comes back again and returns to the same position.
