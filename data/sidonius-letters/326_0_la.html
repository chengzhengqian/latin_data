The Letters of Sidonius

XVI Anderson was puzzled by certain aspects of this letter. Its import is as follows: Constantius was the earlier inspirer of the publication of Sidonius’ letters Books I–VII (Ep. I .1. VII. 18), though Petronius was the instigator of Book VIII just as Firminus was of Book IX (Ep. IX. 1.) Constantius had also recently encouraged Sidonius to publish an addendum (Ep. VIII. 1).
