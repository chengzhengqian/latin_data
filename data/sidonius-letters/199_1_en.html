XX Sidonius To His Friend Pastor,* Greeting

1. Your failure to be present at yesterday’s debate in the city council1 was considered by the better sort to be intentional. They suspected that you wished to avoid having the burden of the coming embassy placed upon your shoulders. I congratulate you on living your life in such a spirit that you are constrained to fear your own election; I praise your efficiency, I look up to your wisdom, I pay honour to your good fortune; indeed, I pray that the like gifts may be possessed by all for whom I have a like affection. 2.
