If this favour is paid in keeping with the excellent services he has rendered it will soon redound to your own profit.2 I am pleading now only in respect of the mark of favour: but you must consider what he deserves—this man of yours who undoubtedly seeks the affection of his master3 more than any reward. Farewell.
