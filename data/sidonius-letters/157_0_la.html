And so in this letter (which I grant to be elaborated and worked up for publication) he wishes to point the difference between the attitude of Maximus, in regard to a normal business transaction, before and after his ordination as priest. Before, he said “business is business.” After, he adjusted his whole behaviour to a rule of Christian discipline and charity, so that when he once realised the plight of Turpio and his family he at once remitted the interest and only asked for the return of the loan.
