Hideous and frightful are their shapes, threatening their carriage. Vaunting Ambition is puffed up, Learning is proud, Eloquence thunders, Deceit contrives snares in secret. Here Abusive Speech snarls throughout the courts, there paltry Philosophy wields the cluba of Hercules and displays her naked Sagesb through the streets, while Idolatry coats smoke-grimed stones with waxc and in pale fear falls prostrate before altars that cannot hear. Alas, with what armed forces does the ruthless enemy press upon the race of men, with what attendant trains under his command does he wage his iron wars, with what dominion triumph over the conquered! The Canaanite rises up to his aid with close-set columns and daunting helm, shaking the weight of bristly beard on his chin and waving the hand that grasps his heavy spear. On another side in burning rage stands the army of the king of the Amorites, and the Girgashites in their thousands pour out in array and come flying over the field. Some smite from a distance, others join in close combat.
