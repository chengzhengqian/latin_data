And let it be that the grim fire swallow me in the chasm of Avernus because for my bodily stain it must needs be so; yet at least may the flames be gentle and the heat of their breath be mild, may their fury die down and their burning moderate. Let others enjoy the glory of infinite light and crown-encircled brows: as for me, may my punishment be light, my torment merciful.
