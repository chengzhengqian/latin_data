Book VIII

No ordinary honour was paid to Berenice too, the only woman allowed to be present at a gymnastic spectacle. That was when she brought her son Eucles to the Olympics to enter the competition.25 She was the daughter of an Olympic champion, and she came flanked by her brothers who had won the same palm.
