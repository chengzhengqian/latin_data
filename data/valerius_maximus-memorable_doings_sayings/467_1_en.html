15. Of Persons Born in the Lowest Station who Tried by Falsehood to Thrust Themselves into Illustrious Families

But the above was tolerable temerity and dangerous only to a single individual. The type of impudence now to follow is altogether beyond bearing and fraught with extensive danger publicly as well as privately.
