3

Repudiation of wife:

Cicero: He has given orders for that actress of his to ‘mind her own affairs,’b and, under the statutes of the Twelve Tables, he has taken away her keysc and turned her out. What a worthy and excellent citizen in men’s eyes thereafter! The most respectable thing he did in all his life was to divorce an actress!d

4

Children born in adultery:

Gellius: The Board of Ten recordede that the birth of a human being be held to occur within ten months, not in the eleventh.

Ulpian: By the Law of the Twelve Tables a child who has been in the womb is admitted into a legal inheritance if he has been born . . .f A child born after ten months since the father’s death will not be admitted into a legal inheritance.
