Concordances For Lucilius

In the following concordances, M stands for Marx’s edition of Lucilius, W for this. By catal. is meant the list of words and phrases which is to be found on pages 418-423 of this book. Useful though Terzaghi’s edition is, neither his nor Bolisani’s is meant to supersede Marx’s. Terzaghi provides a handy text and commentary; Bolisani writes for Italians mainly. Hence no concordance between this work and theirs has been thought necessary.
