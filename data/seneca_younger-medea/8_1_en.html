MEDEA

medea

My father was a king.

nurse

You do not fear arms?

medea

Not even if sprung from the earth.15

nurse

You will die.

medea

I desire it.

nurse

Escape!

medea

I regret escaping.

nurse

Medea—

medea

I shall become her.

nurse

You are a mother.

medea

You see by whom.

nurse

You hesitate to escape?

  • 15Like the armed soldiers that sprang from the dragon’s teeth sown by Jason in Colchis.
