The covenants of this well-separated world were dragged together by Thessaly’s pine, which bade the deep suffer lashes,20 and bade the sea, once alien, become part of our fears. That wanton boat paid heavily, passing through such lengthy terrors, when the two sea stacks, barriers of the deep, their facing <cliffs driven together>21 by a sudden force, groaned with a sound like that from heaven, and the trapped sea sprinkled the stars and the very clouds. Daring Tiphys grew pale, and dropped all the guide ropes from his unnerved hand; Orpheus fell silent, his lyre dumbfounded, and the Argo herself lost her voice. That time when the maid of Sicilian Pelorus, her womb girt round with rabid dogs, opened all her gaping throats at once! Who did not shudder in every limb at the multiple barking of a single monster? That time the melodious voices of those dread demons charmed the Ausonian sea! —when Thracian Orpheus
