What is the extent of the disaster?

messenger

The greedy fire rages through every part of the palace as if under orders; already the building has collapsed completely, and they fear for the city.

chorus

Water must smother the flames.

messenger

A further marvel has happened in this disaster: water feeds the flames, and the more they try to check the fire, the more it blazes; it commandeers the very defenses.
