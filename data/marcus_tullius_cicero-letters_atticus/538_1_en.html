313 (XIII.8)

Tusculum, 9 June 45

Cicero to Atticus

I really have nothing to write about, as you have just left and sent me back my tablets soon after. Please have the package sent on to Vestorius and commission someone to find out whether there is a farm belonging to Q. Staberius for sale in the district of Pompeii or Nola. I should be grateful if you would send me Brutus’ abridgement of Caelius’1 history and get Panaetius ‘On Providence’ from Philoxenus.2 I shall see you and your folk on the Ides.
