  • aCicero’s contention throughout the Brutus is that oratory is late in its origins and development. He is at pains to point out that the existence of an effective early oratory is merely an inference from episodes in Roman history, in which certain men are reported to have accomplished results by the influence and persuasion of their personality. Record of their oratory did not exist.
