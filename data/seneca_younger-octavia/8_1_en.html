octavia

He must destroy me too, or he will fall by my hand.

nurse

Nature has not given you such strength.

octavia

Pain, anger, sorrow, misery, grief will give it.

nurse

Instead you should use submissiveness to win over your unkind husband.

octavia

So he can restore the brother he wickedly stole from me?

nurse

So you yourself can be safe, and so you can one day restore your father’s failing house with children of your own.
