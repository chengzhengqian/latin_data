OCTAVIA

seneca

Praise lies in doing what one should, not what one can.

nero

The mob tramples on a supine ruler.

seneca

But overthrows a hated one.

nero

Steel is the emperor’s protection.

seneca

Loyalty a better one.

nero

It befits Caesar to be feared.

seneca

But more to be loved.

nero

They have to praise me—

seneca

Anything compelled is irksome.

nero

—and obey my orders.

seneca

Then give just commands.

nero

I shall determine what they are.

seneca

In such a way that general approval will ratify them.
