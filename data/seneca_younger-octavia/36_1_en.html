OCTAVIA

messenger

Those soldiers who are celebrating beneath the emperor’s roof must defend the palace! It is threatened by the people’s fury. The Prefects, look, are alarmed and are bringing up cohorts to protect the city. This frenzy, that started so recklessly, is not caving in to fear, but rather gaining strength.

chorus leader

What is this insane fury affecting their minds?

messenger

Partisanship for Octavia has stirred the mob up, and made them run wild, committing great outrage.

chorus leader

What are they making bold to do, what is their aim? Tell us!

messenger

They plan to give Claudia back her deified father’s home and marriage with her brother—her rightful share of the throne.

chorus leader

But now Poppaea holds these things, with pledges given on both sides.

messenger

That is why they are inflamed with an all too stubborn sense of partisanship, and driven pell-mell into mindless rage.
