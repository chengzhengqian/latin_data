Who could make a fitting lament for my troubles? What plaintive nightingale could express my sorrows? I sadly wish that fate had granted me her wings! With swift wings I could get away to escape my griefs, far from the dismal haunts of men and bestial slaughter. Alone in the empty woods, and perched on a slender bough, I could utter a stream of plaintive notes, my song of sorrow.
