OCTAVIA

octavia

The emperor’s house awaits other children.12 I am dragged down by my poor brother’s terrible doom.

nurse

The citizens’ firm support for you should strengthen your heart.

octavia

It consoles me in my troubles, but it does not lessen them.

nurse

The people’s power is great.

octavia

But the emperor’s greater.

nurse

He himself will show regard for his wife.

octavia

His mistress forbids it.

nurse

She is hated by all, you see.

octavia

But dear to her husband.

nurse

She is not yet a wife.

octavia

Soon she will be, and a mother too.

  • 12Poppaea was now pregnant by Nero.
