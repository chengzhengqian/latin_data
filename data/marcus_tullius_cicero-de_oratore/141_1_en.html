And so, Methods of handling these acquired by study. with the former sort, he need only consider the handling of his proofs, but with the latter, the discovery of them as well. And indeed those professors, after distinguishing a larger number of types of cases, suggest proofs in plenty for each type. But, even if this plan is better fitted for training the young, to the end that, directly a case is propounded, they may have authorities from which they can forthwith borrow ready-made proofs, yet it is a symptom of congenital dullness to follow up the tiny rills, but fail to discern the sources of things: and by this time it is the privilege of men of our years and experience to call up what we want from the water’s head, and to discern the springs of every stream.
