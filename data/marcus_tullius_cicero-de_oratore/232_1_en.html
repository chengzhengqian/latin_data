“Oh, as for that,” said Antony, “the amount I Memory; memoria technica: the pigeonhole method. shall have left to you will be for you to decide; if you want complete candour, what I leave to you is the whole subject, but if you want me to keep up the pretence, it is for you to consider how you may satisfy our friends here. But to return to the subject,” he continued, “I am not myself as clever as Themistoclesa was, so as to prefer the science of forgetting to that of remembering; and I am grateful to the famous Simonides of Ceos, who is said to have first invented 352the science of mnemonics.
