“If any man imagines that this power has been explained by the writers on the theory of speaking, or that I can explain it in so short a span, he is very greatly mistaken, not even perceiving the vastness of the subject, much less my own ignorance. For myself indeed, as such was your wish, I have thought fit to reveal to you the springs from which to drink, and the approaches to them, not as one seeking to be myself your guide (an endless and superfluous task), but just indicating the road, and, in the usual 204way, pointing with my finger to the fountains.”
