73 C. IULIUS CAESAR STRABO

Before the Censors (F 13)

After his aedileship, Caesar Strabo acted in a case before the censors.

​

F 13 Varro, On Agriculture

Caesar Vopiscus, an ex-aedile, when he was pleading a case before the censors, said that the plains of Rosea [extremely fertile plains near Reate] were the nursing-ground of Italy, where a rod left there would not be visible the next day because of the grass.
