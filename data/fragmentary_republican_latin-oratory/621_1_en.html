On Q. Ligarius (F 2)

Alongside Cicero (Cic. Lig.), Pansa was a supporter of Q. Ligarius, prosecuted by Q. Aelius Tubero (175 F 3–7), in the trial before C. Iulius Caesar (121) in 46 BC.
