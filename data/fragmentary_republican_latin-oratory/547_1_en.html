F 6 Nonius Marcellus

horrea [“granaries”], of neuter gender, as commonly. Of feminine gender, Calidius in the oration against Quintus Gallius: “their walls, shipyards, granaries, meeting house, and public archives lie in ruins.” According to the same principle by which he also said horreae, he said tabulariae [“archives”; fem. pl.].

On Cicero’s House (F 7)

As praetor in 57 BC, Calidius spoke on the issue of the restoration of Cicero’s house to him after his return from exile, like Cicero himself (Cic. Dom.).
