72B CN. POMPONIUS

​

T 1 Cicero, Brutus

[Cicero:] To these two [C. Aurelius Cotta (80) and P. Sulpicius Rufus (76)] no one of the same generation was added as third in rank; but Pomponius pleased me most or, I should rather say, displeased me least.

​

T 2 Cicero, Brutus

[Cicero:] ...but a vigorous and energetic performer and neither lacking abundance nor mean in his diction was Cn. Pomponius, a man whom you would absolutely venture to call an orator, fighting with lungpower, rousing the auditors, sharp, harsh, accusatory.
