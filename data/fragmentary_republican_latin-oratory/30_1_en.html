Against M’. Aquillius (F 2)

The prosecution of M’. Aquillius (cos. 129 BC) took place in 125 or 124 BC; the defendant was acquitted (Cic. Font. 38; App. B Civ. 1.22.92; Mith. 57.231) (TLRR 23).
