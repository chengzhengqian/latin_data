F 27 Cicero, On the Orator

[Antonius:] If the matter as a whole is at issue, the general idea has to be made plain by a definition, for example: “If sovereignty is the grandeur and glory of the community, it was violated by the man who delivered up to the enemy an army of the Roman People, not by him who delivered the man who had done that into the power of the Roman People.”
