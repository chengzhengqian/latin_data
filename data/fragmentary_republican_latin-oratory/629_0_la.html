Against C. Antonius Hybrida (F13–18)

As a young man, in 59 BC, Caelius prosecuted the ex-consul C. Antonius Hybrida (113), who had been consul in 63 BC with Cicero, presumably for extortion or alleged involvement in the Catilinarian Conspiracy (Cic. Cael.
