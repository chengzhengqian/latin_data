In Support of Friends (F 9)

After his return to Rome from his military tribunate (67/66 BC), Cato is said to have assisted friends in court cases.

F 9 Plutarch, Life of Cato the Younger

After his return to Rome, he [Cato] spent most of his time at home <in the company of> Athenodorus [of Cordylium, Stoic philosopher] or in the Forum assisting his friends.
