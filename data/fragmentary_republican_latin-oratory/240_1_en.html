Testimony Against M. Marcellus (F 50)

The M. Marcellus against whom Crassus gave testimony (Cic. Font. 24) may have been the legate of C. Marius in 102 BC, M. Claudius Marcellus (TLRR 87).

F 50 Valerius Maximus, Memorable Doings and Sayings

L. Crassus too, as great among the judges as Aemilius Scaurus [M. Aemilius Scaurus (43)] among the senators... when he had launched a very violent thunderbolt of testimony against the defendant M. Marcellus, he turned out to be heavy in the onslaught, flimsy in the outcome.
