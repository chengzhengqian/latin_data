Against C. Iulius Caesar Strabo (F 4A)

Like P. Sulpicius Rufus (76 F 17–18), P. Antistius, when Tribune of the People in 88 BC, spoke against C. Iulius Caesar Strabo’s (73 F 14) candidacy for the consulship.
