139 M. AEMILIUS SCAURUS FILIUS

M. Aemilius Scaurus (praet. 56 BC; RE Aemilius 141), a son of M. Aemilius Scaurus (43), administered the province of Sardinia after his praetorship. Upon his return he was prosecuted for extortion by P. Valerius Triarius (148 F 1–2); defended by six advocates, including Cicero (Cic. Scaur.) as well as himself (F 5), he was acquitted (TLRR
