To the Inhabitants of Asia (F17C)

F 17C App. B Civ. 5.4.16–5.24

Appian puts a speech into Antony’s mouth, given to the Greeks and all other peoples who inhabit Asia around Pergamon: Antony outlines that king Attalus left these Greeks to the Romans in his will and that the Romans immediately treated them in a better way than he had.
