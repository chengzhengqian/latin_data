T 11 Tacitus, Dialogue on Oratory

[Aper:] I do not wish to make an attack on Corvinus, because it was not his fault that he did not exhibit the luxuriance and the polish of our time: for we see how little the strength of his mind or of his intellectual power met the demands of his discernment.
