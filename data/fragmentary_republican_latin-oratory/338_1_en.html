T 8 Cicero, Brutus

Then Brutus said: “Why do you look for others? As regards yourself, have we not seen repeatedly what clients chose, what Hortensius himself judged? When he shared cases with you—for I was often present—the place of the concluding speech, where oratory makes the greatest impact, he always left to you.”
