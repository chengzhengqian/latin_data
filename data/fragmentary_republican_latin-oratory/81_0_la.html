30 Q. POMPEIUS

Q. Pompeius (cos. 141, censor 131 BC; RE Pompeius 12) was a homo novus (Cic. Font. 23; Mur. 16) and formed, with Q. Caecilius Metellus Macedonicus (18), the first plebeian pair of censors (18 F 4).
