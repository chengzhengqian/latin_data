25 M. AEMILIUS LEPIDUS PORCINA

​

T 1 Cicero, Brutus

[Cicero:] But, to be sure, M. Aemilius Lepidus, who was called Porcina, at about the same time as Galba [Ser. Sulpicius Galba (19)], though a little younger, was regarded as an outstanding orator, and, as appears from his speeches, was certainly a good writer. [96] In this Latin orator appears, I think, for the first time that smoothness and periodic sentence form characteristic of the Greeks, and, already, so to speak, the artistic pen.
