On Behalf of Terentius Varro (F21–22)

Hortensius defended his relative Terentius Varro, who had been a legate in Asia: upon his return, Varro was charged with extortion by Ap. Claudius Pulcher (130 F4), first before the praetor L. Furius (or Turius: see MRR II 97) in 75 BC and then before the praetor P. Cornelius Lentulus
