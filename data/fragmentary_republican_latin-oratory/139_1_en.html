48 C. SEMPRONIUS GRACCHUS

“I so conducted myself in the province that no one could truly say that I received an as or more than that by way of presents or that anyone was put to expense on my account. I spent a period of two years in the province; if any courtesan entered my house or anyone’s slave boy was seduced because of me, consider me the lowest and basest of all nations. Since I conducted myself so chastely toward their slaves, you will be able to infer from that how you should believe me having lived with your sons.” [4] And then after an interval he says: “Accordingly, Romans, when I left for Rome, I brought back empty from the province the purses that I took there full of money; others have brought home overflowing with silver the wine jars that they took there filled with wine.”
