FRL III: ORATORY, PART 1

In His Defense Concerning Extortion (F3–6)

After a military career, Rutilius went to Asia in the 90s BC and supported the local population against the abuses of tax collectors; thereupon, after his return to Rome, he was prosecuted on a charge of extortion, mainly by Apicius; he defended himself, supported by Q.
