F 6 Gellius, Attic Nights

It was this civic crown that L. Gellius, an ex-censor, proposed in the Senate that the consul Cicero [63 BC] should be awarded by the Republic, because through his efforts that most dreadful conspiracy of Catiline had been detected and punished.
