XI. Then said Pontius, “I will not receive this surrender, nor will the Samnites hold it valid. And you, Spurius Postumius, if you believe in the existence of the gods, why not either reject the whole negotiation or abide by your agreement? The Samnite People is entitled to all whom it had in its power, or to peace in place of them. But why do I appeal to you, who yield yourself a prisoner as honourably as you can? I appeal to the Roman People; if they repent them of the pledge that was given at the Caudine Forks, let them replace their legions in the defile where they were surrounded.
