Unhappy that I am, shall I really never be kept from seeing her? Shall the night never threaten me with someone’s revenge? Am I to fear nothing? Shall I heave no sighs in the midst of my slumbers? Will you do nothing to give me reason to wish you dead? What do I want with a facile husband—with a husband who is a pander? With his failing he ruins our joys. Why not seek another, whom such long-suffering pleases? If you please to have me your rival, forbid it!
