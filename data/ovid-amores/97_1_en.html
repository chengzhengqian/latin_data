O worshipful child, and thou of Amathus, mother of the worshipful child, pluck ye up from my field your golden standards! The hornèd Lyaean hath dealt me a sounding blow with weightier thyrsus; I must smite the earth with mighty steeds on a mightier course. Unwarlike elegies, congenial Muse, O fare ye well, work to live on when I am no more!

  • aThe Social War, 90–89 B.C., by which Rome was compelled to grant citizenship to the Italians. The Paeligni were leaders.
