Andromache

1–2

Servius augmented, on ‘fiscina’ in Virgil: A ‘fiscina’ is a species of utensil, namely a kind of small basket. Naevius in Andromache— b

And this, my son, I beg that you press deep Into your heart, as might the vintager Grapes into a frail.

  • b Thus the augmenter of Servius. It is possible that the play was a comedy (R., 50), and some follow the MS. reading and attribute it to Novius.
