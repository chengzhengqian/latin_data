Catalepton

V

Away8 with you, begone, you empty paint pots of rhetoricians, verbiage inflated with an un-Attic screeching! And you, too, Selius, and Tarquitius, and Varro, 9 a tribe of pedants dripping with fat, begone, you empty cymbals of our youth! Even you, Sextus Sabinus, heart of my heart, farewell! Now fare ye well, my lovely chums!

We are spreading sail for blissful havens, in quest of noble Siro’s learned lore, and will free our life from all worries. Away with you, ye Muses! Yes, away even with you, sweet Muses! For let us confess the truth—you have been sweet. Yet even so, revisit my pages, but come modestly 10 and not too often!
