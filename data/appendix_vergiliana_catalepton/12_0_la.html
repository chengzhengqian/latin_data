XV

Vate Syracosio qui dulcior, Hesiodoque maior, Homereo non minor ore fuit, illius haec quoque sunt divini elementa poetae, et rudis in vario carmine Calliope.

  • 9tum O. Skutsch: aut Ω
  • XV In the manuscripts these two elegiac couplets follow without a break on 14.12 as if part of the same poem, but are clearly a later editorial addition, designed to assert the young Virgil’s authorship of the Catalepton.
