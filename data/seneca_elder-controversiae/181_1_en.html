The Foreign Merchant2

A man with a beautiful wife went off abroad. A foreign trader moved into the woman’s neighbourhood. He three times made her propositions3 of a sexual nature, offering sums of money. She said no. The trader died, leaving her all his wealth in his will, to which he added the clause: “I found her chaste.” She took the bequest. The husband returned and accuses her of adultery4 on suspicion.
