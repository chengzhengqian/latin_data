Metellus Blinded2

A priest must be without defect.3

When the temple of Vesta was on fire, the high priest Metellus lost his sight grabbing the image of Pallas.4 His rights as priest are refused him.

For Metellus: Mother Vesta, you would perhaps have no priest now5 if you had not had Metellus then.—It is the cause of the religious rites6 that I am pleading, not Metellus’. It is more important to them not to lose Metellus as their priest than to Metellus not to lose his priesthood.—Metellus was not so great when we gave him his priesthood.7—The
