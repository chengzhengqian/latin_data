Controversiae 9

“Perhaps it was so that she could exact for the accusation the penalty she was paying for the poisoning.” He added this idea: “I judge this from my own feelings: when, crazy with anger and hatred, I had forgotten innocence, and was looking about me for any road to revenge, had the step-mother had sons of her own, I should have killed them. How often I wanted to rush at this girl herself! But she was safe from me just because she was not safe from her mother.”
