The Hero Son who Would Not Return to His Hero Father

A man disinherited his son, who did not protest. The son became a hero, and for his reward1 sought to return to his father. His father spoke against the idea. Later, the father became a hero; he seeks the return of his son; his son speaks against it.

For the father: I am the braver: after your fight, we went on fighting, after mine we were victorious.—Return;
