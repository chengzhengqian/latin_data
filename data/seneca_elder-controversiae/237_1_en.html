The Son-in-law of a Rich Enemy

A poor man had a son, and an enemy1 who was a rich man with a daughter. He set out overseas. There was a rumour that he had died. The son came to be on good terms again with the rich man, and married his daughter. The father returned and is trying to force his son to divorce his wife. He refuses, and is disinherited.
