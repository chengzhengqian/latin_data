And again, we shall avoid the excessive repetition of the same word,c as follows:

Nam cuius rationis ratio non extet, ei rationi ratio non est fidem habere admodum;d

Again, we shall not use a continuous series of words with like case endings,e as follows:

Flentes, plorantes, lacrimantes, obtestantes.f
