You have met with very great losses, private and public, and now when even greater losses seem to impend, you sit and yawn. During the day you wait for night, at night you wait for day. Every day some troublesome and unpleasant news is announced—yet even now will you temporize longer with the author of these our ills, and nourish him for the destruction of the republic; will you keep him in the commonwealth as long as you can?”

  • bThe renewal (ampliatio) of a case followed the verdict non liquet by the jury, and the president’s pronouncement amplius (cognoscendum).
