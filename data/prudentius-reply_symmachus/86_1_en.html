Else would a messenger come with the news that the river is dry and Memphis lies barren and parched with drought under the dust, while the mud of Pelusium’s marshland has ceased to sweat. Has the source which is hidden in the deep mystery of nature dried up, its spring scarce giving a meagre drop of water? Has the river turned back, not liking to wash our banks, and is it diverting its course towards the sunburnt Indians?c Does its thirsty channel engulf the water of the river in mid course?
