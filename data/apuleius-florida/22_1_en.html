Similarly, in order for my teachers to take myself on as his follower, I learned two things from the practices of the Academy: to be prompt to speak when speech is required, and also to be willing to be silent when silence is required. 27By this middle course, I think I have caused your predecessors to praise me for opportune silence as much as to commend me for timely speech.
