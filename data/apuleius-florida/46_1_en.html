5.* I have long since seen from your hints what it is that you request—that I finish what remains of my subject in Latin. When I began, you all tugged in contrary directions, and I remember promising you that neither side, neither those who wanted Greek nor those who wanted Latin, should leave without a part of my speech. 2So if you agree, my speech has perhaps spent enough time in Attic Greek: it is time to move from Greece to Latium. For I am about midway through my inquiry, and, in my opinion at least, compared to its Greek predecessor this second part will be no weaker in argument, poorer in illustrations, or inferior in eloquence.
