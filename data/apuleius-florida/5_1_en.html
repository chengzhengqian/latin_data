There he would have noticed that those who take the chair and those who take a beating are dressed in nearly identical purple robes;7 4and similarly if he had observed our public games, for there too he would have seen one man presiding and one man fighting it out; and also that togas are worn both for prayer and for mourning, and again that a cloak is used to hide corpses and to clothe philosophers.
