27–8

Priscianus: In the works of archaic writers we find ‘memor’ used in the form ‘memoris,’ masculine and feminine singular, and ‘memore’ neuter. In this we have a witness in Caper, a most learned researcher into archaic lore; for he shows that Caecilius used this form in The Heiress—

Is this true? Is Antipho found to be a slippery customer? Is he really so unremembering? Is his memory so sodden?
