Chrysippus

We can only guess vaguely possible contexts for the five fragments, which reveal neither which version Accius followed nor what model he chose. Chrysippus, the favourite son of Pelops and the nymph Danais (or Axioche), was kidnapped at the Nemean games by King Laius of Thebes (another version makes the kidnapper Theseus), and brought back by Atreus and Thyestes.
