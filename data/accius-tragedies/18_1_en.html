Macrobius, quoting Virgil: ‘Now shall I be lauded either for taking spoils of honour or for a notable death.’ Accius in The Award of the Arms—

Because it is an honourable thing I should win trophy from a warrior brave; But if I should be vanquished, to be vanquished By such an one to me is no dishonour.
