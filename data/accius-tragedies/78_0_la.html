Myrmidones

This play was probably not the same as Achilles (see p. 326). Aeschylus wrote a trilogy Μυρμιδόνες (dealing with the wrath of Achilles and ending with the death of Patroclus), Νηρείδες, Φρύγες ἢ Ἕκτορος λύτρα. Accius possibly followed the first
