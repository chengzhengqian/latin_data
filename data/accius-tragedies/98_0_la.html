Accius

Phinidae

The version used most probably by Accius was the following: Phineus, a king of Salmydessus, ceased to love his queen Cleopatra, imprisoned her, and married Dardanus’ daughter Idaea, who either blinded Phineus’ two sons (whose names vary) by Cleopatra and buried them alive, or else falsely accused them before Phineus so that he maltreated them. They were discovered thus by the Argonauts, who gave them help. They were set free by Zetes and Calais, Cleopatra’ brothers, and Phineus was dethroned; Cleopatra was also freed,
