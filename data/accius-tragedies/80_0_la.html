Neoptolemus

The plot probably ran as follows. After the death of Achilles, Neoptolemus’ mother Deidameia and his grandfather Lycomedes, who dwelt in Scyros, determined not to allow Neoptolemus to leave them. (So he was put in charge of cattle and goats on the island. But he chafed at his employment. Phoenix landed in search of him, because without him the Greeks were unable to take Troy.
