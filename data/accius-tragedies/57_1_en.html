Eurysaces

suggests the time of his expulsion from Aegina. Possibly we have not to do with Telamon, but only with his son Teucer (exiled from Salamis to Cyprus) and Eurysaces. We do not know the legends about Eurysaces, beyond his connexion with Attica, which does not seem to come within the scope of Accius’ play. Cf. R., 419 ff.
