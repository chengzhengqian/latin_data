Eurysaces

The plot of this play in unknown, only one fr. of Sophocles’ play being extant. According to the legend, Telamon killed his own step-brother Phocus, and for this misdeed Aeacus condemned him to banishment. He went to Salamis, where Cychreus bequeathed to him his kingdom.
