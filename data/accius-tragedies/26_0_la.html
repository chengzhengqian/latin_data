Athamas

Of the various tales which were told of Athamas, Accius perhaps followed that which records how Athamas and Cretheus were sons of Aeolus. Cretheus’ wife Demodice loved Phrixus, Athamas’ son, but failed to gain his love in return. In revenge she falsely told Cretheus that Phrixus had waylaid her and had demanded that she give herself to him. Cretheus in wrath tried to make Athamas punish his own son with death.
