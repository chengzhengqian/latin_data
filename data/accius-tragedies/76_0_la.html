Nonius, 111, 21: ‘Facul’ pro faciliter . . . —

Erat istuc virile, ferre advorsam fortunam facul.

Nonius, 502, 31: ‘Fervit’ pro fervet . . . —

heu cor ira fervit caecum, amentia rapior ferorque!
