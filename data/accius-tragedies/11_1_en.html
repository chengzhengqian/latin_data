Nonius: ‘Redhostit (requite), returns . . .—

Tell a me, does this man make any requital while now he may more easily cast even that same thing as a reproach?

Andromeda

daughter Andromeda?) was greater than the beauty of Nereus’ daughters, Neptune sent floods and a sea-monster to plague the land. Relief could come only if Andromeda were given to the beast to be devoured. So she was chained to a seaside rock. Perseus rescued Andromeda and married her.
