Persa

tox

But this one thing is torturing me.

sag

What is it?

tox

This is the day that decides whether my girlfriend will be free or serve as a slave forever.

sag

Then what do you want now?

tox

You can buy my eternal friendship.35

sag

How?

tox

By giving me six hundred tetradrachmas6 which I can pay for her; I’ll return it to you within the next two or three days. Go on, be generous, help me out.

sag

With what nerve dare you ask me for so much money, you40 shameless creature? If I myself were to be sold entirely, it would scarcely be possible to get in return what you’re asking me for: now you’re demanding water from a pumice stone that is dry from thirst itself.

tox

Are you treating me like this?

sag

What should I do?

tox

Do you ask? Get it on loan from someone.

sag

Do the same thing that you ask of me!

tox

I’ve looked for it, but found it nowhere.

sag

I’ll look for it, just in case anyone should give me a loan.

tox

Then I have it in sight, haven’t I?45

sag

If I had it at home, I’d promise it now; the only thing I have in my power is to do my best.

  • 6The term nummus is inherently vague (“coin”), but if we assume that tetradrachmas are meant here, we reach an appropriate price; six hundred sesterces would certainly be too little.
