  • 1Hercules served King Eurystheus for twelve years, during which he did twelve great labors. Among other things, he killed the Nemean Lion and the Lernaean Hydra (a snakelike monster) and caught the Ceryneian Hind. The killing of the Stymphalian Birds is a canonical part of the twelve labors, while the fight against Antaeus, though done by Hercules, is not one of them.
