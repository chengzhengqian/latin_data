But what kind of deceit do you fear?

thyestes

Every kind! What limit can I set on my fear? His capacity is as great as his hatred.

tantalus

What can he do to you?

thyestes

For myself I fear nothing now: you are the ones that make Atreus fearful to me.

tantalus

Despite caution, you fear being deceived?

thyestes

The time for caution is past in the midst of evil. Onward! But this one thing I affirm as your father: I am following you, not leading.
