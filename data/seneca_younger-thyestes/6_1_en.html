Enough of iniquity! Right achieved nothing— nor collective wrong. Myrtilus fell betrayed, his master’s betrayer; conveyed as disloyally as he conveyed others, he made the sea renowned through its changed name; no tale is better known on Ionian ships.10 Received with a cold-blooded sword as he ran for his father’s kiss, the little son fell at the hearth, an unripe victim; your hand, Tantalus, sectioned him to furnish the table for your guests the gods. Such food is avenged by eternal hunger, eternal thirst; for that savage feast no apter penalty could have been appointed.
