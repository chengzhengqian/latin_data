THYESTES

assistant

Then what will such bitterness use as its weapon?

atreus

Thyestes himself.

assistant

This evil goes beyond anger.

atreus

I admit it. A tumult of frenzy is shaking my breast, and churning it deep within. I am swept along, and know not where, but I am swept along. —The ground moans from its lowest depths, the sky thunders though cloudless, the house cracks throughout its structure as if shattered, and the house gods shake and avert their faces. Let it be done, let it be done, this outrage that makes you gods afraid!

assistant

What are you planning to do?

atreus

Something more, greater than the commonplace, beyond normal human limits, is swelling in my spirit and jolting my sluggish hands. What it is I do not know, but it is something mighty!15 So be it.
