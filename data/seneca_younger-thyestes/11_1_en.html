THYESTES

assistant

Let him die by the sword and spew out his hateful life.

atreus

You talk about punishment’s conclusion: I want the punishment! Slaying is for a lenient tyrant; in my kingdom death is something people beg for.

assistant

Does no affection move you?

atreus

Begone, Affection, if ever you existed at all in our house! Let the dread band of Furies come, and the Erinys of strife and Megaera brandishing her twin torches. The madness firing my heart is not big enough, I want to be filled with some greater monstrosity.

assistant

What new scheme is your rage devising?

atreus

Nothing conforming to the limits of ordinary bitterness. I shall leave no deed undone—and none is enough.

assistant

The sword?

atreus

Insufficient.

assistant

What about fire?

atreus

Still insufficient.
