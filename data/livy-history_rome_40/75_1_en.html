That same summer, these authors tell us, Lucius Postumius also fought two magnificent battles with the Vaccaei in Farther Spain and killed some 35,000 of the enemy and stormed their camp. 51.181 The censors faithfully cooperated with each other in revising membership of the senate. The man chosen princeps senatus182 was Marcus Aemilius Lepidus himself, the censor and pontifex maximus; three men were expelled from the House; and Lepidus kept a number on the list that had been omitted by his colleague.183 The following were the public works that the censors completed from the moneys allocated to them, and divided between them:

Lepidus built a causeway at Tarracina, a construction that brought him no thanks because he owned estates there and had included some private charges in the public account. He also contracted out the building of a theater seating area and a stage at the temple of Apollo,184 and the cleaning and whitewashing of the temple of Jupiter on the Capitol and the pillars around it. In addition, Lepidus took away from these pillars statues that seemed inappropriately mounted before them,185 and removed from the columns shields and all manner of military standards attached to them.
