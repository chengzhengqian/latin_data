“While I still live and breathe, you have both, through your unconscionable ambition and greed, already entered into your inheritance from me. You want me alive only to the point when, after surviving just one of you, I make the other the uncontested king at my death. You cannot bear to have either a brother or a father. Nothing is dear, nothing sacred to you. Everything has been supplanted by your insatiable craving for one thing—the throne. Come on, then, befoul your father’s ears, and with your accusations try to settle what you will soon settle with the sword. State openly whatever truths you can or whatever lies you like. My ears are open, but after today they will be closed to the recriminations you make behind each other’s backs.” After Philip uttered these words in furious rage, tears came to everyone’s eyes and gloomy silence long hung in the air.
