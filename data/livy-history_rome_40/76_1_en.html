They also had moneys for joint projects and from them they together put out for contract the building of an aqueduct and its arches. The work was blocked by Marcus Licinius Crassus who would not permit its being taken across his land. The same censors also established numerous transport taxes and rents from public property. In addition, they saw to a number of shrines and public places that had been appropriated by individuals being restored to their former religious and public status, and made them open to the people again. They reformed electoral procedure and designated the tribes by region on the basis of birth, social status and income.189
