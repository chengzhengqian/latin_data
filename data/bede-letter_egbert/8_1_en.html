Now if we believe and confess that some advantage is conveyed to believers by the laying on of hands, whereby the Holy Spirit is received: it is contrariwise plain that this selfsame advantage is lacking to them unto whom the laying on of hands is not granted. And who without doubt are to give account for this withholding of benefit more than those very bishops, which either omit or are unable to exercise the office of spiritual governance over them whose governors they promise to be?
