Soon afterwards he saw the calf brought by a leading-string to the sacrificial altar and coming close to the knife of the priest’s attendant. As he witnessed this he said, “Such is the death given you by the forbearance that leaves you free from my yoke. So then it will be better to endure toil however burdensome than to experience when young an ease that is soon to be lost.”

This is the lot of mortals; death comes swift to the happier ones, while the daily life of the unfortunate refuses them death. a
