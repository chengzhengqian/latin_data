5. With a few changes, a similar procedure is adopted for sea voyages. For an axle is passed through the sides of the hull, with ends projecting beyond the ship. On these axles are projecting wheels with diameters of 4 1/21 feet, having paddles round the edge which touch the water. Also the middle of the axle in the middle of the ship has a drum with one tooth projecting beyond its circumference. At this place a case is fixed with a drum enclosed within, having 400 teeth at equal intervals corresponding to the teeth of the drum which is fixed on the axle. In addition, another single tooth is fixed to the side of the drum and projects beyond it. 6.
