Chapter VI

on astrology

1. I have described the revolution of the firmament round the earth and the arrangement of the twelve signs and of the constellations to the north and south so as to present them to a clear view. For from that revolution of the firmament and the contrary motion of the sun through the signs and the equinoctial shadows of the gnomons, the diagrams of the analemma are discovered.

2. For the rest, as to astrology, the effects produced on the human course of life by the twelve signs, the five planets, the sun and moon, we must give way to the calculations of the Chaldaean astrologers,3 because the casting of nativities is special to them so that they can explain the past and the future from astronomical calculations.
