18* 20M, 17Mc, 15D, 3.81K

A contributing cause (maxima causa, Ascon., p. 68C.5) of the revolt of Rome’s Italian allies in 91 was the consular Lex Licinia Mucia of 95, which purged from the citizen rolls the names of persons who falsely laid claim to Roman citizenship despite being natives of allied communities in Italy south of the Po River.

The the Licinian law was injurious to all people on this side of the Po.
