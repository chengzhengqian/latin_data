Arus. GL 7.486.19–20 = p. 177DC: to illustrate insolitus + gen. of thing.

36* 40M, 38Mc, inc. 12D, 2.42K

Serv. ad G. 3.475: identifies Iapydia as a district of Venetia (a region in northern Illyricum), citing S. for a reference to Iapydia.

Prisc. GL 2.246.4–5: to illustrate the third decl. gen. sing. spelling –is of Orestes.
