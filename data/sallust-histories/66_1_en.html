Character Sketch of Pompey, frr. 17–21

17* 16M, 17Mc, inc. 75D, inc. 41K

The first of a series of fragments outlining Pompey’s character and past military exploits prior to his being sent to Spain in 77: a summation of Pompey’s outward appearance and inward essence. Syme (Tacitus, 150) translates “fair of face but dark within.”
