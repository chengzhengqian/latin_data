38 49M, 43Mc,28D, 52K

The return of Metellus Pius from the Sertorian War in the latter half of 71, sometime after the election of Pompey to the consulship (App. B Civ. 1.121). In contrast with Pompey, who retained his army upon returning to Italy in order to deal with the uprising of Spartacus, as he had been authorized to do by the senate, Metellus dismissed his army.
