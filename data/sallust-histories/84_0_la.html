61* 75M, 87Mc, 45D, 54K

sed Mithridates extrema pueritia regnum ingressus, matre sua veneno interfecta

Serv. & Serv. auct. ad Aen. 5.295: to illustrate pueritia, which Varro distinguished as one of the five periods in the lifetime of a human being, each of those five periods being subdivided, in turn, into three phases: early, middle, and advanced (here extrema).
