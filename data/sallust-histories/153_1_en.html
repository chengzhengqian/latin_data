94* 74M, 54Mc. 52D, 48K

A description of savage people living along the north shore of the Black Sea: the Achaei (Ov. Pont. 4.10.27; Mela 1.13) and the Tauri (a Scythian people inhabiting the Tauric Chersonesus, mod. Crimea).

For the fiercest of all people up to this time are the Achaei and Tauri because, so far as I can tell, they have been compelled by the poverty of their territories to live by banditry.
