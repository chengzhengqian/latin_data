4* 7M, 4Mc, inc. 50D, inc. 16K

Rome’s halfhearted and ineffective measures taken against the pirates prior to Pompey’s appointment in 67 caused harm to Rome’s allies (Dio 36.23.2). Antonius was notorious for his corrupt management of the campaign (Cic. 2 Verr. 3.213).

that the conduct of those who had come as allies to prevent war was more grievous than the war
