Saxaque ingentia et orbes axe1 iuncti per pronum incitabantur, axibusque eminebant2 in modum erici3 militaris veruta binum pedum.

Non. p. 554.30–555M.3 = 890L: to illustrate the noun verutum, defined as a short, thin missile (telum beve et angustum).

{in modum ericii militaris (Serv. ad Aen. 9.503, and Isid. Etym. 18.12.6): to illustrate the noun ericius (hedgehog), a military device named after the animal.}
