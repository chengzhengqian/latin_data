After his defeat, Fufidius summoned help from M. Domitius Calvinus (pr. 81, MRR 3.84), governor of the adjoining province of Hither Spain (Plut. Sert. 12.4).

He summoned the proconsul Domitius from Nearer Spain with all the forces Domitius had raised.
