These words occupy the first eleven lines of the left-hand column on the verso of the same leaf that preserves frr. 38 and 39, which form the upper half of the two columns on the recto. The supplement <annonae intolerabil> introducing this fragment would have come at the end of line 21, at the bottom of the ten lines that have been lost after the last words of fr. 39. The text is preserved on the verso of the Berlin fragment (B). Ends of lines in the manuscript are indicated by |, from which it can be plainly seen that the left margin of most lines in this column of text has suffered the loss of two or three letters. Supplements are to be credited to Pertz (Berl. Abhandl. 1847, 221ff.), unless otherwise indicated.
