SALLUST

31* 34M, 29Mc, 23D, 27K

Inde ortus sermo, percontantibus utrimque: satin salve, quam grati ducibus suis, quantis familiaribus copiis agerent.

Donat. ad Ter. Eun. 978: to illustrate the adv. salve meaning integre, recte, commode.

32* 35M, 30Mc, 24D, 29K

Hic est Marius, qui invita matre Iulia adeptus est consulatum, de quo Sallustius meminit.

Adnot. super Luc. 2.134: a detail included in a note on the battle near the town of Sacriportus, where in the spring of 82 Sulla defeated the younger Marius and caused him to flee to Praeneste in Latium.

33* 36M, 31Mc, 26D, 31K

et Marius victus duplicaverat bellum

Serv. ad Ecl. 2.67: to illustrate duplico meaning augeo.
