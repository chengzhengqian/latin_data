64* 72M, 71Mc, 60D, 18K

In the winter of 69/68 Lucullus visited Gordyene, a region of Armenia (present-day Kurdistan) south of Tigranocerta (Plut. Luc. 29.9; Dio 36.2.3–5).

In the land of the Gordyenes, amonum and other pleasant-scented plants grow.

65 74M, 72Mc, 62D, 20K

Lucullus defeated Tigranes in a series of battles fought in 68, and then moved against Tigranes’ capital, Artaxata, crossing the Arsanias/mod. Murat (Plut. Luc. 31.5–6), a river in western Armenia that rises near Mt. Ararat and is a principal source of the Euphrates.
