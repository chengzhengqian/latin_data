Tragedies by Authors Unknown

The mind was charmed by wine, which is by far The greatest god. a All men are hardened to the ways whereby To make their profit, neither are they squeamish. The woman’s mine—that scare-rattle. b Stay, stay, so that you may hear next. 165Ah me, c mother mine! . . . Encore the brother! . . . Clap your hands all!
  • aCp. Anon. Graec, Meineke: οἶνός μ᾿ ἔπεισε δαιμόνων ὑπέρτατος.
  • bPossibly Crobyle in Caecilius’ Plocium (Remains, Vol. I, 516 ff.).
  • c Vae mihi represents the Greek οἴμοι or ἰώ μοι.
