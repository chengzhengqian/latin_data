I am not able to think out and unfold a reason82–87 for which I may impeach him. For what could you impeach a man of who is honourable, if he has a conscience? And if he possesses a disposition of mind that has no conscience, on what again could you impeach him, who counts it as trifling when he has heard it? . . .   • bFrom a fabula praetexta? The scholiast is here commenting on the Roman custom of taking fresh auspices after a defeat in war.

  • d taura is a cow-calf born with a bull-calf; or a barren cow of hybrid breed, a free-martin.
