Thus we see that citizens of Athens, Rhodes, Sparta,30 and of other states are enrolled from far and wide as citizens of Greek states, and that the same persons are citizens of many states. And I myself have seen certain ignorant men, citizens of ours, misled by this,a sitting at Athens amongst jurymenb and members of the Areopagus,c in a definite tribe, under a definite number, since they did not know that if they had acquired citizenship there, they had forfeited it here, unless they should recover it “by right of subsequent return.”d But no one who understands our custom and our law has ever, if he wished to retain the citizenship of Rome, formally attached himself to another state.
