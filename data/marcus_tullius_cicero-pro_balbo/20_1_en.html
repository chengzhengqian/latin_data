How admirable that legal system which by divine31 guidance our forefathers have built up right from the beginnings of the name of Rome! That none of us can be a citizen of more than one state (for a difference of state must needs entail a difference in legal system), that no one should be removed from his citizen-roll against his will, nor be forced to remain on it against his will! For the unshakable foundations of our liberty are that each one of us has the absolute power of retaining or of renouncing his right of citizenship. But what undoubtedly has done most to establish our Empire and to increase the renown of the Roman People, is that Romulus, that first founder of this city, taught us by the treaty which he made with the Sabines,a that this State ought to be enlarged by the admission even of enemies as citizens.
