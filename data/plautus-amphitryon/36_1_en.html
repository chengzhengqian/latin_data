Amphitryon

alc

You’re testing me. Not long ago at all, just now.

amph

How can that happen the way you’re telling me, I wonder: not long ago, just now?

alc

What do you think then? That I am making fun of you, because you are making fun of me and claiming that695 you’ve now arrived for the first time? Just now you went away from here.

amph

(to Sosia) She’s talking nonsense.

sos

Wait for a bit until she sleeps off one slumber.

amph

What, she’s awake and dreaming?

alc

I am awake and awake I’m telling you what happened: not long ago, before sunlight, I saw both him (points to Sosia) and you.

amph

In what place?

alc

Here in the house where you live.700

amph

That’s never happened.

sos

Won’t you be quiet? What if the ship brought us here from the harbor while we were asleep?

amph

Are even you agreeing with her?

sos

What do you want to be done? Don’t you know? If you705 want to oppose a Bacchant in her frenzy, you’ll turn her from mad into madder and she’ll hit you all the more. If you humor her, you can settle it by receiving just one blow.

amph

But I’ve decided to scold her, since she didn’t want to greet me today on my arrival.

sos

You’ll stir up hornets.

amph

(to Sosia) Be quiet. (to his wife) Alcumena, I want to ask you one thing.

alc

Ask anything you like.
