Plot Summary 1

While Amphitruo was waging war against the Teloboians, Jupiter assumed his appearance and took a loan of his wife, Alcumena. Mercury takes on the form of his absent slave, Sosia. Alcumena is deceived by these tricks. After the real Amphitruo6 and Sosia have returned, they are both made fun of in a fantastic fashion. This leads to a quarrel between husband and wife, until Jupiter sends his voice from the skies, accompanied by a peal of thunder, and confesses to having been the adulterer.10
