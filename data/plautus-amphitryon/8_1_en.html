Who is braver or bolder than me? I know the ways of young people and still I’m walking around alone at this time of night. What would I do now if the Board of155 Three8 were to put me into prison? From there I’d be taken out for a flogging tomorrow, just like from a store room. I wouldn’t be allowed to defend myself; there wouldn’t be any help from my master and there wouldn’t be anyone who didn’t think that I fully deserved it.
