Amphitryon

Characters
  • MERCURY a god; in a slave’s outfit
  • SOSIA a slave; serves Amphitruo
  • JUPITER a god; in a general’s outfit
  • ALCUMENA a married woman; pregnant
  • AMPHITRUO a general; Alcumena’s husband
  • BLEPHARO a captain; Amphitruo’s friend
  • BROMIA a slave-girl; works for Amphitruo and his wife
Staging

The stage represents a street in Thebes. In the middle we can see Amphitruo’s house. To the left, the street leads to the harbor; to the right, to the city center.
