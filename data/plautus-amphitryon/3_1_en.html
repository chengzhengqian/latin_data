As you wish me to give you rich gain in the buying and selling of goods and to support you in everything, and as you wish me to advance the business matters and speculations of all of you abroad and at home and to prosper6 with good and large profit for ever what you have begun and what you will begin; and as you wish me to bring you and all your family members good news and to bring and announce what’s most profitable for your common good (for you already know that I was put in charge of messages11 and profit by the other gods); as you want me to bless you in these matters and to try my best so that you always have constant profit, you will keep silence during15 this play and you will all be fair and just judges. Now I’ll tell you on whose command and for what reason I’ve come, and at the same time I’ll tell you my name. I’ve come on Jupiter’s command. My name is Mercury. My20 father’s sent me here to plead with you. He did know that you were going to do what you were told by way of command, since he realized that you revere and fear him, as one should Jupiter; still, he’s told me to ask you for this by way of entreaty, mildly, with kind words.
