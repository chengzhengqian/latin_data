Amphitryon

mer

Then, indeed, against your wishes. This “indeed” is certain, it’s not optional. (hits him)

sos

Please spare me!

mer

You dare say that you are Sosia, the one I am? (hits him again)

sos

It’s over with me.

mer

(continues to beat him) Compared with what’s coming you’re still saying too little. Whose are you now?375

sos

Yours: with your fists you’ve made me yours by prescription.22 (very loudly) Help, citizens of Thebes!

mer

You’re shouting, villain? Tell me, what did you come for?

sos

So that there’d be someone you could cut down with your fists.

mer

Whose slave are you?

sos

I’m Amphitruo’s Sosia, I’m telling you.

mer

Well then, you’ll get a thrashing all the more because you’re an airbag: I am Sosia, not you. (hits him again)

sos

May the gods do so, so that you are him instead and so380 that I am beating you.

mer

You’re still muttering?

sos

I’ll be quiet now.

mer

Who’s your master?

sos

Anyone you want.

mer

What then? What are you called now?

sos

Only what you tell me to be called.

mer

You said you’re Amphitruo’s Sosia.

sos

I made a mistake: I wanted to say I’m Amphitruo’s associate.

mer

I knew we had no slave Sosia other than myself. You took385 leave of your senses.
