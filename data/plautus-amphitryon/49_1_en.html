alc

jup

Thank you. In human life many things of this sort happen: they reap enjoyment, and they reap misery again. Arguments come up between them, and they’re reconciled940 again. But if by chance any argument of this sort arises between them, and if they’re reconciled again, they’re twice as fond of each other as before.
