The bargain is struck for a space of three days, and then Lawrence is commended and dismissed, standing surety for himself and for the vast riches.

For three days he runs about the city gathering into one flock the companies of the infirm and all the beggars for alms. There a man showing two eyeless sockets is directing his straying, faltering step with the guidance of a staff; a cripple with a broken knee, a one-legged man with his other limb cut short, a man with one leg shorter than the other, are dragging unequal steps along. Here is one whose limbs are covered with sores and running with decayed matter, and one whose right hand is withered, the muscles contracted to the elbow.
