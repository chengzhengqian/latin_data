If we duly reverence the day of thy festival with lips and heart, if we bow down before thy relics rejoicing in them, come down to us here for a little while bringing the favour of Christ, that our burdened souls may feel the relief of forgiveness. So may there remain no long time ere thy noble spirit put on again in resurrection the body which did deeds as valorous as its own, that the body which shared the struggles and bore the hazard in common may with it inherit the glory too through all ages for ever and ever.
