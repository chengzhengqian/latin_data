Exit DIABOLUS to the right. Act Two Enter LIBANUS from the right.

lib Good god, Libanus, it’s better to wake up now and to think up a trick for procuring the money. It’s already a251 long time since you left your master and went to the market to think up a trick for procuring the money there. There you dozed in idleness until this time of the day. Shake off all sluggishness from you, get rid of your laziness, and get back to your old clever ways! Save your master,256 don’t do the same as other slaves do, who have cunning ways only in order to cheat master. (after a pause) Where should I take it from? Whom should I swindle? Where should I steer this speed-boat? (looks around) I have a favorable omen, a favorable sign: the birds let me go in any direction. The woodpecker and the crow on the260 left and the raven and the owl on the right recommend it; 14 I’ve decided to follow your advice. But what’s this?
