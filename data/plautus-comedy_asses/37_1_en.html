leo Follow me this way. (approaches Argyrippus)

arg (addressing the slaves) Is there any salvation? You’ve talked enough.

leo Listen, you two, pay attention, and devour my words. First of all we don’t deny that we’re your slaves. But if you650 get twenty silver minas, by what name will you call us?

arg Freedmen.

leo Not patrons?

arg That rather.

leo There is twenty minas here in this wallet. (holds it up) I’ll give it to you now if you want to.

arg May the gods prosper you always, guardian of your master,655 glory of the people, storehouse of riches, inner salvation of the body and commander of love. Put it here, place this wallet here plainly on my neck.

leo I don’t want you to bear this burden for me, since you’re my master.
