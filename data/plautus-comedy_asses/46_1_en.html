dia (angrily) Follow me this way. Should I bear this or keep810 quiet? I’d rather die than not reveal this to his wife. (shouting into the house) You say so? At your mistress’s place you want to do a young man’s job, and to your wife you want to make excuses and say you’re an old bloke? You want to snatch a prostitute from her lover and throw money at the madam? You want to filch things from your wife at home? (muttering to himself) I’d rather hang myself816 than let you get away with this untold. This instant I’ll go to her from here, to the woman you will shortly, I know it, murder—unless of course she manages to murder you first—just so that you can supply the funds for your luxurious lifestyle!

han (calmly, judiciously) I think one should act like this: it’s820 more honorable for me to reveal this matter than it is for you to do so, so that she doesn’t think you were upset and did it out of love rather than for her sake.
