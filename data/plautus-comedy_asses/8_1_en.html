Exit LIBANUS to the right.

dem There can’t be a worse slave than this one, or one cleverer and more difficult to be on one’s guard against. But if120 you want anything sorted out well, you should give it to this same man. He’ll prefer dying a miserable death to not fulfilling his promise perfectly. I am as certain that this money is waiting for my son as I am certain that I’m looking at this stick. 6 But why am I delaying going to the125 market where I’d set out? I’ll go and stay there at the banker’s.

Exit DEMAENETUS to the right. Enter DIABOLUS from Cleareta’s house.

dia (shouting) So this is what’s happening? I’m being thrown out of the house? Is this the reward given to someone who’s done you so many good turns? You’re bad to the one who does you a good turn, you’re good to the one who does you a bad turn. But you’ll suffer for it!
