IX. Does the Court feel that this devotee of Venus, who came to his province fresh from Chelidon’s arms, has used the name of Venus to secure enough for himself?

Let me now tell you of another false charge, not25 less unscrupulous, though it involved a smaller sum of money. There are two brothers belonging to Agyrium, named Sosippus and Philocrates. Their father died twenty-two years ago. His will provided that, if a certain stipulation were not carried out, the estate should be forfeited to Venus.
