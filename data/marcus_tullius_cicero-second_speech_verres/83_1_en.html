And if the people wept153 then, so did I, so did you, Hortensius, so did these who will presently give their verdict: and for this reason, that the cause concerns us all, and the danger threatens us all, and it is by the rescuing hands of us all that this wickedness must be extinguished like some destroying fire. For we are the fathers of little children, and no one of us can tell how long he has to live: we must take counsel, ere we die, and make provision that their lonely years of childhood may have as strong a rampart as we can build round them. For who can protect our children in their early years against the wickedness of our magistrates? Their mothers, to be sure. And a strong defence indeed little Annia found her mother, great lady though the mother was! Much she could do, with her cries for help to God and man, to stay Verres from robbing her infant daughter of all that the child’s father had bequeathed her! Will guardians save them? An easy thing indeed, with a praetor such as this, with whom Marcus Marcellus pleaded for his ward Iunius, to find that his eloquence, his earnest wishes, his personal influence, all went for nothing!
