Under your 98 governorship, those tiny pirate vessels sailed about freely where only once within human memory had a fleet forced an entrance, the vast and mighty fleet of Athens with her three hundred ships—and it was defeated and crushed in that same harbour by the sheer strength of that harbour’s position. There it was that the hitherto triumphant power of that famous state was shattered and brought low; it was in this harbour that we think of the pride and power and glory of the Athenian people as suffering shipwreck.

XXXVIII. What, has a pirate pushed his way in to a spot where the city was not only no longer in front of him but a great part of it left behind him?
