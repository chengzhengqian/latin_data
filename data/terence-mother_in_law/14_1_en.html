The Mother-In-Law

Act three Enter pamphilus and parmeno left from the direction of the harbour.
pam

I don’t believe anyone has ever had more anguish inflicted on him by love than I have. Oh! What an evil fate! Was this the life I refrained from destroying? Was it for this I was so eager to return home? Oh! How much preferable it would have been to live out my days anywhere else in the world than to come back here and discover this awful situation! For all of us who have some trouble coming our way from somewhere, the time intervening before we discover it is all gain.

par

But this way you’ll get to free yourself from these worries all the sooner. If you hadn’t come back, their quarrels would have become far more serious. As it is, I know your return will bring them both back to their senses, Pamphilus. You’ll ascertain the facts, settle their quarrels, and effect a reconciliation. Things that you’ve persuaded yourself are very serious are actually trifles.
