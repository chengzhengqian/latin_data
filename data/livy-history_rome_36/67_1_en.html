At the end of the year elections were held at Rome. Lucius Cornelius Scipio and Gaius Laelius179 were elected consuls—the eyes of all were on Africanus—to finish the war with Antiochus. The next day the following were elected praetors: Marcus Tuccius, Lucius Aurunculeius, Gnaeus Fulvius, Lucius Aemilius, Publius Iunius, and Gaius Atinius Labeo.
