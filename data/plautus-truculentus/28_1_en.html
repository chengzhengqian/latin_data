He’ll be here presently, I believe; now I’m deliberately taking precautions and am fitted out in such a way475 that I appear unwell as if I were confined to bed after childbirth. (into the house) Bring me some myrrh here and put some fire onto the altar, so that I can do homage to my Lucina!23 Put it here and get out of my sight. (as they obey, into the house) Hey, Pithecium! Let me lie down, come here, help me! (a servant girl appears and assists Phronesium) There, that’s how it should be for a new mother. (into the house again) Take my sandals off and put the cloak here over me, Archilis! (a servant appears and obeys; into the house) Where are you,480 Astaphium? Bring me some verbena24 and dainties from inside.
