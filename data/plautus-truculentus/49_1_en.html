cal Gods, I implore your protection! How much easier it was for one woman than for the other to give birth to one and the same boy! This one gave birth to a boy through another’s labor and without pains. The boy at least got lucky: he has two mothers and two maternal grandmothers. Now I’m concerned about how many fathers there have been. Do look at a woman’s crime!

servant This wickedness has more to do with men than with810 women: a man got her pregnant, not a woman.

cal I know that too. You’ve been a good guardian for her.

servant The one who is stronger can do more. He was a man, he was stronger. He overpowered her and took away what he was after.

cal And to you that same man brought a great thrashing.

servant I’m well aware of that, even if you keep silent, because815 I’ve experienced it in reality.

servant I’ve been silent till now; now I won’t be silent because he’s present and doesn’t show himself.
