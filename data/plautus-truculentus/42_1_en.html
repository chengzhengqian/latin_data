While this is already pleasurable, the other news is absolutely sweet for me: the705 soldier’s gifts were considered hateful and unwelcome. I’m completely joyful. The ball is mine:40 if the soldier is rejected, the woman will be with me. I’m safe because I’m perishing. If I weren’t perishing, I’d have plainly perished. Now I’ll spy on what’s going on there, who is going in, and who is coming out. From here from a distance I’ll observe what’s happening to my fate. As I don’t 710 have anything—well, I’ve removed all my property here —I’ll be at her mercy.
