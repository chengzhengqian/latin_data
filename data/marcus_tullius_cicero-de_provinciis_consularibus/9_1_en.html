VI. Would you not think that such men, instead of13 being at last called back, should be dragged back from their provinces? Would you keep there that couple to be scourges of our allies, murderers of our soldiers, destroyers of our revenue-farmers, devastators of our provinces, blots upon our Empire? You are the same men who endeavoured to recall them last year,a when they had hardly reached their provinces. At that time, if your votes had been free, and if the matter had not been so often adjourned and finally taken out of your hands, you would have done as you desired, and restored your authority by recalling those through whom you had lost it, and wresting from them the very rewardsb which they had obtained in return for their crime and their destruction of our country. If then, very much against your will, they14 escaped this penalty, not by their own resources but by those of others,c yet they have had to submit to one that was far greater and more severe.
