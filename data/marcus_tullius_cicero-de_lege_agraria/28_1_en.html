Here, since I see that your hearts are moved by the dignity of the man and the insult offered by the law, I will repeat what I said at the outset, that it is Kings who are being set up, that your liberty is entirely abolished by this law. Or did you think25 otherwise? did you not think that, when a few men had cast greedy eyes upon all your possessions, the first thing they would do would be to drive out Pompeius from the guardianship of your liberty, from all authority and office, and from the protection of your interests?
