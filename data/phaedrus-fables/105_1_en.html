Shortly afterwards a thief carried off a silver bracelet. When the woman was told that it was nowhere to be found she became furious, called in all the servants, and threatened them with heavy blows if they failed to tell the truth. “Threaten others,” said Aesop, “you will not deceive me, mistress; I was beaten with whips just a little while ago because I did speak the truth.”
