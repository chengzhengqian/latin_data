But lest my strict sense of duty go wrong through insufficient knowledge, take these hives and distill your respective products into the waxen cells, so that from the flavour of the honey and the pattern of the comb, matters now in question, it may be evident who was responsible for these combs.” The drones refused, but the bees were pleased with the proposed test. Then the wasp rendered judgment as follows: “It is plainly evident who can’t have made and who did make the combs; wherefore, I restore the fruit of their labours to the bees.”

14 Concerning Relaxation and Tension

A certain Athenian, on seeing Aesop in a crowd of boys playing with nuts, stopped and laughed at him as though he were crazy. As soon as he perceived this, the old man, who was one to laugh at others rather than one to be laughed at himself, placed an unstrung bow in the middle of the street and said: “Here now, Mr. Philosopher, interpret my symbolic action.” The people gathered around.
