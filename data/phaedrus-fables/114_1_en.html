Perotti’s Appendix

“Greetings,” said the fox, “I hope you are well. Why, pray, did you fly from me? As if I didn’t have plenty to eat in the meadow—crickets, beetles, and a good supply of locusts. You needn’t be afraid; I am very fond of you because of your peaceful habits and your upright life.” The singer replied: “Your statement of principles is excellent. On the ground I am not a match for you, but in the air I am a match. Come, follow me; I’ll entrust my safety to you up here.”
