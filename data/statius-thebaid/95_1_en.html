For then was the deadly banquet and the sun’s midday retreat, there too other brothers joined battle. 46

Now the news had filled Atalanta’s ears, that her son was going a captain to war and moving all Arcadia. Her steps tottered, her weapon fell by her side. Swifter than winged wind she flees the forest over rocks and rivers that blocked her way with brimming banks, just as she was, her robe girt up, the yellow hair on her head scattering in the breeze, as an angry tigress bereft of her cubs follows the tracks of the robber horse. Anon she halts and thrusts her bosom against his bridle (he pale and downcast): 47 ‘Whence this mad desire, my son? Whence this unconscionable valour in your youthful breast? Can you train men for war? Can you bear the burdens of Mars and move among sword-bearing squadrons? But where the strength? Only lately I turned pale to see you pressing your hunting spear against a thrusting boar in close combat, forced back upon bent knee and near collapse; and if I had not shot an arrow from my curving bow, where would your war be now? My shafts will not avail you nor the smooth bow nor this piebald horse with the black spots, in whom you trust.
