  • 19Cadmus, who as sower of the dragon’s teeth is regarded as progenitor of the Thebans. In strictness ‘Harmonia’s race’ (271) should not include the Thebans as a whole, nor were they Mars’ descendants (nepotes), unless because by some accounts the dragon was his child. But again this may be merely lax terminology. Cf. 10.893 Harmoniae populos.
