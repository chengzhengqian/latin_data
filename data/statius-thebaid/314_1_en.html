He feels the fire hiss within him, in helmet and hair, tries to push back the burning corselet with his hand, touches the ashes of the steel at his breast; but still he stands and breathes his last against the stars, leaning his smoking breast against the hated walls. Nor would he have fallen; but his earthly limbs desert him and his spirit is set free. If his body had yielded a little later, he might have hoped for 66 a second bolt.
