Then to the gods of the sea and the Winds and Aegaeon 19 embracing the Cyclades I entrust my father hidden in curved timber. Our mutual tears would have no term, were not Lucifer now dismissing the stars from eastern heaven. Then indeed on the sounding shore, with many a fear in mind and scarce trusting Lyaeus, I separate. With urgent step I go, but my troubled heart looks back; nor do I rest from viewing from every hill the breezes rising in the sky and the waves.

Dawn comes up ashamed and Titan revealing 20 the heavens turns his rays from Lemnos, bringing back his chariot aslant behind a cloud. The madness of the night showed plain, and in fear of the new light sudden shame was upon them all, though all were in like case. They bury their impious crimes in earth or burn them in hasty fires.
