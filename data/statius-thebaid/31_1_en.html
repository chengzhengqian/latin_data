Then sounded many a boxwood pipe and cymbals louder than the beating of bullhide. 10 Cithaeron himself had merrily driven sane 11 mothers through the wooded wilds under a kinder Bacchus. Such feasts do Bistones in wild assembly lay out on Rhodope or amid Ossa’s vales; for them a sheep half living, food shaken from lions’ jaws, and blood diluted with new milk is luxury; but if ever the fierce odour of Ogygian Iacchus breathes upon them, then they love to scatter stones and winecups, and after spilling guiltless blood of comrades to begin the day afresh and reset the festal boards.

Such the night when from the silent air the swift Cyllenian glided to the Echionian monarch’s bed, where he had spread his huge frame on a high mattress, his limbs piled on Assyrian 12 draperies. Ah mortal hearts all unknowing of their destinies! He banquets, he sleeps—even he! Then the ancient does as he was bidden; and lest he could seem a false vision of the night, he takes upon himself the shadowed 13 features and voice and familiar woollen circlets of long-lived Tiresias. His hair remained, as did the white beard combed down from the chin and his own pallor; but a false headband ran through his locks and the grace of fillets entwined with grey olive emerged to view.
