VIA Hymn Before Sleep

Be present, most high Father, whom no man hath seen at any time, and Christ the Word of the Father, and Thou, kindly Spirit; O Thou who in this Trinity art one essence and one light, God of God everlasting, and God sent forth of both. The day’s toil is past and the hour of rest comes again; caressing slumber in its turn relaxes our tired limbs. The mind storm-tossed and careworn drinks deep the cup of forgetfulness.
