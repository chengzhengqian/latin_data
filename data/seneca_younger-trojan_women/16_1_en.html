TROJAN WOMEN

pyrrhus

Yes, of our ancestral sea.25

agamemnon

You were conceived through the furtive seduction of a virgin, fathered by Achilles, but before he came of age!

pyrrhus

That same Achilles who holds the cosmos by a lineage branching into each of the gods’ realms—the sea through Thetis, the underworld through Aeacus, the heavens through Jove.26

agamemnon

That same Achilles who lies dead by Paris’ hand.

pyrrhus

And whom not one god challenged in hand-to-hand fighting.

agamemnon

In my position I could check your words and tame your recklessness with pain. But my sword knows how to show mercy, even to prisoners. Instead, let the gods’ spokesman Calchas be summoned; if Fate demands, I shall agree.

You who untied the fastenings of the Pelasgian fleet and the obstacles to war,27 who unlock the heavens by your skill, to whom the hidden entrails, the sky’s thunder crash, and the star tracing a long fiery trail give signs of fate—and whose pronouncements cost me an enormous price: declare
