Come on, take the other cudgel from him, Sparax. Come on, one of you stand there, the other here. Stand like this, both of you. Now listen: if he touches them with a810 finger against their wishes today and if you don’t give him a taste of your cudgels until he doesn’t know what way to go home, you’re both dead. If he addresses anyone, you two must reply in their place from there. But if he815 wants to go away himself, you must instantly compass about his shins with the clubs as quickly as possible.
