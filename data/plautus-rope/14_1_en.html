Rudens

pal

Whose voice can I hear nearby?

amp

I’ve become scared; who is speaking nearby?230

pal

Kind Hope, please come to my assistance! Free a poor girl from this fear.

amp

Certainly a woman’s voice has touched my ears.

pal

It is a woman, a woman’s voice is coming to my ears. Please, is it Ampelisca?235

amp

Can I hear you, Palaestra?

pal

Why don’t I call her by her name so that she can hear me? (loudly) Ampelisca!

amp

Ha! Who is it?

pal

I’m Palaestra.

amp

Tell me, where are you?

pal

Goodness, right now I’m in very many troubles.

amp

I’m your partner: my share isn’t smaller than yours. But240 I’m keen to see you.

pal

You’re my rival in this.

amp

Let’s follow each other’s voices, step by step. Where are you?

pal

(spotting her) Here I am. Come to me, approach me from the other side.

amp

I’m doing my best.

pal

Give me your hand.

amp

Take it. (they shake hands)

pal

Tell me whether you’re alive, please.

amp

You make me want to live again, now that I can touch245 you. How I can hardly believe myself in this, that I’m holding you! Please embrace me, my hope. (as they are holding each other tight) How you’re already relieving me from all my sufferings!
