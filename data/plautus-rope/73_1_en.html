lab

You’ve hit the nail on the head.

gri

You look the part. But what’s wrong with you?

lab

Last night the ship belonging to me and someone else was wrecked at sea; whatever I had in it, I’ve lost it all.

gri

What have you lost?

lab

A trunk with a lot of gold and silver.
