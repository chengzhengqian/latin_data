What human being can give another the power to understand this? What angel can give it to another angel?130 What angel can give it to a mortal? We must ask it of you. We must seek it from you. We must knock at your door. This, this, is how it will be received. This is how it will be found. This is how it will be opened.131

  • 130Knauer, “Psalmenzitate,” 156n2, identifies this as the angel of Rv 22:6.
  • 131The closing section is a re-presentation of Mt 7:7–8, a “kind of refrain” (GM) picking up Conf. 12.1.1 and answering its questioning. OD notes the shift from subjunctive to indicative, marking the surety of the promises.
