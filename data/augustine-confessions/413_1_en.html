You tell me in reply that you are my God, and you speak with a strong voice to my inner ear, for I am your servant: you break through my deafness108 and cry: “O humanity109—of course what my scripture says is what I say! Nevertheless it speaks in a way that is subject to time; whereas my Word is untouched by time, for it exists with me in coeternity. So what you see by my Spirit is what I see, just as what you say through my Spirit is what I say. This is why, when you see things in a way that is subject to time, that is not how I see them; and in the same way, when you say things in a way that is subject to time, that is not how I say them.”
