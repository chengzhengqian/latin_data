Look, O Lord my God, how much we have written about so few words of scripture—please, look and see how much! Is our strength enough, is there time enough, to treat all your books at such length? So allow me to confess to you more briefly while immersed in them, and choose one meaning that you have inspired as true, sure and good (even if many meanings present themselves, where multiple meanings are possible), in the faith of my confession: so, if I say what your servant meant, in the best, most proper way (it is my duty to aim for this), but I do not succeed, I shall still say what your Truth wills to speak to me through Moses’ words; for to him also your Truth spoke whatever he willed.
