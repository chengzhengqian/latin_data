This was your command, when you said, Sir 3:17“do all your works in gentleness and you will be loved by everyone.” The livestock too will be good, and when they feed it will not be to excess, and when they do not they feel no sense of lack. 22. (32) Look, O Lord our God, our Creator! When our emotions have been kept away from love of this world, in which we were dying by living badly;79 and when our soul began to be alive by living well; and when your word has been fulfilled, by which you said through your apostle, “do not be conformed80 to this world”: there will follow what you immediately added, saying, “instead, be reformed by the renewing of your mind.” This does not mean Gn 1:21“according to your kind,” in the sense of people who follow the example of their neighbors, nor is it living according to the influence of those who are more worthy. After all, you did not say, “let there be human beings according to their kind,” but Gn 1:26“let us make a human being after our image and likeness,”81 so that we could learn what your will may be.
