(2) So I was becoming more and more denseMt 13:15; Acts 28:27 in my heart, and I was not even transparent to my own self. Whatever was not extended through some degree of space, or diffused, or amassed, or enlarged, or had some kind of dimension, or was capable of so having—I judged that no such thing could exist. Just as my eyes are accustomed to range over certain physical shapes, so my heart ranged over the corresponding images, and I did not see that this actual mental effort by which I was forming those very images was not something of a physical kind.
