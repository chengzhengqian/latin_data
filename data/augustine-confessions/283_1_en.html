Also all the emotions of our spirit, in accordance with their various types, have their own particular vocal and singing modes that are stimulated by a kind of mysterious kinship.99 But the sensual pleasure that affects me physically (I should not surrender my mind to this and thus enfeeble it) often leads me astray: as when sense perception does not accompany reason in such a way as to be content with second place, but (as it was only allowed in because it accompanied reason) tries to get to the front and take the lead. In such cases, therefore, I sin without realizing, and then afterward I do realize.
