Their book is never closed,Is 34:4 and their scroll is never rolled up: for you yourself are their book and their scroll for ever.50 You have given them a place above this firmament that you established over the weakness of the peoples dwelling below, so that they could look up and recognize your mercy and your truth declaring your existence in time—you, who made time itself! For your mercy, Lord, abides in heaven Ps 36:5and your truth reaches up to the clouds. The clouds pass away; but heaven abides. The preachers of your word pass from this life to another life; and still your Scripture is stretched out over your peoples, to the end of the age. Mt 24:35Both heaven and earth shall pass away, but your words will not pass away: the skin51 will be rolled up, Is 40:6-8and the grass over which it was stretched out will pass away, together with all its bright splendor, but your Word abides eternally. He now appears to us clouded in mystery, 1 Cor 13:12; cf. 1 Jn 3:2and in the reflection from heaven’s mirror, not as he really is;52 because although we are the beloved of your Son, 1 Jn 3:2Sg 2:9VLSg 1:31 Jn 3:2it has not yet been revealed what we shall be. He gazes through the lattice of our flesh, and encourages us, and sets our hearts on fire, and we run after his sweet fragrance. But when he does appear, we shall be like him, for we shall see him as he really is. Then, Lord, our seeing will be in full reality; but this is not for us just yet.
