Silius Italicus

Liber Secundus

argument

The Roman envoys, dismissed by Hannibal, proceed to Carthage (1–24). Hannibal addresses his men and goes on with the siege (25–269). The Roman envoys are received in the Carthaginian senate: speeches of Hanno and Gestar: Fabius declares war (270–390).
