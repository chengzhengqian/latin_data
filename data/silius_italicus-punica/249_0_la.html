Silius Italicus

Liber Decimus

argument

Description of the battle continued: valour and death of Paulus (1–325). Flushed with victory, Hannibal intends to march on Rome next day; but Juno sends the god of Sleep to stop him (326–370). He yields, in spite of the strong protests of Mago (371–386). The remnant of the Roman army rally at Canusium: their miserable plight (387–414). Metellus proposes that the Romans should leave Italy; but Scipio threatens death to him and his sympathizers (415–448).
