First of all, a black bull was offered to the Invisible King,b and next an unmated heifer to the goddess of Henna.c Lastly, chosen sheep with woolly fleeces were slain in honour of Alectod and of Megaera, the Fury who never smiles. And over them honey was poured, with an offering of wine and milk. “Stand firm, young man,” she cried, “and endure the sight of those who are rising from all Erebus. I see all Tartarus approaching, and the Thirde Kingdom of the world presents itself to our sight. Lo, shapes of all kinds come flocking, and all mankind who have been born and have died since the primal chaos; soon you shall see everything—Cyclopes and Scylla, and the Thracian horses that fed on human flesh.f Fix your gaze firmly upon them all, and grasp your drawn sword undaunted. If any spirits press forward to drink of the blood before the form of the chaste Sibyl advances, hew them in pieces. But meantime look at yonder unburied ghostg which comes quickly hither and desires to have speech with you.
