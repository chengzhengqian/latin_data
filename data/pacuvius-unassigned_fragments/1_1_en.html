(I)The Gods:

1

Varro: Pacuvius has—

When thrice-portentous portents of the gods (II) War?

2–3

Isidore: ‘Volucres,’ ‘flying,’ is an epithet which we apply not only to birds. . . . Of Pacuvius c we have—

On flying axle-tree the chariot runs With team of four.

  • cPossibly in Medus (R., 321); but this fr. might refer to a battle.
