321

Brother and doctor <accuse> one another of poisoning

Brothers sharing an inheritance became enemies. They divided. One of them made a doctor his heir. Later they were reconciled. The one who had the doctor as his friend, after dining with his brother and returning home, said he suspected he had been given poison. The doctor replied that he would give him a potion as an antidote1 and did so. Having drunk it the man died. The brother and the doctor accuse each other of poisoning.

declamation
