A tyrannicide in woman’s clothing

<Let there be> a reward for a tyrannicide. When a tyrant ordered that somebody’s sister be brought to the castle, the brother went up in his sister’s clothes and killed the tyrant. By way of reward the magistrate put up a statue to him dressed in the same clothes. He is accused of injuries.

  • 3A sudden switch: he bethinks him that as soon as the son gets himself reestablished as son and heir he will kill for the inheritance—instead of killing now. Even so, he had intended parricide.
