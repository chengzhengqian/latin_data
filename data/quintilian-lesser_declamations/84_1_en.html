Beater of a person under stigma in a temple

Whoso commits injury in a temple, let him pay ten thousand to the person he has injured and ten to the community. Let a person under stigma be barred from taking action for injuries. A man beat a person under stigma in a temple. The magistrate claims ten thousand in the name of the community.
