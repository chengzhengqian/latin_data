271

Three times a hero against third hero

A poor man and a rich man were enemies. The poor man’s son became a hero as did one of the rich man’s sons. Under the law the heroes contended in arms. The rich man’s son was killed. Again, another son of the rich man became a hero, as did the poor man’s son, and was killed fighting about the reward. In a third war the rich man’s remaining son also became a hero, as did the same son of the poor man. The rich man forbids his son to fight. When he disobeys, he disowns him.
