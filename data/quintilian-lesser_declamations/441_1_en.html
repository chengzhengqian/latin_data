384

Virgin sacrificed for plague

In a plague it was responded1 that a virgin must be sacrificed. A virgin was chosen by lot, but her father denied that she was a virgin.2 The magistrate sacrificed her. The plague did not end. They drew lots again. A second was sacrificed. The plague subsided. The magistrate is prosecuted for murder by the father of her predecessor.
