It was unnecessary, gentlemen, for me to discourse about the rationale of this act which is produced as a charge. I know what suffices for an advocate. It would be enough of itself if I exculpated the defendant under the law. Other things might perhaps be done for his dignity, his conscience, his credit, but all that concerns you is whether the accused is liable under the law governing your inquiry. The more drastic that law is, the more carefully you must look into it.
