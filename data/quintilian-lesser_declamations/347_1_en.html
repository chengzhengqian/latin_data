If the laws allowed and you permitted, she would ask to be tortured, demand that she be put on fires, lacerated with lashes. What are you doing, woman? You act rashly; you are a woman, an old woman. Perhaps you might bear the first torments, but pain will prevail and at the finish you will give up. Gentlemen, what wonderful words! “Just let him torture,” she says, “I am a mother. A foster mother and a slave, and with her master torturing, that’s another thing.” Come, when you yield your last breath in this utterance, to whom shall we give the youth? Will you want him to go back to his father and that stepmother?27
