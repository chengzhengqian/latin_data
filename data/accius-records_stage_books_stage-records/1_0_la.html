Accius

Didascalicaa sive Didascalicon Libri

This work of Accius dealt with the same subjects as the Greek διδασκαλίαιb which aimed at producing chronological surveys of playwrights and their plays, with notes on the most famous actors therein. Accius was the first to attempt such a survey in Latin, and including under his title details of wider scope than his Greek models, wrote at least nine books which were undoubtedly in Sotadic metre (Lachmann, in Index Lect., Berl.
