Records of the Stage

Book IX

14–15

The many types of poems:

Charisius: ‘Poematorum.’ . . . Accius in the ninth book of Records of the Stage—

Then learn, dear Baebius, if you please, how varied The sorts of poems are, and how by far They differ one from another.

16

Uncertain:

Priscianus: ‘Magnificens.’ . . . Accius in the ninth book of Records of the Stage has ‘magnificissimei’ and ‘excelsissimei’—

and the magnificalest, loftiest too in honour.

17

Charisius: ‘Statim.’ Accius in the ninth book of Records of the Stage—

Let the duesa be brought out instantly and kept safe.
