The sound of the gospel with its piercing word has loosened the frosts of Scythia, and its warmth unlocked the Hyrcanian winter, so that Rhodopeian Hebrus, freed from ice, is now a kindlier stream as it flows from the rocks of Caucasus.a The Getans have grown peaceable and the bloody, savage Gelonian, when he thirsts, fills bloodless cups with pure milk, for he will taste the holy draught of the blood of Christ. The once treacherous land of Moorish Atlas has learned to dedicate its long-haired kings at Christ’s altar. Since the Spirit, that Spirit who is God, touched a mortal womb and God entered into a mother’s body and by a virgin made himself man, the cavernb of Delphi has fallen silent, its oracles condemned; no longer does the cauldron direct responses from the tripod. No longer does a priest possessed utter with foaming mouth and panting breathc fates drawn from Sibylline Books. Lying Dodona has lost its maddening vapours. Cumae is dumb and mourns for its dead oracles, and Ammon returns no answer in the deserts of Libya.
