I’ll have to wait for the time of year which sees the start of lawsuits brought by the entire populace. 10 And even then, I’ll have to put up with a thousand frustrations and delays. Often, the benches are just being set out and eloquent Caedicius 11 is now taking off his cloak and Fuscus 12 is now taking a leak—and though we are all ready, we disperse. That’s how we battle it out in the sticky arena of the forum. But the men protected by armour and circled by a sword belt have the times of cases set at their own convenience. Their resources are not frittered away by the long drag of a lawsuit.

Besides, it’s only soldiers who have the right to make a will while their fathers are still alive. 13 You see, it’s been decreed that wealth acquired in military service should not form part of the assets controlled entirely by the father. And so while Coranus 14 follows the standards and earns a soldier’s pay, he is courted by his own father, though he’s already doddering. The son is promoted by the advancement which is his due and gets the rewards for his fine efforts.
