Besides, who’d accompany you so far from Rome? Who’d be like Pylades 6 and venture beyond the massive Embankment? 7 Let’s dry our tears at once. Let’s not bother our friends who will only make their excuses. When the judge says, “Call your witness,” suppose the person who saw the attack has the nerve to say “I saw it,” I’d think him good enough for the beard and long hair of our ancestors. 8 You can more quickly produce a false witness against a civilian than someone to tell the truth against the property and honour of a military man.
