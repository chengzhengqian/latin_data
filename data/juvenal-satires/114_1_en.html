What’ll I do when winter starts blowing? What’ll I say, please, to the boys’ shoulder blades and feet in December’s northerly gales? ‘Hang on and wait for the cicadas’? 16

“But though you ignore and disregard my other services, how do you value the fact that if I had not been your devoted and obedient client, your wife would still be a virgin? You know very well indeed how often you asked for that favour—the different ways you wheedled and the promises you made. Your bride was actually walking out on you when I grabbed her and embraced her. She’d even destroyed the contract and was already in the process of making a new arrangement. I spent the whole night on it and only just managed to retrieve the situation, with you sobbing outside the door. My witness is the couch—and you—you could surely hear the sound of the bed and its mistress’ voice. There are many households where a lover has saved a marriage that’s shaky and starting to fall apart and already more or less dissolved. Which way can you turn? What are your priorities? Is it no service, no service at all, you ungrateful cheat, that your little son or your daughter is my child? After all, you acknowledge them as your own and you’re delighted to splash all over the newspapers the proofs of your virility.
