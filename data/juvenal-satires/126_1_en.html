No, it will be that famous avenger of Cannae, retaliating for all the bloodshed—a little ring. 31 Off you go, you maniac, zoom through the hostile Alps—to entertain schoolboys and to be put into their speeches.

One world is not enough for the young man from Pella. 32 In discontent he seethes at the narrow limits of the universe as if confined on the rocks of Gyara or tiny Seriphus. 33 But once he’s entered the city that’s fortified by potters, his coffin will be big enough. 34 It’s only death that reveals the minuscule size of human bodies.
