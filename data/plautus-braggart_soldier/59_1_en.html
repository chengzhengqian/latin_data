That you don’t spurn the woman who desires you, who now lives through your life; whether she has hope or not depends entirely on you.

To address you, embrace you, caress you; unless you come to her help, she’ll lose all hope. Go on, my dear Achilles, fulfill my request, save that beautiful woman beautifully, display your kind nature, you capturer of1055 cities,44 you slayer of kings.
