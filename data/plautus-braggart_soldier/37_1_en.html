I’ll show you more of my charm than I’ll make a show of it. I never make a move on another’s prostitute at a banquet, I don’t snatch away the titbits, I don’t take the cup out of turn, and no disagreement ever arises from me at a banquet on account of the wine. If anyone is tedious655 there, I go home and cut off the conversation; I practice Venus, love, and loveliness while reclining at table. pal All your ways are geared toward charm. I’ll make you yourself admit that I’m a youngster in character, so overflowing with kind acts will I appear in all things. Do you need a grim, angry advocate? Here I am! Do you need a mild one? You’ll call me milder than the silent sea and I’ll be gentler than the zephyr. From the665 very same place I’ll produce for you the most joyful guest or a first-rank hanger-on and perfect caterer. Next, when it comes to dancing, a catamite isn’t as soft as I am.
