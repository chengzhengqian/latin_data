Miles Gloriosus

per

Bravo, bravo, splendid! I praise your idea!

pal

So if that fellow slave of mine accuses her in front of the soldier, saying that he’s seen her kissing someone else, I’ll rebut him and say that he’s seen this one at your place embracing and kissing her lover.245

per

Perfect! I’ll say the same if the soldier asks me.

pal

But you must say that they’re absolutely alike, and Philocomasium needs to be taught so that she knows, so she won’t make a slip if the soldier questions her.

per

A terribly clever trick! But if the soldier wants to see both together, what do we do?250

pal

That’s easy: hundreds of excuses can be found: “She’s not at home, she’s gone for a walk, she’s sleeping, she’s getting dressed, she’s bathing, she’s having lunch, she’s having drinks: she’s busy, she doesn’t have time, she can’t,” as much delay as you wish, so long as we can make him believe from the outset that the lies she’ll be telling are true.

per

I like it the way you tell me.255

pal

Then go in and if she’s there, have her return home quickly, and tell her, show her, teach her this, so that she knows how we’ve established our plans about the twin sister.

per

I’ll give her to you shrewdly and thoroughly instructed. Anything else?

pal

Yes, go in.

per

Yes.

Exit PERIPLECTOMENUS into his house.
