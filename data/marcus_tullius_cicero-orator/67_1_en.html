Cartagoa; after a while,—a long while indeed—the reproof of the ear forced me to abandon the correct pronunciation; I yielded to the people in the matter of usage, and kept the knowledge for myself. Further-more, though it now seems somewhat country-fied, 161 it was once considered refined to drop the last letter, if the word ended in the same two letters as optimus, unless a vowel followed.
