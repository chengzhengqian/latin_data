Is it not perfectly plain why we say cum illis, but use nobiscum rather than cum nobis? If the latter were used the letters would coalesce and produce an obscene meaning, as they would have done in this sentence unless I had placed autem between cum and nobis.c From that come mecum and tecum, used rather than cum me and cum te, so as to resemble vobiscum and nobiscum.

But certain people are beginning late in the day 155 to emend the speech of our fathers, and they criticize this irregularity.d Instead of deum in the phrase prodeum atque hominum fidem they use the form deorum.
