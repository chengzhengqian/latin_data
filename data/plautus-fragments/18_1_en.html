4 Find some replacement to entertain you at home.

Schematicus1

for your father has never rubbed heads with her

Non. 525: limare, “rub, file off,” also means “join.”2

  • 1Another obscure title, if it is a title at all; Ritschl conjectured σχηματικῶς, “in a figure of speech,” which would refer to the Plautine metaphor (which, however, Nonius appears not to have understood properly).
  • 2Limare does not mean “join.” Nonius does not understand the metaphor (“rub heads” = “kiss”).
