Dedications (to Mercury?) of men named Orbius, 1 and belonging to a guild of Mercuriales, at Delos. Presented to merchants.

(i) Lucius Orbius. On a pedestal. 87 b.c.

Lucius Orbius, son of Marcus, foreman for the men 3 from Italy.

(a) Lucius Orbius and Lucius Orbius, freedmen of Lucius, foremen for the men from Italy; a Spartan sweating-room.

(b) Lucius Orbius Licinus, and Lucius Orbius Diphilus, freedmen of Lucius, having become officers for the worship of Mercury, for the men from Italy. 4
