Who is to decide that there was no reason? He will make out that there was. How are we to refute him, and establish that he might have abstained from giving, had he not wished to do so? X. I will not therefore deal with your20 gang of Sardinian conspirators and their perjuries so ingeniously elicited, wrung, and wheedled from them, nor will I amass painfully elaborated arguments to refute them; no, I will meet onset with onset in front-to-front encounter. Not for me to drag each individual successively from your ranks and fight a series of single combats to the death; at one fell swoop the whole army must be routed. For there is one most important charge in the matter21 of corn which concerns the whole of Sardinia, and upon which Triarius has examined all the Sardinians; it is a point which has been supported by the one concerted testimony of all those who gave evidence. And before I touch upon this charge, I beg you, gentlemen, to allow me to lay down certain fundamental principles that shall govern my whole defence; if these are once well and truly laid in accordance with my purpose and expectation, there is no part of the prosecution that I shall have to fear.
