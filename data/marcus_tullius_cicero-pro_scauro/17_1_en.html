For what province is there save Sardinia that does not contain a single state that is a friend of the Roman people, and free? Africa, which is herself the parent of Sardinia, and which45 waged many bitter wars against our ancestors, maintained itself, as Utica can testify, free of all participation in the Punic Wars, not only in its staunchly loyal kingdoms, but in the province itself.
