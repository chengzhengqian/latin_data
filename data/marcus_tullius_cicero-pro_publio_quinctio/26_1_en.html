XVII. I will now consult these gentlemen on your54 behalf, in regard to a matter that is now past and with which I am not concerned, since you forgot to consult them at the proper time on what was your personal affair. I ask you the following questions, Gaius Aquilius, Lucius Lucilius Balbus, Publius Quinctilius, and Marcus Claudius Marcellus.a A partner and relative of mine has not answered to his recognizances; I have long been intimate with him, but have recently been engaged in a dispute with him about money matters. Am I to make application to the praetor to authorize me to take possession of his goods? or, since he has a house, a wife, and children at Rome, should I rather leave a notice at his house? I should like to know your opinion on this matter.
