Act Five Enter CURCULIO from Phaedromus’ house, running and carrying a ring in his hands.
cur

I’ve heard that an ancient poet wrote in a tragedy that two women are worse than one. That is true. But I haven’t seen or heard of a woman worse than this girlfriend of Phaedromus is, and a woman worse than she is595 can’t be mentioned or invented: when she saw that I have this ring, she asked me where I got it from. “Why are you asking me this?” “Because I need to ask.” I refused to tell her. She snatched my hand, biting me in the process, in order to pull it off. I hardly managed to get outside and run off. Away with that little bitch!
