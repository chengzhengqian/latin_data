But she’s chaste and doesn’t sleep with men yet.

pal

I’d believe it if any pimp had chastity.

phae

On the contrary; what do you think she’s like? Whenever she has the opportunity, she comes over to me furtively,60 and when she’s planted a kiss on me, she runs away. It happens like that because this pimp is torturing me, the sick man who sleeps in the sanctuary of Aesculapius.

pal

How so?

phae

At one time he demands thirty minas for her from me, at another, an Attic talent, and I can’t get anything decent or65 just out of him.

pal

It’s unfair of you to demand from him what no pimp has.

phae

Now I sent my hanger-on off to Caria to ask a friend of mine to lend me the money. If he doesn’t bring it, I don’t know where to turn to.
