Whatever it is, he’ll prefer to go by himself.

cap

In the name of all that’s sacred on heaven and earth! That695 I should be dragged off in this way without sentence and without witnesses against me! I implore you, Planesium, and you, Phaedromus, bring me help!

pla

My brother, I beg you, don’t kill this man without sentence. He looked after me well and chastely at his house.
