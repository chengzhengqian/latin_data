sup

Goodness, it’s a charming swindler that Phaedromus has charmingly got hold of. I don’t know whether I should say he’s more of a trickster or of a prankster. I’m afraid I might not be able to get back the costumes I hired out; although465 I have no business with that chap: I entrusted them to Phaedromus himself. Still, I’ll be on my guard. But until he comes out I’ll show in which place you can easily find which sort of person, so that no one labors too laboriously if he wants to meet someone, be it a man of vice or a man without vice, be it a worthy or a worthless character.
