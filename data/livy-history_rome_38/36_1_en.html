Sometimes, when the skin has been lacerated and the gash is wide rather than deep, they think luster is added to their fighting. But when the point of an arrow or sling projectile deeply imbedded in a seemingly minor wound causes intense pain and the projectile does not come forth despite their attempts to extract it, they succumb to fits of rage and shame at so slight an affliction bringing destruction and fling themselves to the ground. So, on that occasion, they lay stretched out at various points.105 Others were rushing into the enemy and receiving wounds from every side, and when they came to close quarters they were slaughtered by the swords of the skirmishers. This type of soldier carries a three-foot shield and, in his right hand, some spears that he uses at long range; and at his side he has a Spanish sword.106 If he has to engage hand to hand, he transfers the spears to his left hand and draws the sword.

By now few Gauls were left alive and these could see that they had been beaten by the light-armed troops and that the legionary forces were bearing down on them. In headlong flight they headed back to their camp, which was already full of panic and confusion, this being where the women, children and other noncombatants had been brought together. Deserted by the fleeing enemy, the hills welcomed the victorious Romans.
