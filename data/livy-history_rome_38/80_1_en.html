Let them tell you how often their lands were devastated, how often their property pillaged—at a time when they barely had the wherewithal to ransom their prisoners and when they heard of human sacrifices and the immolation of their own children.207 Be assured that your allies had been paying tribute to the Gauls208 and that even now (when, thanks to you, they have been freed from the king’s rule) they would still be paying it had I remained inactive.

48. “The further Antiochus was driven back, the more despotic Gallic rule in Asia would have been, and all territory west of the Taurus range you would have added to the Gauls’ empire, not to your own. All right, you could argue, that may be so, but at one time the Gauls pillaged Delphi,209 oracle shared by the human race and navel of the world, and the Roman people did not declare war or make war on them for that. Yes, indeed, but I used to think that, regarding your control and supervision of what was happening in those territories, there was a difference between that time when Greece and Asia were not yet under your jurisdiction and authority and this one when you have fixed the Taurus range as the boundary of Roman dominion—a time when you grant independence and tax exemption to communities, add further territories to some, take land from others, and impose taxes on yet others, a time when you augment, diminish, give and remove kingdoms, and consider it your responsibility to see that these peoples have peace on land and sea.
