Thus they had a longer range using that weapon than does the Balearic slinger,120 and a more accurate and powerful shot. (Furthermore, the sling is not made of a single strap like the Balearic sling and that of other peoples. Instead, it has a pocket of three layers reinforced by seams at close intervals so that the bullet does not wobble when slung as it would if the strap were slack but, sitting well-balanced, is discharged as though shot from a bowstring). Since they were accustomed to hitting rings of small circumference at long range, these men would inflict wounds not only on the heads of their enemy but on any part of the face that they targeted.

These slings curbed the frequency and the boldness of the Samean counterattacks, so much so that the townspeople entreated the Achaeans from their walls to withdraw for a short time and simply watch their engagements with the Roman outposts without participating.

Same endured the siege for four months. They were few at the start, but they also suffered daily casualties, either dead or wounded, and the survivors were physically and mentally drained. Then, by night, the Romans crossed the wall in the area of the citadel that they call Cyaneatis (so-named because the city slopes down to the sea and faces westward121) and reached the forum.
