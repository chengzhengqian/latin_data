If you had lacked pedigree and lain unknown to fame, no heritage to boast, by that citizen you proved yourself Greek, sprung from blood of Euboean forbears. So often did he press his temples with your garlands when he sang at the regular quinquennial festival in lauded verse, after surpassing the eloquence of the Pylian ancient and of Dulichium’s king,23 binding his hair with either guerdon.
