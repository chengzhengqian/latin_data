He had spoken. They find the limbs lying in languor now, breath struggling. Both gird themselves Paeonian fashion24 and together they readily give and take advice until with various medicine they have broken the banes and the sinister cloud of unwholesome sleep. He himself assists the gods; stronger than all his malady, he anticipates their aid. No more swiftly was Telephus cured by Haemonian skill nor fearful Atrides’ cruel wounds closed by Machaon’s balm.

Amid so many gatherings of Fathers and people what room for anxious prayers of mine? Yet I call the stars on high and you, Thymbraean father of poets, to witness how I spent every day and night in terror, ever clinging to the doorway, watchful to pick up every hint now with eye, now with ear; as a little skiff attached to a great ship, when the storm blows high, takes in her small share of the raging waters and tosses in the same south wind.
