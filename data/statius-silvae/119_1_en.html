When Caesar bade Romulus’ magnates and the columns of robed Knights recline here together at a thousand tables, Ceres herself with her dress girt up and Bacchus toil to supply their wants. So flowed the bounteous path of sky-borne Triptolemus; so Lyaeus shaded bare hills and sober fields under his clustered vines.

But not for the viands or the Moorish wood resting on Indian columns12 or the ordered troops of servants had my eager gaze the time; for him, only him—calm of visage, softening its radiance with serene majesty, modestly lowering the banner of his fortune; yet the hidden splendor shone in his face. Even thus would a barbarian enemy and races unknown have recognized him had they seen him.
