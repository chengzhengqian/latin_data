With such a combination of virtues and vices Hannibal served under Hasdrubal’s command for three years, overlooking nothing that needed to be done or seen by a man destined to be a great leader.

5. Now from the day he was declared commander it was as if Italy had been decreed as his province17 and the war with Rome his assignment. Thinking there should be no delay in case, like his father Hamilcar and then Hasdrubal, he too should be overtaken by some accident while he vacillated, he determined to launch an attack on Saguntum.18 Because there was no doubt about such an attack eliciting an armed response from Rome, he first led his army into the territory of the Olcades,19 a people south of the Ebro in the zone of the Carthaginians rather than under their control. He could thus appear not to have specifically targeted the Saguntines but to have been drawn into that conflict in the course of events, that is in his conquest of neighboring tribes and annexation of their territories.

He took by storm and pillaged the wealthy city of Cartala,20 the tribal capital, and from the fear that this inspired the smaller communities capitulated and accepted the imposition of an indemnity.
