When ambassadors came from allies on behalf of allies, your fine commander would not admit them to his camp and thus violated international convention. However, after being excluded from what is not even denied to embassies of an enemy, these men have come to you. Reparations in accordance with their treaty are being demanded; they ask our state to clear itself of responsibility; and they demand the surrender of the guilty party, the man accused of the crime.

“The softer their approach and the slower their initial moves, the more unrelenting, I fear, will be their wrath once they have begun. Keep in mind the Aegates Islands and Eryx43—those twenty-four years of tribulation that you suffered on land and sea. And your leader was not this boy44 but his father, Hamilcar himself, a second Mars, as those men have it.45 We had not kept our hands off Tarentum, that is off Italy, as the treaty required, just as now we are not keeping our hands off Saguntum. And so the gods and men prevailed and, to settle the argument of which of the two peoples had broken the treaty, the outcome of hostilities stood as a disinterested judge and gave victory to the one with right on its side.

“It is against Carthage that Hannibal is now bringing up his siege sheds and assault towers; it is the walls of Carthage that he batters with his ram.
