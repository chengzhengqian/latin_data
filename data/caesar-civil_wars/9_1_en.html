10. With this message in hand Roscius, along with Lucius Caesar, arrived in Capua. There he found the consuls and Pompey and presented Caesar’s demands. 2After deliberation they replied in writing and sent the message to Caesar through Roscius and Lucius Caesar. The gist was this: 3Caesar was to return to Gaul, leave Ariminum, dismiss his army. “If you do this, Pompey will go to Spain. 4Meanwhile, until we get some guarantee that you will do what you promise, we will not discontinue recruiting.”
