Indeed almost all of the soldiers had made shirts or cloaks of felt or patchwork or hides in order to escape the projectiles.

Each side exerted itself with great vigor in occupying positions, Caesar in order to confine Pompey within the narrowest limits, Pompey in order to occupy as many hills as possible in the widest circuit.
