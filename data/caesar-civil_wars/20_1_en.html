26. In response Pompey outfitted the large cargo vessels he had seized in the port of Brundisium. He erected three-story towers on each, and after filling these with a multitude of catapults and all sorts of projectiles he brought them to bear on Caesar’s works, aiming to break up the pontoons and disrupt his efforts. The fighting went on thus for days with a long-distance exchange of slingshot, arrows, and other projectiles.
