27. At this it was possible to see a sudden reversal of fortune. Those who had recently been afraid for themselves were received into a perfectly safe harbor; those who had been a danger to our ships were forced to be afraid of their own danger. 2Thus with the change of circumstance the weather gave our men shelter and inflicted such havoc on the Rhodian ships that every one of the decked ships, sixteen in number, was lost to shipwreck, and some of their large number of rowers and fighters were dashed on the rocks and killed, others were rescued by our men. Caesar spared all of these and sent them home.
