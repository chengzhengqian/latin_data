fears. Unconcerned towards men, unconcerned towards Heaven, they have achieved a consummation very difficult: they have nothing even to ask for.1

Beyond this all else that is reported is legendary: that the Hellusii and Oxiones2 have human faces and features, the limbs and bodies of beasts: it has not been so ascertained, and I shall leave it an open question.
