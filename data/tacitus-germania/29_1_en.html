35. Hitherto we have been gaining knowledge of Germany towards the west. Now the country falls away with a great bend4 towards the north, and first of all come the Chauci.5 Though they start next the Frisii and occupy part of the seaboard, they also border on all of the tribes just mentioned, and finally edge away south as far as the Chatti. This vast block of territory is not merely held by the Chauci, but filled by them.
