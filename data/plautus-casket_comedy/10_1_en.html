This old woman is both a great gossip and a great drinker.150 She has barely left me, the god, any subject to talk about, seeing how she rushed into revealing the fraudulent introduction of the girl into the family. If she’d kept quiet about it, I would have told you nevertheless; being a god, I’d have been able to do so more clearly: my name is Help. Now pay attention that I may give you a clear outline155 of this plot. A long time ago there was a festival in honor of Dionysus in Sicyon. A merchant from Lemnos came to the games here and, being a very young lad, raped a virgin girl here in the street, with violence, drunk, and late at night.
