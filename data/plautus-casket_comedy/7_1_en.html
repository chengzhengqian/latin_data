I want you two to pay attention. I’ll tell you why you were sent for. Well, since I didn’t want to be called a prostitute, my mother humored me and obeyed me, her pleading, obedient daughter, so that she allowed me to live with the85 man I was madly in love with.

No, with no one, except for Alcesimarchus, and no one else has done damage to my chastity.

During the festival of Dionysus3 my mother took me to90 watch the procession. While I was returning home he spied on me and followed me secretly up to the door. Then he came to be on friendly terms with my mother and with me at the same time through flattery, services, and gifts.
