Cistellaria

Act Five Enter DEMIPHO from the right.
dem

What’s this business that everybody’s talking about in every street that my daughter has been found? And they say775 Lampadio has been looking for me in the forum.

lam

Master, where are you coming from?

dem

From the senate.

lam

I’m happy that you now have more children through my efforts.

dem

I don’t like it. I don’t care for getting more children through another’s efforts. But what is it?

lam

Quickly go in here to your relative by marriage. (points to the house of Alcesimarchus) You’ll recognize your daughter780 inside at once. Your wife’s also there. Off with you, quickly!

dem

I’m resolved to sort this out before anything else.

Exeunt DEMIPHO and LAMPADIO into the house of Alcesimarchus. Enter the whole TROUPE.
troupe

In case you’re waiting, spectators, for those people to come out here to you, no one will come out, they’ll all sort out their business inside. When this is done, they’ll put away their costumes. Then anyone who made a mistake785 will get a beating and anyone who made no mistake will get a drink. Now what remains for you, spectators, is this: following the custom of your forefathers give us your applause at the end of the comedy.
