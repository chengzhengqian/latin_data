Prudentius

munus ecce fictile 30inimus intra regiam salutis. attamen vel infimam Deo obsequellam praestitisse prodest. quidquid illud accidit, iuvabit ore personasse Christum.1

  • 1Many MSS. in which line 1 is omitted (thus leaving the sentence incomplete) add after 34 the line quo regente vivimus, which had apparently been inserted at some early time to equalise the numbers of the longer and the shorter lines; but the Hipponactean couplet, as in Horace, Odes II, 18, begins with the shorter line.
