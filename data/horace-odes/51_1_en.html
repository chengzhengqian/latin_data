How close I came to seeing the kingdom of dusky Proserpine, Aeacus sitting in judgment, and, set apart, the abodes of the righteous, and Sappho complaining on her Aeolian strings about the girls of her city, and you, Alcaeus, with your golden plectrum singing in more resonant tones about the harshness of life at sea, the bitter harshness of exile, the harshness of war! The ghosts marvel at both as they sing of things that demand a reverent silence; but the crowd, packed shoulder to shoulder, drink in more eagerly with their ears tales of battles and banished tyrants. What wonder, when, entranced by those songs, the hundred-headed beast28 relaxes his black ears, and the snakes entwined in the Furies’ hair sink to rest? Why, even Prometheus and Pelops’ father are beguiled of their pain by the pleasant sound, and Orion has no interest in hunting lions or timid lynxes.
