12My Muse prefers love to war

You would not wish the long-drawn-out war against ferocious Numantia, or rugged Hannibal, or the Sicilian Sea red with Punic blood to be set to the soft melodies of the lyre,22 nor the savage Lapiths and Hylaeus mad with wine, and the sons of Earth23 vanquished by Hercules’ hand when their threat caused old Saturn’s bright abode to tremble.24 You, Maecenas, will better describe in the prose of history the battles of Caesar and the necks of menacing kings dragged through the streets.
