27Galatea and Europa

May evil people be sped on their way by the omen of a hooting owl and a pregnant bitch or a tawny wolf running down from the Lanuvian fields or a vixen with young;65 and may a snake interrupt the journey they have begun by darting like an arrow across their path and frightening the ponies! But on behalf of one for whom I, as an augur with second sight, feel concern, I shall rouse with my prayer the prophetic raven from the rising sun before that inspired bird, which forecasts imminent rainstorms, can fly back to the stagnant marshes.66

I hope you’ll be happy, Galatea, wherever you choose to be, and that you won’t forget me. May no ill-omened woodpecker or roving crow prevent your departure.
