But you—make sure that your neighbour Enipeus does not please you more than he ought, even though no one else on the grass of Mars29 is seen to be so skilful at maneuvering his horse, and nobody can swim so swiftly down the Tuscan river.30 When dusk falls, lock up your house; at the music of his plaintive pipe, don’t look down into the street; and though he calls you hard-hearted again and again, remain unyielding.
