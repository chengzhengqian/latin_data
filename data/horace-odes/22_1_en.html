21In praise of Latona and her children

Sing, young girls, of Diana; sing, boys, of long-haired Cynthius, and of Latona so dearly loved by highest Jove. Praise, you girls, her who delights in rivers and the leafy groves that stand out boldly on cold Algidus or in the dark woods of Erymanthus or verdant Gragus; you males, sing as many praises to exalt Tempe and Delos, birthplace of Apollo, and that shoulder which is noted for the quiver and his brotherâ€™s lyre.46 Moved by your prayer, he will drive away mournful warfare, he will drive away wretched famine and plague from our people and Caesar, our leader, and direct them against the Persians and Britons.
