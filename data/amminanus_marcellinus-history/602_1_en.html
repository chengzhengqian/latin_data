The Surviving Books of the History of Ammianus Marcellinus

Book XXVII

1. The Alamanni rout the Romans in battle and kill the generals Charietto and Severianus.

1. While throughout the Orient the changing365–6 course of events was developing as we have narrated, the Alamanni, after the sad losses and wounds which they had suffered from their frequent battles with Julianus Caesar, having at last renewed their strength (which yet did not equal its old vigour), and being an object of dread for the reasons which we have mentioned above,1 were already overleaping the frontiers of Gaul. And immediately after the first of January, while throughout those icebound regions the grim season of winter bristled, they hurried forth in divisions,2 and, without restraint a host was ranging everywhere.
