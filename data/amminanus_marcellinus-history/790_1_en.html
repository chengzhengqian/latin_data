3. All this almost in plain speech showed that this kind of death1 threatened him. Furthermore, the ghostly form of the king of Armenia and the piteous shades of those who shortly before had been executed in connection with the fall of Theodoras,2 shrieking horrible songs at night, in the form of dirges, tormented many with dire terrors.

4. A heifer was found lying lifeless with its windpipe cut, and its death was an indication of great and widespread sorrow from funerals of the people.
