35. But enough for the present of this account of affairs in the city. Now let us return to the other events which were caused by various incidents in the provinces.

5. The Saxons in Gaul, after a truce had been made, were ambushed by the Romans. Valentinian, under promise of joining forces with them, roused the Burgundians to invade Alamannia, but they, after being betrayed and deceived, killed all their prisoners and returned home.
