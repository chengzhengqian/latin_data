8. Slanders and calumnies in the camp of Constantius Augustus, and the greed of the courtiers.

1. After Marcellus had been worsted, as I have said, and had returned to Serdica,2 his native place, in the camp of Augustus, under pretext of upholding his imperial majesty, many abominable acts were committed.
