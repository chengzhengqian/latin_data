stra

chae

You’re stupid beyond what your youth and bravery would make me accept, Stratippocles. Are you ashamed because you bought a captive girl born in a good family in the booty? Who will there be who’d find fault with you for that?

With this deed I’ve found as enemies all those who envy me. But I’ve never done any damage or disgrace to her110 chastity.
