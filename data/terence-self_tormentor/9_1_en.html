chr This is splendid news. If only I’d pressed my invitation to Menedemus to join us a bit harder! I could have been the first to present him with this unexpected joyful news in my own home. And there’s still time.

clit Don’t do it, father. It’s not a good idea.

chr How so?

clit Because it’s still unclear what he’s going to do with himself. He’s only just arrived, he’s apprehensive about everything—his father’s anger and how his girl friend feels towards him. He’s desperately in love with her. It was because of her that this trouble arose and he left home.

clit Now he’s sent his slave into town 25 to fetch her, and I’ve sent our Syrus along with him.
