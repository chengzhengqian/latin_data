Or rather may your mind still race with insanity; may blind error continue as it began: the only thing now that can offer you innocence is madness; after pure hands, the next best fate is ignorance of the evil.54 ​ Now let Hercules’ palms beat his breast till it resounds; let those arms, accustomed to bearing the sky, be struck by his avenging hands. Let heaven hear his mighty groans, let the queen of the dark world hear them, and fierce Cerberus where he skulks in his deep cave, his necks still bound with mighty chains.
