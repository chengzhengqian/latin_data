But now let the past be completely forgotten; when the victor lays down arms, the vanquished should also lay aside hatred. I am not asking you to do homage to your ruler on bended knee; I approve of the fact that you bear your overthrow with great spirit. You are a worthy wife for a king: let us make a marriage alliance.

megara

A cold shudder runs through my stunned body. What outrage has struck my ears? I did not tremble when peace was shattered and the din of war resounded around our walls; I endured all that fearlessly. But marriage I shudder at; now I recognize myself as a prisoner. Let chains burden my body, let me face a lingering drawn-out death by slow starvation: no violence will overcome my loyalty. I shall die as your wife, Alcides.

lycus

You take courage from a husband sunk in the underworld?

megara

He visited the underworld to gain the upper world.

lycus

He is held down by the weight of the vast earth.

megara

No burden will hold down the one who carried the heavens.

lycus

You will be forced.
