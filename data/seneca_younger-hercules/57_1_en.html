O faithful friend, Theseus, search out a distant, obscure hiding place; and since as witness to others’ crimes you always show love to the guilty,66 pay me thanks and recompense for my service to you: return me, I pray you, to the shades of the underworld, and reinstate me in your chains as your replacement. That place will hide me—but it too knows me.

theseus

My land awaits you. There Gradivus had his hand purified of bloodshed and returned to warfare. That land summons you, Alcides, which customarily restores gods to innocence.
