Plays

213

Polydorus called ‘Deiphilus’ is astonished to find his parents unharmed:

Nonius:

‘Offendere (hit upon, meet with),’ to find . . .

Polydorus

whom I hit upon unharmed, just as I wished.

214

Iliona reveals the truth to him:

Nonius:

‘Lactare (dupe)’ means to lead on or coax, ‘fleece,’ cheat . . .—

Iliona

So that the error which at present dupes you May fret you thus no longer.
