FRL I: ENNIUS

BOOK XII

The content of this book is uncertain. Q. Fabius Maximus Verrucosus (dict. 217, cos. V 209 BC), the great Cunctator, died in 203 BC, and since the book number provided for F 1 by Macrobius is reliable, the famous praise of Fabius there must be retrospective, but the occasion for it is unknown. Whether F 2 describes a specific episode (Skutsch) or makes a general statement about the exhaustion of even a victorious army (Vahlen) cannot be determined.
