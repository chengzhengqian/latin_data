ANNALS: BOOK I

Not “masters” nor “lords” did they call those they justly obeyed, and indeed, not even “kings,” but “guardians of the fatherland,” “fathers,” “gods,” and not without reason. What do they say next?

you have led us to the realms of light

They believed that life, honor, and distinction were given to them by the justice of their king.
