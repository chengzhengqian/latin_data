23 Servius Danielis, Commentary on Virgil

through Assaracus he says they were Trojans, for Assaracus was the grandfather of Anchises. Ennius:

to Assaracus was born the excellent Capys, and he in turn begat faithful Anchises

  • 1Text (and sense) are uncertain since loss of context makes it impossible to know whether the line opens with the preposition cum [with] or the conjunction quom [when]. If the former, the impossible suo might be corrected to saevo [ferocious]; if the latter, the likely emendation is sos [them], that is, “When great Titan was suppressing them . . .” The victims are in either case presumably the sons of Saturn.
