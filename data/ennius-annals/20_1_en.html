Ilia, divine granddaughter, what troubles you have borne

  • 1Nonius is mistaken: parumper invariably means “for a short time,” i.e., paullisper.
  • 2Skutsch’s emendation has great appeal. Aeneias was a cult title of Aphrodite that Ennius would have encountered at Ambracia and was easily, if dubiously, associated even there with the hero Aeneas (Dion. Hal. Ant. 1.50.4).
