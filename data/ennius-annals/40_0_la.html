FRL I: ENNIUS

BOOK IV

This book dealt with the early days of the Republic, but specific references are difficult to identify. Anxur (F 3) was captured by the Romans in 406, 400, and 329 BC. The solar eclipse of F 4 must have been that of June 21, 400 BC.
