*7 Festus

nictit:1 of a dog whimpering gently when it scents the tracks of wild animals, as Ennius in Book 10:

just as when a swift hunting dog held by a chain complains if it chances to pick up a scent of <wild animals?> with its keen nose: it whimpers with its own voice and then whines keenly

From that comes [the noun] “whimpering.”
