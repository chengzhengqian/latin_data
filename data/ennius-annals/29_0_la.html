FRL I: ENNIUS

BOOK II

Fragments explicitly attributed to this book by their sources confirm that Annals 2 covered the reigns of the three kings Numa Pompilius (F 1–4), Tullus Hostilius (F 5–11), and Ancus Marcius (F 12–19), but identification of specific lines with specific events is highly problematic.
