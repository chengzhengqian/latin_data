FRL I: ENNIUS

F 50–51: A Prayer to Mars and Quirinus

The “consorts” provided for them here are in origin abstract attributes: Valor (Nerio), Will (Herie), and Exhortation (Hora). Ovid’s metamorphosis of Romulus’ wife Hersilia into Quirinus’ consort Hora (Met. 14.829–51) cannot be traced back to Ennius.
