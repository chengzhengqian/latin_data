FRAGMENTS F 1: Ennius Calls upon the Muses

​

1 Varro, On the Latin Language

I shall speak in this book of the words used by poets, first concerning places . . .

Muses,1 who strike great Olympus with your feet

[20] The Greeks call the heaven Olympus. All people call the mountain in Macedonia Olympus, for which reason, I am inclined to think, the Muses were called “daughters of Olympus.”
