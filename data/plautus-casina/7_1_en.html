Chalinus, I haven’t forgotten my duty. I’ve put someone105 in charge on our country estate who’ll look after it properly despite my absence. If I achieve what I’ve come here for, to the city, that is, to marry the woman you are in love with, the pretty and tender Casina, your fellow slave, when I’ve taken her to the country with me as my wife,110 then I’ll permanently sleep in the country, in my own territory.
