(also to Lysidamus) No, subdue that one: he has learned how to submit.11

lys

(to Chalinus) Put the urn here and give me the lots. (to all) Pay attention. (to Cleostrata) And yet I thought I could persuade you, my dear wife, that Casina should be365 given in marriage to me. And even now I think so.

cleo

She should be given to you?

lys

Yes, to me—oh, I didn’t mean to say that; when I meant “to me,” I said “to him,” and when I desired that to me—I’ve been making mistakes in speech for a while already now.

cleo

Yes, you have, and in action too.

lys

To him—no, to me—bah! at last I’ve just about managed to return to the right path.

cleo

You’re making a lot of mistakes.370

lys

That’s what happens when you want something so much. But, recognizing your right, we both, I and he, appeal to you.

cleo

What do you mean?

lys

I’ll tell you, my honey: do our overseer here a favor about that Casina of yours.

cleo

No, I’m not doing him any favor, and I’m against it.

lys

In that case I’ll distribute the lots on both sides now.

cleo

Who forbids it?

lys
