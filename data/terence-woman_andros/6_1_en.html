sim Meanwhile the funeral procession continued. We followed and came to the burial ground. The woman was laid on the pyre, and the lamentations began. Meanwhile this sister I was talking about approached the flames with little regard for her own safety and was in real danger. At this point Pamphilus revealed by his alarm the love he’d disguised and hidden so well. He ran forward, grasped the woman round the waist, and said “My dear Glycerium, what are you doing? Why are you risking your life?” She fell into his arms and wept. It was such an intimate scene—you could readily see that they were lovers.
