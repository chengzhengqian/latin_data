The Woman of Andros

mys (to Davus) Why did you leave me here alone, for heaven’s sake?

dav (ignoring her) Hey! What’s this nonsense? (to Mysis) Here you, Mysis, where does this baby come from? Who brought it here? (he winks at her)

mys (uncomprehending) Are you in your right mind? Asking me that?

dav Who am I supposed to ask? There’s no one else here.

chr (aside) I wonder where it does come from.

dav (to Mysis, threateningly) Are you going to answer my question?

mys (as Davus grabs her arm) Ow!

dav Come over here to the right. (drawing her further away from Chremes)

mys You’re crazy. Didn’t you yourself—?

dav (whispering) Just answer my questions. If you utter a single word more, look out! (aloud) Abusing me, are you? Where does it come from? Tell me straight.

mys It’s from our house.

dav Ha! Just the shameless behaviour you’d expect from that class of woman!

chr (aside) This is the Andrian woman’s maid, unless I’m mistaken.

dav Do you think we’re the sort of people you can make fools of?

chr (aside) I arrived in the nick of time.

dav Hurry up and remove the baby from the doorstep. (whispering) Wait! Don’t move an inch from where you are.

mys May the gods blast you! You’re frightening a poor woman out of her wits.

dav (aloud) Are you listening to me or not?

mys What do you want?
