LXXXVIII

Pliny to the Emperor Trajan

It is my prayer, Sir, that this birthday2 and many others to come will bring you the greatest happiness, and that in health and strength you may add to the immortal fame and glory of your reputation by ever new achievements.

LXXXIX

Trajan to Pliny

I write in acknowledgement of your prayers, my dear Pliny, that I may spend many birthdays made happy by the continued prosperity of our country.
