He did indeed once (to his greater glory) return temporarily to active life when he was chosen by our friend Corellius to assist him in the purchase and distribution of land made possible through the generosity of the Emperor Nerva:1 and there could be no higher honour than to be the special choice of so great a man from such a wide field for selection.

Such splendid conduct deserves praise for its very rarity, seeing that the majority of people remember their dead friends only to complain about them. This, then, is the man who so much desires your friendship, so that you should, if you will trust me, welcome him with open arms, or rather summon him to your side with affection as though returning a favour. For, according to the code of friendship, the one who takes the initiative puts the other in his debt and owes no more until he is repaid.
