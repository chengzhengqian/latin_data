Philippic 2

He behaved like a man; but we deserve contempt, who hate the author but defend his acts.119

Where is the seven hundred million which is recorded 93a120 in the account books kept at the Temple of Ops? That money has an evil history to be sure,121 but if it was not going to be returned to its owners it could save us from having to pay levies.122 As for you, how was it that before the first of April you ceased to owe the forty million sesterces that you owed on the fifteenth of March?

[38] What am I to say about the endless memoranda, 97the innumerable handwritten documents? There are actually peddlers who sell them openly like programs for the gladiators. Such great heaps of coin are built up at Antonius’ house that the money is no longer counted but weighed. But how blind avarice is! An official notice was lately posted exempting the richest communities in Crete from taxation and decreeing that after the proconsulship of Marcus Brutus, Crete shall cease to be a province. Are you in your right mind? Should you not be in a straitjacket? Could Crete be freed at the end of Marcus Brutus’ term as governor by an order of Caesar’s, when Crete had nothing to do with Brutus in Caesar’s lifetime? By the sale of this order, in case you gentlemen imagine that nothing has happened, you have lost the province of Crete. To sum it up, Antonius has been ready to sell anything to anybody who was ready to buy.
